# Regular Perks (Level-Up)

These perks are primarily unlocked through normal level progression.
Requirements now use the final **MACHINE Stats** (1–10 scale) + relevant skills.

| Perk Name                  | Requirements                                      | Ranks | Effect / Benefit |
|----------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|-------|------------------|
| **Ripple Weaver**          | Investigation 6, Engine 6                         | 2     | Predict power grid ripples and blackouts. +15% chance to find salvage during fluctuations (Rank 2: +25%). Enables hidden path combos. |
| **Jury-Rig Virtuoso**      | Agility 7, Might 6                                | 2     | Repair or repurpose damaged items/nodes using dissimilar materials. Bonus when working during crises. Core for Independent Lattice. |
| **Data Ghost**             | Calculation 7, Arcanet Navigation 50              | 1     | Stealthier data extraction and reduced detection when hacking or leaking information. |
| **Empathy Protocols**      | Humanity 7, Empathy Protocols skill               | 2     | +10% success on human/NPC dialogue checks. Unlock unique non-lethal or compassionate options. |
| **Thermal Equilibrium**    | Engine 7, Thermal Engineering 40                  | 2     | Better heat/power allocation management. Reduce negative effects from blackouts in residential districts. |
| **Lattice Architect**      | Calculation 7, Engine 6                           | 1     | Bonus when designing or linking decentralized power nodes. Strongly supports hidden bypass paths. |
| **Frontier Resilience**    | Engine 6, Might 6                                 | 2     | +Cold resistance and better performance in Sagittarius/Frostward. Bonus to modular outpost building. |
| **Non-Lethal Specialist**  | Agility 6, Might 6                                | 2     | Increased effectiveness with restraint/subdual weapons or tactics. Fits the “we don’t want to kill each other” tone. |
| **Siligel Chemist**        | Calculation 7, Siligel Chemistry 50               | 2     | Improved crafting and efficiency with siligel-based items/power sources. |
| **Arcanet Weaver**         | Calculation 8, Arcanet Navigation 60              | 2     | Faster/better hacking and data manipulation on the Arcanet. Bonus to information-based hidden paths. |

**Design Note**: Perk requirements have been updated to use the final MACHINE stats (M, A, C, H, I, N, E). Many of these perks synergize strongly with the Independent Lattice and other secret endings.
