## Design Notes & Open Questions

**Unshakeable — redesign needed**
"Never fail a Nerve check" is trivially achievable with a Nerve 10 build and is not a genuine achievement. Three options under consideration:
- *Option A (Behavioral):* "Complete the game without ever fleeing from a combat encounter." Cannot be cheesed with stat investment; requires a consistent behavioral choice across every fight.
- *Option B (Threshold):* "Pass the single highest-threshold Nerve check in the game." Rewards specifically building for the hardest Nerve gate rather than having high Nerve generally.
- *Option C (Comprehensive):* "Pass every available Nerve check in a single playthrough." Requires consistent Nerve investment across the whole game.
Option A is likely strongest — it changes how the player plays, not just what they build.

**[NAME PENDING] — "You Break Too Easy" achievement**
Achievement confirmed: receive every recruitable companion's romance signal line in a single playthrough. Name needs decision. Options:
- "The Reject" (user suggestion)
- "Signal Received" (uses the game's own terminology)
- "Wrong Build" (meta, slightly funny)
- "Not Your Type" (clean, universally readable)
- "0 for [X]" (blunt; X = total companion count)

**Axis Mundi — redesign needed**
Name confirmed for a Hub-related achievement. "Pass through the Hub 50 times" is too easily gamed (enter/exit loop). Three replacement directions under consideration:
- *Option A:* Broker a resolution between two opposing factions in the Hub — genuinely hard, diplomatically meaningful.
- *Option B:* Have every named faction representative meet you in the Hub in a single playthrough — rewards thoroughness.
- *Option C:* A specific quest resolution that makes the Hub the site of a significant convergence — tied to a particular story beat rather than a generic counter.
