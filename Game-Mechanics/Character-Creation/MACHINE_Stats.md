### MACHINE Stats Overview

| Letter | Stat            | Core Focus                                                      | Fallout Equivalent      |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------|
| **M**  | **Might**       | Raw physical strength and power                                 | Strength                |
| **A**  | **Agility**     | Speed, reflexes, coordination, and fine motor control           | Agility                 |
| **C**  | **Calculation** | Logical processing, computation, optimization, problem-solving  | Intelligence + Luck     |
| **H**  | **Humanity**    | Emotional intelligence, empathy, social awareness, connection   | Perception + Charisma   |
| **I**  | **Investigation** | Pattern recognition, information gathering, analysis, deduction | — (New)                 |
| **N**  | **Nerve**       | Mental resilience, willpower, influence over companions         | New stat                |
| **E**  | **Engine**      | Operational endurance before burnout                            | Endurance               |
