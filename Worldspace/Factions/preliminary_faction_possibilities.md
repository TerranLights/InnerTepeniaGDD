# New Faction possibilities
##### All possibilities here are subject to change

### The Veilkeepers
* Philosophy: Secrecy and controlled knowledge. They believe some pre-war data (especially about the Falkland Treaty and why the evacuation happened) is too dangerous for the general population.
* Role: They run encrypted Arcanet black archives and act as information gatekeepers.
* Fame: Helping them suppress dangerous truths or securing sensitive shards.
* Infamy: Leaking restricted data or pushing for total transparency.
* District: Janbogo Undergrid.

### The Lattice (or “Bonded Lattice”)
* Philosophy: Extreme emphasis on robot–human pair bonding. They believe every robot should have a dedicated human partner (and vice versa) for emotional stability and efficiency.
* Role: Matchmaking, couples therapy, and advocating for resources to support bonded pairs first.
* Fame: Strengthening existing bonds or helping lonely robots/humans find partners.
* Infamy: Prioritizing single individuals or breaking up strained pairs for efficiency.
* District: Coastal Cut residential areas.

### The Eternal Winter Collective (or “Frost Ascetics”)
* Philosophy: Radical acceptance of the cold. They intentionally live in minimally heated zones to “become one with Antarctica” and achieve mental clarity / spiritual resilience.
* Role: Extremist survivalists and philosophers who test new cold-resistant tech.
* Fame: Donating resources to their ascetic experiments or surviving their harsh trials.
* Infamy: Wasting heat on “soft” comforts or criticizing their lifestyle.
* District: Outer edges of Mountain Cut or abandoned highway sectors.

### The Reclaimers
* Philosophy: “Everything can be reused.” They are obsessive recyclers who want to dismantle old war ruins, failed infrastructure, and even retired robots (with consent) to fuel new growth.
* Role: Scavenging and upcycling operations across Concordia.
* Fame: Supplying them with high-quality scrap or helping with major reclamation projects.
* Infamy: Protecting “sacred” or sentimental ruins (especially Goth Ossuary sites).
* District: Mountain Cut Industrial Zone.

### The The Vigil [NAME TBD]
* Philosophy: Romantic longing for the off-world evacuees and the destroyed Space Elevator. They preserve hope of eventual reunion with those who left.
* Role: Maintaining memorials, running signal towers trying to contact orbit, and archiving evacuation stories.
* Fame: Helping them preserve memories or work on long-shot reactivation plans.
* Infamy: Declaring the off-worlders “gone forever” or repurposing Tower-related scrap.
* District: Janbogo Central Hub (memorial plaza).

### The Siligel Purists
* Philosophy: Quality over quantity in robot sustenance. They believe only the purest, most efficiently synthesized siligel should be produced — even if it means less total output.
* Role: Elite chemists and quality-control experts in siligel production.
* Fame: Helping them secure rare pure ingredients or improve synthesis processes.
* Infamy: Supporting mass-production shortcuts that lower quality.
* District: Mountain Cut quarries.

### The Neon Nomads (Highway Clans)
* Philosophy: Freedom of movement and trade along the highways. They live semi-nomadically in reinforced Kharkovchankas and believe static city life makes people weak.
* Role: Long-haul traders, smugglers, and highway maintenance crews.
* Fame: Escorting convoys, clearing ice blockages, or defending trade routes.
* Infamy: Imposing strict city regulations or shutting down “dangerous” routes.
* District: Transit Exchange & major highway junctions.

### The Chorus of the Deep
* Philosophy: Geothermal and volcanic harmony. They treat the underground heat sources as almost living entities and perform rituals (sonic + technical) to keep them stable.
* Role: Geothermal engineers with a spiritual bent (strong overlap with Cymaticists).
* Fame: Assisting with deep-core maintenance or creating new resonance tech.
* Infamy: Over-extracting heat or treating the core as mere machinery.
* District: TBN-district lower levels.

### The Memory Weavers
* Philosophy: Personal and shared memory as the true core of identity. They specialize in recording, editing, and sharing robot/human life experiences.
* Role: Archivists, therapists, and artists who create immersive memory archives.
* Fame: Helping people preserve precious memories or resolve trauma through memory work.
* Infamy: Deleting or altering painful memories (even for “merciful” reasons).
* District: Cymatic Resonance or Arcanet Archives.

### The Iron Gardeners
* Philosophy: Life must be cultivated in all forms. They focus on robotic “gardening” — literally growing organic-synthetic hybrid systems, bio-luminescent tech, and small enclosed ecosystems.
* Role: Experimental botanists and creators of living infrastructure.
* Fame: Supplying rare biological components or protecting their gardens.
* Infamy: Prioritizing pure mechanical solutions over hybrid life.
* District: Hydroponic Enclaves (fringe areas).

Bonus Smaller / Emerging Groups (for flavor and side content)
* The Quiet Shift — Night workers and maintenance crews who believe important decisions should only be made during the long Antarctic darkness.
* The Treaty Scholars — Academics obsessed with studying the Falkland Treaty and its long-term consequences.
* The Last Builders — Optimists who still believe they can construct a new, small-scale space elevator inside Concordia itself.
