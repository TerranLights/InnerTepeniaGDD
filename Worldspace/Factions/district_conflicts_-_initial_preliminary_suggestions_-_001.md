# District conflicts (initial preliminary suggestions)
 
## A. at least two (ideally up to four or five, if it's possible, but at least two) conflicting interests where, if the player helps/promotes one, it ends up harming/hurting the other 
## B. at least two (ideally three or four, if possible) other districts in Concordia outside of itself whose interests conflict 
* if the player helps/promotes one, it ends up harming/hurting that initial district
* keeping attempts to make sure district-conflicts overlap as little as possible
* This way, there's no one district that's somehow "more disadvantaged" (or "more advantaged") than any other, in terms of their interests
