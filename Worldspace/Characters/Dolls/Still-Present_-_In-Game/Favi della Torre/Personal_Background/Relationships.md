## Key Individuals

* **The elderly scientist (name TBD)** — Deceased; Upper Earth (Italy). The man who commissioned Favi's construction — he did not build her himself, but specified what he needed (a scientific assistant to cross-check his results and correct errors) and had her made to those specifications. He came to love her as a daughter figure after losing his real daughter to a vehicular accident years before; he never fully recovered from that first loss, and Favi filled the absence. When Favi was exiled, he lost his daughter a second time — the second loss reopened the first wound, and the combined grief is what killed him. He died not long after her exile.

  The relationship was not romantic in any sense. Whether it is best characterized as paternal/daughter or something more complex is TBD.

  Favi does not know any of this at the start of the game. She discovers it when the player brings her Italian-historical datashard records obtained during DLC 5 — she finds a brief records entry mentioning his death, notices the date, calculates when that was relative to her exile, re-reads the entry, and arrives at the cause.
