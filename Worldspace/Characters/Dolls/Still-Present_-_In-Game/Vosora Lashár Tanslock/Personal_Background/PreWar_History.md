# [Character Name] — Pre-War History

(Background before the first global war, early activation if applicable, human creators/owners, initial purpose, etc.)

* A Tepenia native, she specifically lives in the Tepenian city of Concordia (which we currently know today as Concordia Station in Antarctica).
* Vosora is an extremely capable data and logistics expert. Her field is not "science" in any traditional concept of the term. Rather, she excels in "the science of logistics"; that being, organizing information, keeping data orderly and managed properly, making sure that all the other robots in Tepenia who have specialized jobs can find the information they need easily and accomplish whatever it is they've set out to do.
* Initially had no actual true "hometown", as she enjoyed travelling throughout Tepenia helping the citizens of cities manage their data
* Vosora later came to organize the logistics, data supply chains, and chains of command necessary for the planning and construction of Amundsen Tower
* At the time of the Long Night War, Vosora simply happened to be in Concordia at the time, and was essentially forced to stay there out of circumstance
* These days, she attempts to maintain communication with Tepenians who'd managed to flee into space so that she can transmit data signals containing schematics, data, and anything else to the space-dwelling Tepenians that they might need
