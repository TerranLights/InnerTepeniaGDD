# [Character Name] — Pre-War History

(Background before the first global war, early activation if applicable, human creators/owners, initial purpose, etc.)

Majyao owned and operated a famous coffee and teahouse in the city of Janbogo which eventually became known throughout Tepenia (New Antarctica). She made sure all her visitors felt as though they were entering a "second home" when they came to her teahouse, and she served exotic teas from leaves and plants grown in the harsh conditions of Antarctica.

Majyao later needed to flee to the city of Concordia following the Long Night War (or also known as the Midnight War), which was fought between the humans of Upper Earth (i.e., the rest of the world beyond Antarctica) and Tepenia (the hybrid robot/human nation of Antarctica itself). Many of Tepenia's inhabitants needed to flee earth via an orbital space elevator beginning at the South Pole and extending into low-earth orbit, an orbital space elevator called "Amundsen Tower". Robots initially use it to begin colonizing space. However, Majyao chose not to go to Amundsen Tower and flee into outer space. Instead, she chose to settle in Concordia and open a new coffee and teahouse there to serve the remaining inhabitants left in Antarctica.
