## Backstory Summary
Majyao owned and operated a famous coffee and teahouse in the city of Janbogo. It became well-known throughout Tepenia — a place where every visitor felt they were entering a second home. She sourced exotic teas and coffees grown in the harsh conditions of Antarctica, including her signature **Blood River Tea** from the iron-rich soil of Blood Falls in Taylor Valley.

When the Long Night War came, she chose not to flee to Amundsen Tower and escape into space. She chose to stay on Earth. She relocated to Concordia and opened a new coffee and teahouse there to serve the people who remained.

The Concordia teahouse is her second — rebuilt from everything the first one was, in the only city left.

## Design Notes & Open Questions
- **Origin:** Her original coffee and teahouse was in Janbogo, where it became famous throughout Tepenia. The Concordia teahouse is her second — chosen deliberately when she decided to stay on Earth rather than flee through Amundsen Tower.
- **Coffee and tea:** She serves both, not tea only. The original Janbogo establishment was a coffee and teahouse. The Concordia version is the same.
- **The teahouse:** A warm, serene sanctuary for the tired, weary, overworked, and overstressed. A place to forget for a few hours that life in post-war Antarctica is brutal. Name TBD.
- **Tea sourcing:** She sources from across Antarctica as a point of pride and philosophy. Signature specialty was **Blood River Tea** — grown in soil nourished by Blood Falls (Taylor Valley, McMurdo Dry Valleys). The Long Night War cut the supply line. She can no longer serve it. The gap is real and matters to her.
- **Character music:** Fusion Jazz — a hybrid of Scandinavian Avant-Garde Jazz and Chinese Traditional Folk. Sophisticated and experimental in structure; rooted and organic in feeling. Bridges traditions without belonging fully to any one.
- **Patron relationship:** She loves her patrons and interacts with them directly. Not a distant curator. But the space is always deeply hers — people feel it when they are in it.
- **Neutrality:** She is not affiliated with any faction. Her teahouse functions as neutral ground. Whether this is deliberate policy or simply because she loves everyone who walks in is a useful ambiguity.
