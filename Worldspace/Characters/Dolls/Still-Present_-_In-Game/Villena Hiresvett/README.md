## Backstory Summary
A Tepenia native, originally from Belgrano. Moved to Palmer City chasing fame as a Rockabilly/Electroswing singer and entertainer — the big city lights, the national stage. When the Long Night War came, she tried to flee south to Amundsen Tower. She missed the last ride up and watched the tower burn in front of her. In a flash decision she turned toward Concordia and made it.

She has been in Leo ever since — performing the same music she has always loved, building residencies at 2–3 regular venues, earning her standing in the district's recognition hierarchy. She has no consistent band yet; the game takes place shortly after the war's end and the ensemble hasn't had time to form.

She will never see the stars. She will never have the national fame she came to Palmer City for. There is no nation left — only a city. She keeps performing.

## Personal Questline Hook
- Quest Title: "The Last Stage" (working title)
- Core conflict & emotional stakes: She has been moving forward since the war — performing, building, staying in motion — and it has worked. The companion quest is the moment it stops working, when something forces her to sit with the permanent loss of the dream that brought her to Palmer City. On the other side, she has to decide what her life means now that there is no nation left to be famous in.
- Major themes: Grief for a foreclosed future; the difference between performing joy and having it; what you become when the original answer is permanently unavailable
