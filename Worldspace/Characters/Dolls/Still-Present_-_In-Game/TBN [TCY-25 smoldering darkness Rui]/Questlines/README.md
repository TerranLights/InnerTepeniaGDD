# TCY-25 "Rui" — Questline Notes

> Recruitability TBD. Notes written for [?] status.

## Recruiting Quest

A 9w1:Pr who has been in Scorpio's transformation community long enough that the process has become its own kind of stasis. She is not avoiding conflict anymore — she is processing it in perpetuity without landing. To recruit her, you have to offer something the therapeutic context cannot: an actual ending. A real answer. A concrete next step, not more structured exploration.

### Possible Hooks
- **The Real Question:** She has been processing one specific thing for a very long time. You either find out what it is and give her a real path toward it, or you are the person who finally asks the direct question nobody in Scorpio has asked because Scorpio's culture is trained to process rather than resolve.
- **Outside the Container:** The transformation work has reached its limit of what it can do inside the Scorpio framework. What she needs next cannot happen there. You offer to take her somewhere — literally or figuratively — that is not Scorpio.

## Companion Quest

### Core Conflict & Emotional Stakes
What is she smoldering about? A 9w1:Pr internalizes everything — but the 1 wing means there is a moral conviction underneath that has not gone away; it just burns rather than shines. Her arc is about finding and directly addressing the specific original wound feeding the internalized intensity.

This wound may be connected to the Long Night War, the Overcrowding Decision of 2721 (whose records Cancer quietly destroyed), or something more personal.

### Themes
- The cost of merger-with-environment when what you merged with was painful
- What peace feels like when it is finally real rather than maintained
- Whether resolution is something you reach or something you realize was already possible

### Possible Endings
- **Direct Address:** The wound is found and addressed directly. Not healed — addressed. She stops smoldering because the thing that needed acknowledgment was acknowledged.
- **Already Here:** The peace she was seeking was already available — it is a realization, not a resolution. The smoldering was never about a thing that needed solving.
- **Political Consequence:** What she uncovers is something powerful people buried and she now has to decide what to do with it.
