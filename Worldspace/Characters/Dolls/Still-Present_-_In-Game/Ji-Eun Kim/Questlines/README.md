## Recruiting Quest

Ji-Eun has been managing alone until the problem exceeded her capacity — classic 5w4 behavior. Finding her follows Calethina's questline thread to her ruins. Once found, the path to recruiting her is:

### Possible Hooks
- **The Unfinished Work:** She is mid-project on something she cannot finish alone. You help her complete it (or reach a stable stopping point), and then joining you becomes the next logical step rather than an interruption.
- **The Resource She Needs:** She cannot access something she requires — her ruined facility, a specific material, a record that has been sealed. Providing that access earns her enough trust that working together becomes possible.
- **The Better Problem:** She does not join out of loyalty. She joins because what you are doing is now the most interesting available problem, and it would be professionally irresponsible not to pursue it.

## Companion Quest — "The Ruins at the Edge of the Map"

### Core Conflict & Emotional Stakes
Her testing facility is in ruins. By whom? Why? And what was she working on when it happened — something that needs to be finished, or something that needs to be permanently stopped?

This quest traces those questions to their answers, which may lead into Concordia's intelligence layer (Eyes of Gold), Calethina's history, and the nanotech research underground.

### Themes
- The vulnerability of solitary mastery: what happens when what you know makes you a target
- Exile within exile — sent to Concordia, then driven from her own work
- The difference between knowledge that should exist and knowledge that is too dangerous to exist

### Possible Endings
- **Good:** The work is recovered and deployed for the city's benefit. The ruins become something new.
- **Neutral:** The work is destroyed before it can be misused. Loss, but chosen.
- **Rebuild:** She establishes a new facility — this time with actual protection rather than isolation.
- **Dark / Hidden:** She accepts that some work is meant to stay lost, and what she finds in the ruins confirms why.

### Cross-Questline Connections
- Calethina's questline leads *to* Ji-Eun — the player may encounter her from that direction first.
- The Eyes of Gold's gold ocular prosthetics trace back to her designs — a player pursuing the Eyes will encounter her history from the other direction.
- Both threads converging on the same person is the intended design.
