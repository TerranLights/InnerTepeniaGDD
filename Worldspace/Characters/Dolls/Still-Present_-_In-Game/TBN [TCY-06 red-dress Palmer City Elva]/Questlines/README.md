# TCY-06 "Elva" — Story & Faction Notes

*Elva is not recruitable. These are narrative/faction design notes.*

## District Role — Leo (Entertainment district)

A 7w8:Sc performer in Leo's entertainment hierarchy — assertive, ambitious, climbing toward recognition that the district's medieval social structure makes hard to hold once gained. She is somewhere in the middle tiers: past "known face," working toward "crowd-stopper," aware that the gap above her is political as much as artistic.

She is likely affiliated with the **Star War established house** — the winning side of Leo's mid-2740s performance-house civil war, which still holds institutional power. Whether she believes in the house's philosophy or is simply using it strategically is a character detail to develop.

## Faction / Collective Affinity

Leo's performance establishment — the winning house of the Star War. Her presence gives the player a window into Leo's entertainment hierarchy and the political history beneath its artistic surface.

She and TCY-42 "Lillian" (7w8:Sc, legacy house) represent the two sides of the Star War divide. Their relationship — rivalry, grudging respect, or something more complex — is a vehicle for navigating Leo politics.

## Narrative Hooks for Player Interaction
- Gate-keeper to Leo's establishment: access to venues, performers, or political figures in Leo's upper tiers may require her approval
- Information source on the Star War and which current figures in Leo trace their power to which side
- Potential obstacle if the player allies with Lillian's side, or unexpected ally if the player helps her achieve something the establishment house cannot give her
