## Visual Description

*(See `Reference_Images/` folder — EW1A78xx/79xx series.)*

Warm honey-gold hair, long and loose — not styled for anyone, just how it falls. It has natural volume and slight wave; in some images partially swept up, in others fully down. Grey-blue eyes, slightly heavy-lidded, with a quality of looking without quite focusing on what's in front of her. Not vacant — watching, but inward. A slight moodiness lives in the eye shape. Full lips, soft features, a small beauty mark visible in close shots. Full-figured and soft — the body of someone whose life doesn't require physical exertion. A delicate chain necklace with a small pendant, a smartwatch-style bracelet, a small toe ring in some shots. Nothing decorative in the expensive sense — just the things she keeps on because she always has.

Her signature at-home clothing is a white floral halter dress with small red flowers scattered across it — light, short, clearly domestic. Not dressed for going out; this is how she dresses when she's only dressing for herself. The folder name ("white shirt Fenny") anchors this as her visual identity.

The expression across every image is the same: quiet, not performing warmth, not cold. Waiting for something she hasn't named. The hands in several shots are held together or resting on herself — self-contained, slightly uneasy with being observed. One image has her looking up from below with something younger and more vulnerable than the forward-facing shots reveal. The clearest image of her character is the one where she's sitting on the stool, chin resting on her fist, not looking at anything in particular: someone who has spent a great deal of time sitting with their own thoughts, not in distress, but not at peace either.

The setting in the reference images — curated sideboard, floral lace tablecloth, tea cup, birdcage lamp, wine bottles — looks like her home should look: warm, privately arranged, cared for without announcement.
