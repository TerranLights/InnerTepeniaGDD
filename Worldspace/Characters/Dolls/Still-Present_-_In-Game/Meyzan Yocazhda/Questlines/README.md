## District Role — Leo (most likely) or Capricorn

A Self-Preservational 3w2 achieves to be secure, using warmth and charm as tools toward that end. Both Leo (entertainment hierarchy) and Capricorn (merit spires) offer visible achievement structures she could ascend. Leo is the stronger candidate for character contrast: she may be the one person in a district where performing yourself is the norm who privately understands the gap between the performance and the person.

## Faction / Collective Affinity (TBD pending setting)

- **If Leo:** Professional entertainers guild or production-side network — she wants to be the person the machine cannot function without. Probably a producer, agent, or choreographer rather than a performer.
- **If Capricorn:** A meritocratic professional guild, possibly on the fringes of the robot rights undercurrent if her drive to succeed has run up against the rigged merit system.
