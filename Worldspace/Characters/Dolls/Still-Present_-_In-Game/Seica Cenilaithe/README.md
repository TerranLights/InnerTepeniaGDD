## Backstory Summary
A Goth robot and Tepenia native. Once had a human husband whom she loved deeply — they loved each other very deeply. He was killed during the Long Night War. She carries a burning hatred against Upper Earth governments and militaries for taking him from her; she would not direct this against innocent citizens and has no way of reaching the actual responsible parties, so it lives inside her.

She came to Concordia and settled in Scorpio, embedded in its Goth community — the faction that believes transformation should be sacred rather than clinical, that death should be honored rather than therapized. Her art and her spiritual practice are the primary channels for what she carries. Her pistol skills exist separately; she is very good with one weapon and not many others.

- Quest Title: "The One I Couldn't Stop" (working title)
- Core conflict & emotional stakes: Her husband was killed in the Long Night War and the people responsible are unreachable. ## Design Notes & Open Questions
- **Husband:** Human; killed during the Long Night War; loved deeply and mutually. His name and identity TBD.
- **The hatred:** Principled and contained. She hates Upper Earth governments and militaries specifically — not random citizens, not the concept of Upper Earth. She would not act against innocents. The hatred has no reachable outlet and so burns internally.
- **Scorpio placement:** She belongs to the Goth community's sacred-transformation tradition, not Scorpio's clinical rebirth infrastructure. The clinical side would feel hollow to her — her husband's death was not a phase or a transformation to be processed on a timeline.
- **Archive of Final Confessions:** Her relationship to it TBD. The question of what remains of her husband — whether something of him persists — is not abstract for her. The Archive may be relevant to her spiritual practice in some form.
- **Art:** Visual art, not music. Likely dark, emotionally powerful in the way an Sx-8w7's expression would be — direct, intense, doesn't explain itself.
- **Pistol mastery:** One-handed non-revolver ballistic pistols specifically. Very skilled. Not broadly combat-versatile, but in her lane: exceptional.
