## Backstory Summary
A Tepenia native from a city that no longer exists (specific city TBD — pending pre-war geography development). Before the Long Night War she was a devoted Heavy Metal and Industrial scene participant — embedded in the community, played guitar privately but almost never on stage. When the war came she watched her home destroyed and ended up in Concordia, settling in Pisces.

She has been salvaging scrap from ruins across wherever she can reach ever since — not as research or collection, but as practical maintenance. She keeps a residential hab compound functional through that work. Over time, a community of people (both robot and human) has gathered around her stable hab; she did not organize this and does not lead it, but she anchors it.

She still listens to Metal, Industrial, and Hard/Garage Rock. She plays guitar occasionally — less than she used to, but the instrument has never left.
