## Backstory Summary
Originally from Upper Earth (exact origin TBD). A protector of the weak and defender of the innocent — this is not a role she was assigned but who she is.

During the final evacuations before Amundsen Tower's destruction, she was at the South Pole holding off Upper Earth military forces with an SMG and ballistic weapons while innocents desperately caught the last rides up the tower. She is, in the most literal sense, someone who stood between people and the guns pointed at them.

The tower was destroyed. She is stranded at what was once the base of Amundsen Tower — now a mountain of scrap at the South Pole. Having spent her life fighting to protect others, she is now the one who needs rescuing. This is the central irony and the central dignity of her DLC.

## Design Notes & Open Questions
- **DLC context:** Kendra is stranded at the South Pole and is the protagonist of her own starring DLC. She does NOT begin in Concordia. The Capricorn affiliation and The Reclaimed Record faction are her background/origin story — the place and cause she came from. Her recruiting arc and companion quest likely unfold entirely within the DLC context (South Pole setting), not in the main city.
- **Capricorn connection:** Even though she's absent from Concordia during the game, her presence is felt there — through The Reclaimed Record movement she helped seed, through other characters who reference her, or through documents/evidence the player can find. She matters to Concordia even before she's encountered.
