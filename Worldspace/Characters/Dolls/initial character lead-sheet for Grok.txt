Name:
	Michelle Stanton
Enneagram series:
	Personality Type: 5w6 (Social Subvariant)
	Major Theme: Thinking
	Hornevian Group: Withdrawn
General Summary:
	A construction robot. She operates a Kharkovchanka with a small team of other robot as well as some very dedicated humans. They travel across Antarctica/Tepenia to build the physical infrastructure for the Arcanet. She's personally responsible for seemingly every regional subnet, i.e., the Palmer ("American") Subnet, the Halley ("Atlantic") Subnet, the Mawson Subnet, the Mirny ("Australian") Subnet, the Janbogo Subnet, and the Byrd ("Pacific") Subnet. She works diligently to make sure Tepenia has a well-developed internet infrastructure.


Name:
	Naizelle d'Edjordoś
Enneagram series:
	Personality Type: 5w6 (Self-Preservational Subvariant)
	Major Theme: Thinking
	Hornevian Group: Withdrawn
General Summary:
	A Tepenia native. She's robot built as a bit of an iconoclast, yet developed very "problem-solving" tendencies, Naizelle is very much a Rocker at heart. During peacetime, she was a gritty yet beautiful soul (to the extent that robots can have souls) who lived at "society's edge". When the Long Night War (or also known as The Midnight War) came, Naizelle was effectively forced to watch the destruction of the only home she's ever known. Now living in post-war Concordia, she spends her time finding scrap hardware, maintaining the residential hab unit that she and her friends (both human and robot) live in, and generally surviving one day at a time.


Name:
	Nilda Faviola "Favi" della Torre
Enneagram series:
	Personality Type: 6w5 (Self-Preservational Subvariant)
	Major Theme: Thinking
	Hornevian Group: Compliant
General Summary:
	Originally created in Italy as a robot assistant in a scientific lab, Favi della Torre served under an elder, frazzle-haired Italian scientist who initially had her built to serve as a sort of "extra set of eyes and ears", double-checking for any possible mistakes and oversights that he may have missed. Over time, the elder scientist came to care for Favi as though she were his own daughter, as his original human daughter had been killed in a vehicular accident years earlier, and he never quite got over the loss. Later exiled to Antarctica following the Falkland Treaty of 2564, she joined up with the Eyes of Gold after falling in love with one of their members, another Italian man, a human. Having outlived him, she now lives in Concordia tending to scientific maintenance, ready to once again take up the gun should the situation require her to.

	Major Theme: Instinctive
	Hornevian Group: Assertive
General Summary:
	Part of a future DLC. A proud and strong robot goddess of war, Kendra prides herself in her ability to protect the weak and defend those who cannot defend themselves. Name:
	Ji-Eun Kim
Enneagram series:
	Personality Type: 5w4 (Social Subvariant)
	Major Theme: Thinking
	Hornevian Group: Withdrawn
General Summary:
	A nanoengineer robot originally from Unified Korea, she'd had to fight her way to the top to gain recognition and respect for her abilities, due to growing anti-robot sentiment leading up to the War of Upper Earth and the signing of the Falkland Treaty. Once resettled in Antarctica, she became the most well-respected nanoengineer in her field, and has built many ocular optic implants for the Eyes of Gold. During the Long Night War, all signs of Ji-Eun Kim were lost. It is currently unknown what happened to her, or her whereabouts, though it is suspected that Ji-Eun may still be alive.


Name:
	Trisha Miller
Enneagram series:
	Personality Type: 8w7 (Social Subvariant)
	Major Theme: Instinctive
	Hornevian Group: Assertive
General Summary:
	A radio DJ robot from Midwestland (located in Old New North America). She was exiled to Antarctica following the Falkland Treaty. A silver-tongued "woman of the people", she's very much of a populist, and although there were some who hated her because she's a robot, she still loved her fans very very dearly. Originally taking up residence in the Tepenian city of Belgrano (which is a "post-war development" of Belgrano Station II in current-day Antarctica), following the Long Night War, she resettled in Concordia and has once again taken up a profession as a radio DJ, and, as always, is very much of a populist, a robot "woman of the people", and loves her fans dearly.


Name:
	Majyao Bisyugota
Enneagram series:
	Personality Type: 4w5 (Self-Preservational Subvariant)
	Major Theme: Feeling
	Hornevian Group: Withdrawn
General Summary:
	A Tepenia native. Originally from the city of Janbogo (which is a "post-war development" of Jang Bogo Station in Antarctica), she owned and operated a teahouse where they served teas made from leaves from all over Antarctica, as well as some illegally smuggled tea leaves from South America. Their specialty was Blood River Tea, which was made (as the name suggests) from leaves grown in soil nourished by Antarctica's Blood Falls. Following the Long Night War, She had to flee Janbogo. However, she chose not to flee into outer space, but rather, to resettle in Concordia, open a new teahouse there, and serve the residents of the city, providing a calm, relaxing environment where they could sit and unwind from the hardships of life in Antarctica.
