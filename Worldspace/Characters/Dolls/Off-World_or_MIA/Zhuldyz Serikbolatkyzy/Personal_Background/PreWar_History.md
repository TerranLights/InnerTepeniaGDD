# [Character Name] — Pre-War History

(Background before the first global war, early activation if applicable, human creators/owners, initial purpose, etc.)

* Originally built to work as a helping hand and assistant on a farm in Kazakhstan
* The Patriarch of the Serikbolat family spent his entire life savings to buy her for his son, Turar Serikbolat, as he was to inherit the land and property, and there would be no other surviving children to work the farm
* Zhuldyz began working on the mechanics of farm equipment (tractors, cutters, plowers, etc)
* Turar and Zhuldyz fell madly, deeply in love and dedicated themselves to each other
* Soon afterwards, the War of Upper Earth came. Turar and Zhuldyz escaped to Antarctica together
* In Tepenia, Zhuldyz put her mechanic skills to use salvaging equipment
* When the Long Night War came, Zhuldyz escaped via Amundsen Tower and later put her skills to use once again cutting up derelict spaceships and selling the scrap for money to pay rent for the hab unit inside the O'Neill Cylinder she lives aboard
* She later moved to Mars, where she continues to live today, and runs her own scrapyard
