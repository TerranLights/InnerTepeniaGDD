## Background

**Early Life at U.R.U.K.**

Hana Jinn was a Data Analytics and Research Expert who initially lived and worked at U.R.U.K. — the Underwater Research Union: Kappa, off the coast of Hawaii. U.R.U.K. holds particular significance in Tepenian history: it is the facility where the first sentient robot was created. What exactly Hana researched there is currently uncertain.

What is known: she was a great lover of whalesong. She listened to the whales singing in the ocean. For a 4w3, this is character in a single detail — deep aesthetic sensibility, the kind of beauty that others walk past.

**Transfer to A.R.U.Ta.G.**

Hana was requested by name to relocate to the Aerial Research Union: Tau Gamma (A.R.U.Ta.G.). She was brought there specifically to research metamaterials for the construction of mass drivers capable of launching reusable rockets into low-Earth orbit — the early engineering groundwork for Tepenia's eventual orbital infrastructure.

Being requested by name matters. She was not reassigned; she was sought out. Her specific expertise was considered irreplaceable.

**Historical Status**

What happened to Hana Jinn after her work at ARUTaG — and whether she survived to see the Falkland Treaty or the wars — is not yet established.
