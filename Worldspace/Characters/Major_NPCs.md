# Characters & Companions

This file contains all named characters and recruitable companions created so far for *Inner Tepenia*.

## Recruitable Companions

Each companion is strongly tied to one Zodiac district and reflects its core values and culture.

### 1. Cancer – Havenward
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-01_Caregiver**  
A deeply empathetic human-robot hybrid caregiver. Warm, protective, and self-sacrificing. She prioritizes the vulnerable above all else and has a calming presence even in crisis.  
**Role**: Support / Healer / Moral Compass  
**Key Traits**: High Humanity, Empathy Protocols expert.

### 2. Taurus – Hearthward
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-02_Cmty_Leader**  
A steadfast, tradition-loving family patriarch. Grounded, patient, and deeply loyal to home and legacy. He values stability and generational continuity.  
**Role**: Tank / Community Leader / Storyteller  
**Key Traits**: High Might & Humanity, strong Diplomatic Negotiation.

### 3. Leo – Resonance Crown
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-03_Performer**  
A charismatic, dramatic performer and cultural icon. Larger-than-life personality with a flair for the theatrical. Craves admiration but deeply believes in the power of art.  
**Role**: Face / Buffer / Inspiration  
**Key Traits**: High Humanity & Agility, Cultural Performance & Resonance expert.

### 4. Scorpio – The Threshold
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-04_Psych-Hl**  
A radical psychologist and mind-healer who specializes in trauma and personal transformation. Intense, perceptive, and unafraid of darkness.  
**Role**: Psychological Support / Insight / Moral Depth  
**Key Traits**: High Investigation & Humanity, Memory & Consciousness Manipulation.

### 5. Aries – Thermal Spire
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-05_Combat-Vet**  
A grizzled, battle-hardened veteran engineer who thrives under pressure. Blunt, resilient, and deeply respectful of raw strength and endurance.  
**Role**: Combat Engineer / Tank / Crisis Leader  
**Key Traits**: High Engine & Might, Thermal Engineering expert.

### 6. Capricorn – Forgeward
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-06_Industrial-Craftsman**  
An ambitious, highly competent industrial visionary. Strategic, hierarchical, and driven by long-term results and efficiency.  
**Role**: Crafter / Strategist / Production Expert  
**Key Traits**: High Calculation & Engine, Siligel Chemistry & Precision Maintenance.

### 7. Aquarius – Ascendant Research
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-07_Eccentric-Dctr**  
A brilliant but eccentric researcher who pushes ethical boundaries in pursuit of progress. Visionary, curious, and sometimes reckless.  
**Role**: Science / Experimental Support / Wild Card  
**Key Traits**: High Calculation & Investigation, Decentralized Systems Design.

### 8. Libra – Governmental Apex
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-08_Diplomat**  
A master diplomat and negotiator with impeccable poise. Calm, balanced, and skilled at finding compromise between opposing sides.  
**Role**: Face / Diplomat / Reputation Manager  
**Key Traits**: High Humanity & Nerve, Diplomatic Negotiation expert.

### 9. Gemini – Janbogo Subnet Nexus
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-09_Master-Hacker**  
An enigmatic information broker who exists half in the physical world and half in the data streams. Witty, elusive, and always three steps ahead.  
**Role**: Information / Hacking / Social Engineering  
**Key Traits**: High Calculation & Investigation, Arcanet Navigation & Hacking.

### 10. Pisces – The Veil Market
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-10_Charmer**  
A charming, silver-tongued underworld operator. Smooth, dangerous, and deeply knowledgeable about the black market and criminal networks.  
**Role**: Smuggler / Infiltrator / Criminal Connections  
**Key Traits**: High Agility & Humanity, Stealth & Infiltration.

### 11. Sagittarius – Frostward Reaches
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-11_Frontiersman**  
A rugged, independent frontier scout and explorer. Tough, resourceful, and deeply in tune with the harsh Antarctic wilderness.  
**Role**: Scout / Survivalist / Explorer  
**Key Traits**: High Engine & Might, Frontier Survival & Cold Adaptation.

### 12. Virgo – Concordia Undergrid
**yet-TBN-NPC_D-12_Mtnc-Engineer**  
A quiet, brilliant maintenance engineer who knows the Undergrid better than anyone. Practical, understated, and deeply committed to keeping the city functioning.  
**Role**: Technician / Repair / Infrastructure Expert  
**Key Traits**: High Agility & Engine, Precision Maintenance & Repair.

---

## Design Notes
- All companions are designed to feel native to their district’s culture and values.
- Each companion has strong mechanical synergy with their district’s themes and playstyle.
- Recruiting and building relationships with them unlocks district-specific storylines, unique perks, and special endings.
