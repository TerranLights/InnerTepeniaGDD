# Tepenian Federation — Cities Overview

All Tepenian cities grew from real Antarctic research stations. For full city profiles (highways, neighbor cities, lore consistency rules), see **[`City_Relationship_Database.md`](City_Relationship_Database.md)**. For individual in-depth city spec files, see **[`Specs/`](Specs/)**. For the original station→city name conventions and design notes, see **[`Station_to_City_Map.md`](Station_to_City_Map.md)**.

Most coastal cities were destroyed or severely damaged during the Long Night War. Only Concordia (inland, Dome C) remains as a fully functioning major city. Byrd and Janbogo survive in damaged or struggling states. Several inland stations survived the war but were never viable population centers due to extreme altitude.

## Reference Notes

- **Six Arcanet subnets:** Palmer ("American") · Halley ("Atlantic") · Mawson · Mirny ("Australian") · Janbogo · Byrd ("Pacific"). Colloquial nicknames in parentheses are informal; official subnet names are always the hub city name.
- **Amundsen Station** was the inter-subnet relay. Its destruction during the Long Night War caused the **Planetary Split Brain** — permanent informational isolation between all six subnets. Each subnet now holds a different version of shared history, and the subnets have no way to reconcile their records except via the pre-split archive cached at the South Pole ruins.
- **Dumont d'Urville and Cape Denison** are geographically in the Dumont d'Urville Sea area but are members of the **Janbogo** subnet — not Mirny.
- **Dome Fuji** is geographically in inland East Antarctica but is in the **Halley** subnet. Subnets were organized by Arcanet cable routing, not strict geographic proximity.
- **Signy** (South Orkney Islands) has weak/intermittent Arcanet connectivity — dashed border on the subnet map. Maritime access only; no road connection possible. Possibly overlooked by Upper Earth targeting due to its peripheral status.
- **[NAME TBD] (Maitri Station location)** and **[NAME TBD] (Bharati Station location)** — both city locations are confirmed for development; "Maitri" and "Bharati" are placeholder names only. Final names TBD.
- **Zukelli** is the confirmed Tepenian city name for Mario Zucchelli Station.
- **Governmental capital of Tepenia:** TBD — Palmer City was the cultural capital, not the governmental one.
