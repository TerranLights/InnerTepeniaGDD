# Tepenian Federation — Cities Overview

All Tepenian cities grew from real Antarctic research stations. Full mapping: see `Station_to_City_Map.md` (same directory). Summary also in `to-be-integrated/miscellaneous/World_History_Reference.md`. Most coastal cities were destroyed during the Long Night War. Concordia (inland, Dome C) is the last major surviving city.

| City | Based On | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Palmer City | Palmer Station | Destroyed (Long Night War) | First settled location in Tepenia; cultural/entertainment capital |
| Janbogo | Jang Bogo Station | Damaged; partially operational | Supplies Concordia; source of refugees |
| Fort McMurdo | McMurdo Station | TBD | "Fort" prefix suggests military significance |
| Belgrano | Belgrano Station II | TBD | Coastal |
| Neumayer | Neumayer Station III | TBD | Coastal |
| Concordia | Concordia Station (Dome C) | Last major surviving city | Primary game setting |
| All others | All other Antarctic stations | TBD | Same station-to-city pattern applies |

**Governmental capital of Tepenia:** TBD — Palmer City was the cultural capital, not the governmental one.
