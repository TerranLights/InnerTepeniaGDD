# District Natural Allies

Genuine synergy — districts whose core functions complement each other, share overlapping goals or external threats, have resource or operational synergies, and/or align in ways that make cooperation feel natural rather than forced. Enneagram alignment noted where relevant.

*Note: District 13 (Concordia Central Hub / Axis Mundi) is absent from this mapping — its neutral crossroads role means it maintains working relationships with all districts rather than strong preferential alliances. To be developed separately.*

---

## 1. Cancer (TBN) – Nurturing / Protective
**Primary ally:** Taurus (TBN) — Both are deeply emotional/residential and focused on bonded life, family stability, and protecting the vulnerable. Cancer's protective services directly enable Taurus' domestic stability.
**Secondary ally:** Libra (TBN) — Cancer's work with refugees, defectors, and the vulnerable benefits from Libra's diplomatic and policy-level protection.

*Why they work well together:* Shared Feeling + Compliant leanings create a "duty to care" synergy. They face similar external pressures (resource competition from industrial districts) and can team up on humanitarian/resource allocation issues.

---

## 2. Taurus (TBN) – Residential / Stability
**Primary ally:** Cancer (TBN) — Mutual (see above).
**Secondary ally:** Virgo / The Undergrid — Taurus needs reliable infrastructure and maintenance to keep its insulated domes, gardens, and daily life running smoothly.

*Why they work well together:* Both prioritize long-term stability and "keeping things running" for ordinary residents. Taurus provides the human/emotional face; Virgo provides the hidden mechanical backbone.

---

## 3. Leo (TBN) – Cultural / Performative
**Primary ally:** Gemini (Janbogo Subnet Nexus) — Culture needs strong information networks, promotion, and audience reach. Gemini's data hubs and communication systems are ideal for broadcasting festivals, performances, and artistic movements.
**Secondary ally:** Pisces / The Markets — Gray-market synergy. Underground cultural scenes, black-market art, and illicit entertainment venues thrive in Pisces' chaotic environment.

*Why they work well together:* Leo brings the spectacle and emotional release; Gemini amplifies it city-wide. Assertive + Feeling energy in Leo pairs naturally with Gemini's information flow.

---

## 4. Scorpio (TBN) – Psychological / Transformative
**Primary ally:** Aquarius / The Labs — Both deal with radical change, experimentation, and pushing human/robot boundaries. Scorpio's rebirth clinics and transformation work align with Aquarius' hybrid tech and enhancement research.
**Secondary ally:** Cancer (TBN) — Deep psychological work often requires emotional support, grief processing, and safe spaces for the vulnerable undergoing transformation.

*Why they work well together:* Shared intensity around personal/collective transformation. They can collaborate on ethical rebirth programs or trauma-recovery tech while facing similar external suspicion from more conservative districts.

---

## 5. Aries (TBN) / The Power Core – Raw Survival / Energy
**Primary ally:** Capricorn (TBN) — Industry runs on power. Aries' energy production directly fuels Capricorn's manufacturing, forges, and heavy infrastructure.
**Secondary ally:** Virgo / The Undergrid — The power grid and geothermal systems require constant high-level maintenance and repair.

*Why they work well together:* Classic producer + heavy consumer + maintainer triangle. All three have strong Instinctive/Assertive or Compliant leanings and deal with high-stakes physical systems.

---

## 6. Capricorn (TBN) – Industrial / Ambition
**Primary ally:** Aries (TBN) / The Power Core — Mutual (see above).
**Secondary ally:** Gemini (Janbogo Subnet Nexus) — Modern industry benefits enormously from data networks, logistics tracking, and information systems for efficient production and supply chains.

*Why they work well together:* Shared drive toward production, efficiency, and long-term building. Capricorn's disciplined ambition pairs well with Gemini's information infrastructure.

---

## 7. Aquarius (The Labs) – Visionary / Experimental
**Primary ally:** Gemini (Janbogo Subnet Nexus) — Research generates and depends on massive amounts of data, verification, and information exchange.
**Secondary ally:** Scorpio (TBN) — Mutual (see above).

*Why they work well together:* Both are forward-looking and experimental (Thinking + Competency overlap). They can collaborate on ethical tech development, hybrid systems, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible in Concordia.

---

## 8. Libra (TBN) – Diplomatic / Balance / Governance
**Primary ally:** Virgo / The Undergrid — Governance needs reliable infrastructure to enforce policies and maintain city-wide order. Virgo's maintenance work makes Libra's rules actually function.
**Secondary ally:** Cancer (TBN) — Protective humanitarian policies require diplomatic backing and resource allocation at the central level.

*Why they work well together:* Both are system-oriented and dutiful (Compliant + Thinking/Competency). Libra sets the rules and balances competing interests; Virgo ensures the physical systems behind those rules keep working.

---

## 9. Gemini (Janbogo Subnet Nexus) – Information / Communication
**Primary ally:** Aquarius / The Labs — Mutual (see above).
**Secondary ally:** Leo (TBN) — Mutual (see above).

*Why they work well together:* Information is the lifeblood of both innovation (Aquarius) and culture (Leo). Gemini acts as the connective tissue that amplifies both.

---

## 10. Pisces (The Markets) – Black Market / Dissolution
**Primary ally:** Sagittarius / The Frostlands — Frontier expansion and smuggling routes often go hand-in-hand. Pisces' illicit trade can supply (and be supplied by) the outer frontier.
**Secondary ally:** Scorpio (TBN) — Thematic overlap. Both operate in morally gray, intense, transformative spaces (vice, underground economies, personal change).

*Why they work well together:* Shared fluid, opportunistic, gray-area nature. They form uneasy but mutually beneficial alliances around resource flows that bypass official channels.

---

## 11. Sagittarius (The Frostlands) – Frontier / Exploration
**Primary ally:** Pisces / The Markets — Mutual (see above).
**Secondary ally:** Aries (TBN) / The Power Core — Frontier outposts need reliable power and heat pushed outward.

*Why they work well together:* Both have an outward, expansive, somewhat rebellious spirit against central control. They thrive on pushing boundaries (literal and figurative).

---

## 12. Virgo (The Undergrid) – Maintenance / Service
**Primary ally:** Libra (TBN) — Mutual (see above).
**Secondary allies:** Aries (TBN) / The Power Core and Capricorn (TBN) — Heavy industrial and power systems require constant, high-quality maintenance.

*Why they work well together:* Virgo is the invisible but essential backbone. It naturally aligns with any district that depends on reliable infrastructure — especially power and industry.
