# District Pair Distinctions – Specific Player Actions

These are concrete actions the player can take that feel native to one district in the pair but would feel out of place (or even hostile) in the other. They help reinforce the unique identity of each Zodiac district.

---

## 1. Cancer (TBN-district) vs Taurus (TBN-district)

**Cancer Focus**: Active protection and caregiving of the vulnerable.  
**Taurus Focus**: Home, tradition, and personal/family stability.

**Suggested Player Actions**:

- **Cancer-specific**: Offer to personally escort a sick child or elderly resident to a safer shelter during a blackout.
- **Cancer-specific**: Help repair or reinforce a damaged community nursery or medical tent.
- **Taurus-specific**: Help a family restore or protect a long-standing family garden or traditional home altar.
- **Taurus-specific**: Participate in (or help organize) a private family memorial or generational ritual.
- **Cross-distinction**: Stealing medicine meant for the vulnerable (angers Cancer more). Stealing a family heirloom from inside a home (angers Taurus more).

---

## 2. Leo (TBN-district) vs Scorpio (TBN-district)

**Leo Focus**: Public image, performance, and cultural expression.  
**Scorpio Focus**: Personal transformation, trauma, and inner shadow work.

**Suggested Player Actions**:

- **Leo-specific**: Volunteer to perform on stage during a major festival (even if badly).
- **Leo-specific**: Help restore or protect a famous public mural or performance venue.
- **Scorpio-specific**: Participate in (or assist someone through) a private psychological transformation ritual.
- **Scorpio-specific**: Help someone confront and process a deeply buried trauma in a safe space.
- **Cross-distinction**: Publicly mocking a performer on stage (angers Leo heavily). Forcefully exposing someone’s private trauma or memories (angers Scorpio heavily).

---

## 3. Aries (TBN-district) vs Capricorn (TBN-district)

**Aries Focus**: Raw power, direct survival, and personal strength.  
**Capricorn Focus**: Organized production, hierarchy, and long-term output.

**Suggested Player Actions**:

- **Aries-specific**: Volunteer for a dangerous, high-risk maintenance shift in the active power core.
- **Aries-specific**: Help defend a critical power relay during an external threat.
- **Capricorn-specific**: Help optimize or speed up a production line through clever organization.
- **Capricorn-specific**: Assist in negotiating better working conditions or resources for factory workers.
- **Cross-distinction**: Recklessly overloading power systems for short-term gain (angers Aries). Sabotaging production quotas or efficiency systems (angers Capricorn).

---

## 4. Aquarius / The Labs vs Gemini (Janbogo Subnet Nexus)

**Aquarius Focus**: Bold experimentation and pushing scientific boundaries.  
**Gemini Focus**: Information flow, communication, and data integrity.

**Suggested Player Actions**:

- **Aquarius-specific**: Volunteer as a test subject for a risky new experimental procedure.
- **Aquarius-specific**: Help recover or protect dangerous but valuable prototype technology.
- **Gemini-specific**: Help verify and spread accurate information during a misinformation crisis.
- **Gemini-specific**: Assist in restoring corrupted data archives or communication networks.
- **Cross-distinction**: Stealing experimental data for personal gain (angers Aquarius). Deliberately spreading false information across the network (angers Gemini).

---

## 5. Libra (TBN-district) vs Pisces / The Markets

**Libra Focus**: Official law, diplomacy, and structured political order.  
**Pisces Focus**: Criminal codes, loyalty, and shadow economy rules.

**Suggested Player Actions**:

- **Libra-specific**: Help mediate or resolve a formal diplomatic dispute between factions.
- **Libra-specific**: Assist in drafting or enforcing new district regulations.
- **Pisces-specific**: Help complete a high-stakes black market deal without getting caught.
- **Pisces-specific**: Help protect a criminal safehouse or smuggling route from rivals.
- **Cross-distinction**: Publicly snitching on government corruption (angers Libra). Snitching on a major criminal operation (angers Pisces).

---

## 6. Sagittarius (The Frostlands) vs Virgo (Undergrid)

**Sagittarius Focus**: Harsh frontier survival and personal independence.  
**Virgo Focus**: Infrastructure maintenance and quiet, essential service.

**Suggested Player Actions**:

- **Sagittarius-specific**: Volunteer for a dangerous long-range scouting or supply run into the ice.
- **Sagittarius-specific**: Help rescue or support stranded explorers in extreme conditions.
- **Virgo-specific**: Help repair critical life-support or power systems deep in the tunnels.
- **Virgo-specific**: Assist in mapping or clearing blocked maintenance routes.
- **Cross-distinction**: Abandoning or betraying someone during a frontier expedition (angers Sagittarius). Sabotaging tunnel infrastructure that keeps the city alive (angers Virgo).

---

## Design Notes

- These actions can be used for:
  - Positive reputation gains
  - Unique questlines
  - Emergent roleplaying opportunities
  - Clear ways to show players “this district values different things”

- Many of these actions can also serve as **reverse triggers** — doing the opposite action in the “wrong” district can cause hostility.
