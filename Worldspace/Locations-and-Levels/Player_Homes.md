# Player Homes

*This document is a stub. Player home locations, layouts, and acquisition conditions require dedicated design work.*

---

## Overview

The player-character can acquire and use player homes throughout Inner Tepenia. There are two categories of player home:

**Standard player homes** — acquired through normal gameplay (purchase, quest reward, faction standing, etc.). These are documented in this file.

**Companion homes** — each romanced companion unlocks their personal home as an additional player home. These are documented in `Game-Mechanics/Core-Mechanics/Companion_System.md` (Romance Reward section) and in each companion's individual README. They exist separately from and in addition to standard homes.
