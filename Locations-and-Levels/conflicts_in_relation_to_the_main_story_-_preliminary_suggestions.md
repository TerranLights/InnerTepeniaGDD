# Act 1 / Act 2/3 triggers for the central main story
### Per "district internal/external conflicts"; how each district is affected (either positively or negatively) by conditions in the world as well as actions by the player (either positive or negative) in relation to the energy crisis.
### Goals:
###### How everything ties together around the core story:
###### (tentative and subject to change)
* Act 1: The Heating Grid Failure (initial crisis in/around the Central Hub / Thermal Spire)
* Act 3 Climax: The Great Blackout (city-wide catastrophe)

####### How the energy crisis and the player’s choices ripple through every district, using their internal and external conflicts.

## 1. To-Be-Named District (Cancer) – Nurturing / Protective
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Blackouts cause mass panic, hypothermia in vulnerable populations, and breakdown of emotional support systems.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Routing emergency heat here saves lives and builds strong Fame.
* Negative: Diverting heat away causes high Infamy (families suffer, children get sick).
##### Internal Ripple: Emotional care programs collapse without power.
##### External Ripple: Blames Hearthward and Thermal Spire.

## 2. Hearthward District (Taurus) – Residential / Stability
##### Energy Crisis Impact: The most emotionally devastating district during blackouts — bonded couples huddling in freezing homes, private gardens failing.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Prioritizing heat here earns massive loyalty from residents.
* Negative: Cutting power here feels like a personal betrayal → huge Infamy.
##### Internal Ripple: Luxury vs emergency housing becomes life-or-death.
##### External Ripple: Strongly conflicts with industrial & cultural districts over heat.

## 3. The Resonance Crown (Leo) – Cultural / Performative
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Spectacles and lights go dark. The district loses its soul and identity.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Allowing emergency power for performances gains cultural goodwill.
* Negative: Cutting power here makes the district feel abandoned and humiliated.
##### Internal Ripple: Grand shows vs grassroots survival events.
##### External Ripple: Furious at Thermal Spire and Hearthward for “selfish” heat priority.

## 4. To-Be-Named District (Scorpio) – Psychological / Transformative
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Blackouts trigger mass despair, suicide spikes, and “death rituals” become disturbingly common.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Providing stable power enables therapeutic rebirth programs.
* Negative: Cutting power here leads to radical, sometimes violent reactions.
##### Internal Ripple: Confronting death becomes literal.
##### External Ripple: Blames the “surface” districts for ignoring psychological needs.

## 5. Thermal Spire & Power Core (Aries) – Raw Survival / Energy
##### Energy Crisis Impact: The epicenter. Overworked, unstable, on the verge of cascading failure.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Stabilizing or upgrading the core earns huge Fame here.
* Negative: Overloading or neglecting maintenance causes catastrophic Infamy.
##### Internal Ripple: Maximum output vs safety becomes the central dilemma.
##### External Ripple: Hated by almost every other district when it fails.

## 6. To-Be-Named District (Capricorn) – Industrial / Ambition
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Factories shut down, production halts, workers idle or desperate.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Allocating power to manufacturing supports long-term growth.
* Negative: Cutting power here stalls the entire city’s industry.
##### Internal Ripple: Quality vs quantity becomes irrelevant when everything stops.
##### External Ripple: Competes fiercely with residential districts.

## 7. Ascendant Research Quarter (Aquarius) – Visionary / Experimental
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Experiments fail, prototypes lose power mid-test, breakthroughs delayed.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Granting research priority enables game-changing tech.
* Negative: Cutting power here stalls humanity’s future.
##### Internal Ripple: Visionary goals vs immediate survival.
##### External Ripple: Seen as selfish by residential districts.

## 8. Governmental Apex (Libra) – Diplomatic / Balance
##### Energy Crisis Impact: The Council is paralyzed trying to fairly distribute failing power.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Helping them enforce fair (or strategic) rationing builds central authority.
* Negative: Undermining them leads to loss of governmental legitimacy.
##### Internal Ripple: Equality vs merit becomes explosive.
##### External Ripple: Blamed by everyone no matter what they decide.

## 9. Janbogo Subnet Nexus (Gemini) – Information / Communication
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Data flows slow or corrupt, rumors explode, coordination breaks down.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Keeping comms online helps coordinate relief.
* Negative: Blackouts here cause city-wide misinformation panics.
##### Internal Ripple: Speed vs accuracy under stress.
##### External Ripple: Accused of spreading panic or hiding truths.

## 10. The Veil Market (Pisces) – Black Market / Underworld
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Business booms — illegal generators, smuggled power cells, desperate customers.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Tolerating or using the Veil Market gets you emergency resources.
* Negative: Cracking down on them during crisis causes underground rebellion.
##### Internal Ripple: Helping desperate vs exploitation peaks.
##### External Ripple: Profiteers from everyone else’s suffering.

## 11. Frostward Reaches (Sagittarius) – Frontier / Exploration
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Outermost districts freeze first. Supply lines collapse.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Sending power outward supports expansion and discovery.
* Negative: Abandoning them feels like betrayal of the pioneer spirit.
##### Internal Ripple: Idealism vs harsh reality becomes brutal.
##### External Ripple: Resents the “soft” central districts.

## 12. Concordia Undergrid (Virgo) – Maintenance / Service
##### Energy Crisis Impact: Tunnels lose power → life support fails, repairs become impossible.
##### Player Actions:
* Positive: Supporting Undergrid prevents total systemic collapse.
* Negative: Ignoring it leads to cascading failures city-wide.
##### Internal Ripple: Perfectionism vs desperate improvisation.
##### External Ripple: Blamed by surface districts when things break.
