### Fast Minmax Endings (Sub-1-Hour Capable)

These high-MACHINE-stat endings are the most realistic secret endings that can be completed in under 1 hour (some in under 30 minutes) with optimized routing and knowledge of the game. They require only **MACHINE stats** and **Traits** from character creation — no perks or skills needed.

#### 1. M + A + E – The Eternal Engine (Best Overall Speed Candidate)
**Requirements**: Machine 8+ + Agility 8+ + Engine 8+

**Why it’s fast**: Heavy emphasis on personal mobility and self-augmentation. You turn yourself into a self-sustaining machine and walk away from the city.

* Heavy focus on personal mobility and self-sufficiency.
* Doesn’t require completing long quest chains or building reputation.
* Can be triggered relatively early by focusing on personal augmentation and leaving the city.

**Optimized Path**:
1. Start with high Agility + Engine for immediate movement and survival advantages.
2. Rush into the Undergrid using risky shortcuts.
3. Locate early augmentation/salvage nodes and rapidly optimize yourself.
4. Head toward the Frostlands exit while the city is still in early chaos.
5. Trigger the ending once you are sufficiently augmented to survive indefinitely in the wastes.

**Estimated Time**: **15–35 minutes**

---

#### 2. M + A + C – The Iron Nomad
**Requirements**: Machine 8+ + Agility 8+ + Calculation 8+

**Why it’s fast**: Focuses on acquiring/building a mobile fortress-vehicle and leaving Concordia behind.

* The goal is building (or acquiring) a mobile fortress-vehicle and simply leaving.
* High Agility helps with fast travel and risky movement.
* Calculation helps with quick optimization of whatever vehicle/tech you find.

**Optimized Path**:
1. Use high Agility to quickly reach industrial or salvage zones (Capricorn or Undergrid hubs).
2. Locate vehicle parts and power sources using Calculation to identify optimal components.
3. Rapidly assemble and optimize a mobile rig.
4. Drive/escape into the Antarctic wasteland.
5. Trigger the ending once you have a functional mobile base.

**Estimated Time**: **20–40 minutes**

---

#### 3. A + N + E – The Phantom Courier
**Requirements**: Agility 8+ + Navigation 8+ + Engine 8+

**Why it’s fast**: Pure mobility-focused independent operator who profits from the chaos without fixing anything.

* Pure mobility and independence focus.
* You operate as a smuggler/messenger who profits from the chaos without fixing anything.
* High Agility + Navigation lets you move freely and find routes others can’t.


**Optimized Path**:
1. High Agility + Navigation for superior routing through Undergrid and Frostlands edges.
2. Quickly establish yourself as a smuggler/messenger by moving goods/information between key points.
3. Operate independently without committing to any faction.
4. Trigger the ending once you’ve proven you can thrive as a ghost in the region.

**Estimated Time**: **20–40 minutes**

---

#### 4. M + A + I – Ghost of the Undergrid
**Requirements**: Machine 8+ + Agility 8+ + Investigation 8+

**Why it’s fast**: You stay underground, claim key tunnel sections, and build a hidden personal empire.

* You stay underground and build a personal hidden empire.
* High Agility + Investigation lets you explore the Undergrid very efficiently.
* Less need to surface or deal with district politics.


**Optimized Path**:
1. Immediately descend into the Undergrid with high Agility.
2. Use Investigation to rapidly map hidden routes and resource nodes.
3. Claim strategic tunnel junctions and siphon resources.
4. Trigger the ending once your underground domain becomes self-sustaining.

**Estimated Time**: **25–45 minutes**

---

#### 5. M + A + N – Unbreakable Ronin (Honorable Mention)
**Requirements**: Machine 8+ + Agility 8+ + Navigation 8+

**Why it’s fast**: You become a legendary lone operative who answers to no one.

* Lone operative who answers to no one.
* High mobility + Navigation lets you operate independently very early.

**Optimized Path**:
1. Use high mobility to avoid faction entanglements.
2. Take or ignore jobs opportunistically while treating the crisis as background noise.
3. Perform a few high-impact independent actions.
4. Trigger the ending once you are fully detached from Concordia’s power struggles.

**Estimated Time**: **25–50 minutes**
