# Alternate Min-Max Endings
**"Fuck You, Not My Problem" Endings for Extreme Specialization**

These endings are available only to heavily min-maxed characters with **three or more MACHINE stats at 8+**. They represent the ultimate expression of player agency — completely bypassing the central conflicts of Concordia through extreme specialization.

## 1. M + A + C — **The Iron Nomad**
You construct a colossal, mobile, self-powered fortress-vehicle using raw strength, precision agility, and flawless computational optimization. You drive off into the Antarctic wasteland, becoming a living legend of absolute independence. Concordia’s energy crisis is nothing but a distant memory in your rear-view mirror.

## 2. M + A + H — **The Wandering Guardian**
You reject the city entirely and become a nomadic protector of frontier settlements and refugee caravans. Your physical dominance and graceful movement let you defend the weak, while your deep Humanity turns you into a beloved folk hero. You help people survive *around* the failing grid without ever fixing it.

## 3. M + A + I — **The Ghost of the Undergrid**
You vanish into the labyrinthine tunnels and abandoned infrastructure, creating a hidden empire of salvaged resources and secret routes. Combining raw power, agility, and unmatched pattern recognition, you siphon just enough for yourself while the surface city slowly collapses.

## 4. M + A + N — **The Unbreakable Ronin**
You become a legendary lone operative who answers to no faction or district. Your overwhelming physical presence, speed, and unbreakable willpower allow you to take any job — or none at all — while treating the entire energy crisis as irrelevant background noise.

## 5. M + A + E — **The Eternal Engine**
Through relentless physical optimization and superhuman endurance, you augment yourself into a being that barely needs external power. You walk away from Concordia and wander the frozen wastes indefinitely, a living machine that never stops.

## 6. M + C + E — **The Forge Lord**
You establish a secret, hyper-efficient personal industrial enclave (likely in the Undergrid or frontier). Using brute strength, cold calculation, and endless endurance, you build your own decentralized forge system that outproduces entire districts. The main grid can fail — yours never will.

## 7. M + H + E — **The Last Shepherd**
You gather a loyal group of humans and robots and lead them out of the dying city. Your strength, empathy, and superhuman endurance allow you to guide them across the ice to found a new, small, self-sufficient settlement. You didn’t fix Concordia — you saved who mattered.

## 8. M + I + E — **The Relic Hunter**
You dedicate your life to hunting pre-war relics and lost technology across Antarctica. Your physical power, analytical genius, and tireless endurance let you survive expeditions no one else can. You eventually disappear into legend with a trove of ancient secrets.

## 9. M + N + E — **The Unyielding Tyrant**
You carve out and fortify your own sovereign territory through sheer force of will and physical dominance. Your unbreakable nerve and endurance allow you to rule it as you see fit while the rest of the city tears itself apart.

## 10. A + N + E — **The Phantom Courier**
You become the ultimate independent smuggler, messenger, and information broker — a ghost who moves freely across the entire region. Your agility, willpower, and endless endurance let you profit from everyone’s problems without ever solving them.

## 11. C + N + E — **The Architect of Collapse**
You coldly calculate the exact moment and manner of the central grid’s failure, then position yourself (and possibly a small chosen group) to perfectly thrive in the aftermath. Your intellect, nerve, and endurance turn societal collapse into your personal golden age.

## 12. H + N + E — **The Beloved Exile**
You inspire and lead a genuine mass exodus of people who choose to abandon Concordia. Through deep empathy, unbreakable will, and superhuman endurance, you help found a new society based on cooperation rather than the old failing system.

## 13. I + N + E — **The Eternal Seeker**
You pursue the deepest truths of pre-war history and Tepenia’s hidden secrets. Your analytical brilliance, willpower, and tireless endurance allow you to explore places no one else can reach. You eventually vanish into legend, caring nothing for the city’s petty crises.

---

### Design Notes
- These endings are intentionally **extremely difficult** to achieve (requiring three 8+ stats + significant investment in related skills/perks).
- They all maintain the core philosophy of **maximum player agency** and “Fuck you, not my problem.”
- Many can combine with or branch from the main Hidden Paths (Independent Lattice, Lone Lattice, etc.).
- Epilogue slides / narration should feel powerful and mythic, rewarding the player for extreme specialization.
