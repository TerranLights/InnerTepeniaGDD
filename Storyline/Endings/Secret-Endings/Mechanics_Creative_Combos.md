# Mechanics Creative Combos
**For Hidden Paths & Secret Endings**

This document catalogs the **non-obvious mechanical synergies** that enable the high-agency bypass routes (Independent Lattice, Burn and Rebuild, Lone Lattice, Narrative Lattice). These combos are deliberately complex and rewarding — they require understanding how different systems (MACHINE stats, perks, environmental interaction, timing, reputation, crafting, and district ripples) interact.

## Core Design Principle
Every major hidden path must reward **creative chaining** of mechanics rather than raw stat grinding. Blackouts, overclocks, and district conflicts are not obstacles — they are **tools**.

---

### 1. Timing & Environmental Interaction Combos
**Core Hook**: The ongoing Thermal Spire crisis creates natural “windows” the player can exploit without fixing anything.

- **Ripple Weaver Perk + Blackout Timing**  
  During a natural blackout (triggered by the crisis the player is ignoring), scan fluctuating power lines → convert environmental damage into usable data or salvage.  
  *Best used in*: Virgo Undergrid, Pisces tunnels, Sagittarius frontier outposts.

- **Jury-Rig Virtuoso + Crisis Fluctuations**  
  Turn leaking pipes / overloaded nodes / ice damage into assets (self-cooling micro-nodes, wind-assisted generators, etc.).  
  *Signature for*: Independent Lattice Stage 2 & 3.

- **Environmental Hazard Exploitation (Virgo + Sagittarius)**  
  High Maintenance + Survival during overclock events → repurpose damaged infrastructure into decentralized prototypes without alerting central authorities.

---

### 2. Information & Data Combos
**Core Hook**: Gemini and Aquarius are the information backbone of the bypass paths.

- **Data Ghost Perk + Gemini Rumor Networks**  
  Stealthily extract suppressed pre-war research while spreading selective misinformation.  
  *Used in*: All four hidden paths.

- **Analysis/Data (7+) + Aquarius Lab Access**  
  Cross-reference Gemini rumors with restricted experimental data during a power dip (when security is weakest).  
  *Signature for*: Independent Lattice Stage 1 and Narrative Lattice.

- **Ripple Weaver + Information Warfare**  
  Predict exactly when a blackout will hit → time data leaks or rumor campaigns for maximum destabilizing (or stabilizing) effect.  
  *Best for*: Burn and Rebuild + Narrative Lattice.

---

### 3. Crafting & Fabrication Combos
**Core Hook**: Crafting is never just “make item X” — it’s about turning district resources and crises into parallel solutions.

- **Jury-Rig Virtuoso + Capricorn Neutral Deals**  
  Fabricate components without promising to support the main grid (diplomacy check required).

- **Maintenance (7+) + Pisces Smuggling**  
  Smuggle salvaged Virgo nodes + black-market stabilizers → create hidden micro-reactors that run independently.

- **Modular Crafting + Survival/Exploration**  
  Build expandable “lattice nodes” that can be deployed in harsh Sagittarius conditions and later linked into residential districts.  
  *Signature for*: Independent Lattice & Lone Lattice.

---

### 4. Social & Reputation Combos
**Core Hook**: Diplomacy and faction reputation become force multipliers when combined with technical proof.

- **Diplomacy (6+) + Prototype Evidence**  
  Show working micro-nodes to desperate residential districts (Cancer/Taurus) or frontier groups (Sagittarius) → they adopt the Lattice organically.

- **Enneagram Group Understanding + Targeted Dialogue**  
  Use knowledge of district Enneagram leanings (Compliant caregivers in Cancer, Assertive independents in Pisces/Sagittarius, etc.) to push the right emotional buttons without committing to central solutions.

- **Neutral/Independent Reputation Management**  
  Maintain low entanglement with Aries/Capricorn power blocs while building positive ties with frontier, underworld, and certain robot religions.

---

### 5. Path-Specific Signature Combos

**Independent Lattice**  
Ripple Weaver + Jury-Rig Virtuoso + Modular Crafting + Diplomacy timing during blackouts.

**Burn and Rebuild**  
Ripple Weaver + Stealth/Sabotage + Information Warfare + Sagittarius evacuation prep.

**Lone Lattice**  
Extreme personal Crafting + Survival + Aquarius hybrid tech stealth acquisition + portable rig construction.

**Narrative Lattice**  
Data Ghost + Analysis/Diplomacy + Evidence Forging + Gemini/Libra information control.

### Implementation Notes for Designers
- **Difficulty Curve**: These combos should feel almost impossible on a first blind playthrough. Drop subtle hints via environmental storytelling, NPC rumors, and lore documents.
- **Feedback**: When a player successfully chains a combo, give strong positive feedback (special animations, unique sound cues, or NPC reactions).
- **Failure States**: Failing a combo should not soft-lock the game — it simply makes the hidden path harder or shifts the player toward a different ending.
- **Balance**: These paths should be slightly *more* difficult than the “standard” routes, but far more satisfying for completionist / creative players.
- **MACHINE Stat Synergy**: Encourage hybrid builds (high Engineering + high Analysis + solid Diplomacy) rather than pure min-maxing.
