### Speedrun Design & Optimal Routes

**Target Times**:
- Casual optimized run: ~45–60 minutes
- Serious speedrun (with knowledge): **under 20 minutes**
- Theoretical god-run (heavy sequence breaking): **8–15 minutes**

#### Fastest Theoretical Route (Any% Main Quest)
1. Leave Calethina’s lab → immediately head to the most critical blackout zone.
2. Perform the absolute minimum triage tasks to unlock the first major decision hub.
3. Rush to the Central Grid Access Point (bypass most district content via Undergrid routes or risky shortcuts).
4. Commit hard to one path early (usually **Technocratic Renewal** or **Lattice Federation** are fastest).
5. Complete only the 2–3 required key decisions for that path.
6. Trigger the ending sequence at the Final Stabilization Node.

**Intentional Sequence Breaks to Support**:
- Skipping entire districts by using environmental exploits (blackout timing, pipe systems, condemned areas).
- Early access to key items/terminals via high-risk jumps or glitches (we can make some “intended” sequence breaks).
- Starting data cores or overrides that let you skip straight to governance decisions.
- Using emergency Calethina radio prompts (if she’s not ignored) for fast travel or shortcuts.
- “Just Works” style exploits where you jury-rig temporary fixes that count as progress toward multiple chapters at once.

**Route Variants**:
- **Technocrat Speedrun** — Fastest overall (centralized systems = fewer locations to visit).
- **Lattice Speedrun** — Slightly slower but very sequence-break friendly (build small lattices in 2–3 key spots and call it done).
- **Minimalist Hybrid** — Requires the most precise movement but shows off game knowledge.
