# City Grid-Map with Color-Coded Districts
## General Guidelines
* Rings: Ring 1 (innermost annular, touching center) → Ring 2 → Ring 3 → Ring 4 → Ring 5 (outermost).
* Radials: 12 slices clockwise. Use the labeled highways as landmarks (Hwy 37 ramp roughly left/9 o’clock area, Hwy 110 rightish/2 o’clock, Hwy 183 bottom/6 o’clock).
* Each district gets a connected block (or small cluster of connected sub-blocks if needed for flow).

## District Assignments (with cell count, placement, and rationale):
### 1. Cancer (Nurturing/Protective – Sanctuary, care facilities, refugees, gardens): 
* **Pittance:** 3 cells
* **Occupancy:** Mid rings (Rings 2-3), spanning 2-3 adjacent radials in a compact-to-medium block near central access.
* **Rationale:** Needs space for creches, healing gardens, shelters, and refugee integration without being too sprawling. Good central-ish access for inflows from outer districts. Clusters naturally with Taurus.
### 2. Taurus (Residential/Stability – Bonded habitation, family homes, comfort):
* **Pittance:** 6 cells
* **Occupancy:** Mid-to-outer rings (Rings 2-4), broader connected arc/block (spanning ~3 radials) for the “habitation rings” feel. Adjacent to Cancer.
* **Rationale:** Primary living space — largest footprint for multi-generational homes, private gardens, schools, and community areas. Lore emphasizes it as a major residential sector.
### 3. Leo (Cultural/Performative – Amphitheaters, spectacles, arts):
* **Pittance:** 4 cells
* **Occupancy:** Mid rings (Rings 2-4), prominent block near a major spoke/highway for visibility and event access (e.g., near Hwy 110 or central radials).
* **Rationale:** Needs prominent, accessible space for venues and festivals. Benefits from good connectivity without dominating the absolute center.
### 4. Scorpio (Psychological/Transformative – Therapy, rebirth rituals, intense inner work):
* **Pittance:** 4 cells
* **Occupancy:** Mid-Outer rings (Rings 3-4), contained block.
* **Rationale:** More compact and specialized/intense vibe; ritual and therapy spaces don’t need vast sprawl. Can feel somewhat secluded yet accessible.
### 5. Aries (Raw Survival/Energy – Power Core generation, heroic infrastructure):
* **Pittance:** 5 cells
* **Occupancy:** Mid rings (Rings 2-3), positioned with strong radial reach (near or between industrial/power-relevant spokes).
* **Rationale:** Generation and distribution hub (with central control under Neutral 13). Needs space for pipes, reactors, and heroic-scale infrastructure while creating natural tensions with neighbors. Not the absolute center (control is there).
### 6. Capricorn (Industrial/Ambition – Foundries, manufacturing, merit-based production): 
* **Pittance:** 6 cells
* **Occupancy:** Mid-to-outer along/near Hwy 37 (Mountain Cut) area — connected block spanning relevant radials.
* **Rationale:** Heavy industry and production footprint; aligns with “Mountain Cut Industrial Zone” lore. Larger area for yards, forges, and worker housing. Clusters with Aries (power) and Virgo (maintenance).
### 7. Aquarius (Visionary/Experimental – Labs, prototypes, innovation):
* **Pittance:** 7 cells
* **Occupancy:** Mid rings (Rings 2-4), block that can feel somewhat specialized/connected to industrial or testing areas.
* **Rationale:** Needs dedicated lab/testing space with some isolation for experiments but good connectivity. Lore ties it to power demands and occasional ethical tensions.
### 8. Libra (Diplomatic/Balance/Governance – Council, resource allocation, diplomacy):
* **Pittance:** 4 cells
* **Occupancy:** Inner-mid rings (Rings 1-2 or 2-3), block close to the central Neutral District for governance synergy (distinct from the Hub itself).
* **Rationale:** Political/admin heart with need for council chambers and brokerage space. Benefits from proximity to the central nexus without overlapping it.
### 9. Gemini (Janbogo Subnet Nexus – Information/Communication, data hubs):
* **Pittance:** 5 cells
* **Occupancy:** Inner rings or strong central connectivity (Rings 1-2), block with good spoke access.
* **Rationale:** Data/comms infrastructure benefits from proximity to the central hub and transit. Compact but well-connected.
### 10. Pisces (Black Market/Underworld – Hidden bazaars, smuggling, vice):
* **Pittance:** 5 cells
* **Occupancy:** Mid rings (Rings 2-4), tucked but connected block near a highway ramp (ideally near Hwy 183 or 37 for smuggling access per lore).
* **Rationale:** Needs discreet/hidden character with highway connections (Smuggler’s Highway entrances). Smaller footprint fits its shadowy nature.
### 11. Sagittarius (Frostlands/Frontier – Outer expansion, harsh environment, outposts):
* **Pittance:** 8 cells
* **Occupancy:** Outermost ring (Ring 5 primarily) + some Ring 4, contiguous arc on one “side” of the circle (outer frontier feel).
* **Rationale:** Explicitly peripheral/outer in layout lore. Larger area for partially completed domes, watchtowers, and frontier character. Good for refugee intake at the edge.
### 12. Virgo (Undergrid/Maintenance – Underground network entrances): 
* **Pittance:** 3 cell [separate/non-contiguous]
* **Occupancy:** Ring 1 (innermost), directly touching the central Neutral 13th District circle. (Position it logically near industrial/power areas for maintenance synergy, e.g., toward the Hwy 37 side.)
* **Rationale:** Minimal surface presence — most of Virgo is underground. One (or optionally two separate) entrance cell(s) in the inner ring fits perfectly. Lore emphasizes it as the connective under-layer with hidden access tunnels.

## Overall Balance & Flow Notes
* Visual/functional balance:
    * Roughly even distribution around the circle
    * Larger blocks (Taurus, Capricorn, Sagittarius) give weight where needed;
    * smaller ones keep specialized districts distinct
    * No single district dominates half the map.
* Adjacencies & synergies (from conflicts and history):
    * Cancer-Taurus cluster for care + living
    * Aries-Capricorn-Virgo for power-industry-maintenance logic
    * Pisces near highways
    * Sagittarius outer with some adjacency to industrial/frontier tensions
    * Leo prominent
    * central-ish support for Libra/Gemini/Aries control flow
* Highway & radial integration:
    * Districts cluster or border the labeled ramps where it enhances lore
        * industrial near Hwy 37
        * black market access
        * frontier outer
* Undergrid handling:
    * Minimal surface presence
    * Near inner ring
    * Serves as multi-port “entranceway” to the underground network
* Player/quest flow:
    * Central hub as true nexus
    * Outer frontier for exploration/survival
    * Power/industry clustered for crisis propagation
    * Cultural and residential prominent yet accessible
