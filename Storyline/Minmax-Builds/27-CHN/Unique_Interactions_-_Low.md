# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 27: Calculation + Humanity + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **socially tone-deaf, panicky, terrible-planner**: a character who makes awful strategic decisions, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, panics and freezes under pressure, and struggles to connect with or inspire others, often becoming a pitiable burden.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s poor planning, emotional clumsiness, and easy panic. These limitations frequently cause awkward social mishaps, failed leadership or decision-making attempts, and situations where the player is seen as a harmless burden. This can lead to pity-based alliances, protective behavior from others, or accidental usefulness when the player is dismissed as irrelevant.
