# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 27: Calculation + Humanity + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a brilliant unbreakable empath: a coldly calculating, deeply compassionate leader who plans perfect long-term outcomes, inspires loyalty through heart and moral clarity, and holds unbreakable nerve through the worst crises while rejecting Concordia’s failing systems.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Architect of Collapse (visionary leader variant)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (strategic empath variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated moral leadership, empathetic foresight, and fearless endurance.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward strategic foresight, genuine emotional connection, and unbreakable will under pressure. The player calculates optimal paths with heart and intellect, inspires and protects a chosen few, and endures hell with perfect composure — then leads their circle away from the city’s collapse.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Calculation + Humanity + Nerve triad — blending strategic foresight, genuine empathetic leadership, and unbreakable will under pressure.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (strategic command sanctuary) → NPC 9 (caravan leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while alienating larger districts reliant on brute force or central planning.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you calculate the best path with heart and intellect, endure hell with unbreakable nerve, protect those you choose, and leave the rest behind.
