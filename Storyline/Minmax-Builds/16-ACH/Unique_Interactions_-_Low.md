# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 16: Agility + Calculation + Humanity (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, difficulty with optimization), and **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds).  
This creates a **clumsy, socially awkward, terrible-planner**: a character who trips over everything, makes awful plans, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, and struggles to connect with or inspire others, often becoming a pitiable burden.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, bad planning, and social tone-deafness. These limitations frequently cause awkward moments, failed attempts at leadership or stealth, and being underestimated or pitied. This can lead to pity-based alliances, accidental usefulness when others compensate, or situations where the player’s incompetence inadvertently reveals information.
