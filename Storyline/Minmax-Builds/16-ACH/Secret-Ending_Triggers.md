# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 16: Agility + Calculation + Humanity

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding).  
This creates a graceful strategic diplomat: a lightning-fast, brilliantly calculating leader who moves like a ghost, plans perfect outcomes, and inspires deep loyalty in a chosen few while rejecting the failing systems of Concordia.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Phantom Courier (charismatic variant)  
- Iron Nomad (strategic diplomat variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful mobility, calculated leadership, and selective heartfelt care.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward fluid high-speed action, cold strategic foresight, and genuine emotional connection. The player darts through danger with grace, calculates optimal paths for their chosen circle, and inspires loyalty through charisma and moral leadership — then leads their small group away from the dying city.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Calculation + Humanity triad — blending graceful high-speed action, strategic foresight, and genuine empathetic leadership.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (high-mobility command vehicle) → NPC 9 (caravan leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while alienating larger districts reliant on brute force or central control.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you move like lightning, calculate the perfect path, protect those you choose with heart, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
