# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 16: Agility + Calculation + Humanity

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding).  
This creates a **graceful strategic diplomat**: a lightning-fast, brilliantly calculating leader who moves like a ghost, plans perfect long-term outcomes, and inspires deep loyalty in a chosen few while rejecting the failing systems of Concordia.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Phantom Courier (charismatic variant)  
- Iron Nomad (strategic diplomat variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful mobility, calculated leadership, and selective heartfelt care.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine fluid high-speed action, cold strategic foresight, and genuine emotional connection. The player is seen as an elegant, trustworthy leader who can slip through danger, calculate the best path forward, and inspire others to follow.
