# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 33: Humanity + Investigation + Engine (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details), and **Engine** (poor mechanical aptitude, inability to repair or craft effectively, tendency to break things).  
This creates a **socially tone-deaf, oblivious, destructive tinker-failure**: a character who misses obvious clues, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, breaks or ruins technology they touch, and struggles to connect with others, often becoming a pitiable burden or walking disaster.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s social awkwardness, investigative blindness, and mechanical incompetence. These limitations frequently cause awkward social mishaps, broken equipment, missed opportunities, and situations where the player is seen as a liability or harmless fool. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others fix what the player broke.
