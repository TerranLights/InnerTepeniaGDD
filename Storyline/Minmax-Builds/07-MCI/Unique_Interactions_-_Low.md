# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 07: Might + Calculation + Investigation (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Might** (physical weakness, poor endurance, and limited protective capability), **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), and **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details).  
This creates a **clumsy, oblivious weakling**: a physically frail character who constantly misses important details, makes terrible plans, and fails to connect obvious clues, often stumbling into situations through sheer incompetence or being underestimated.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s physical frailty, mental sluggishness, and investigative blindness. These limitations frequently cause the player to overlook dangers, make poor decisions, and appear harmless or pitiable. This can lead to unexpected advantages through pity, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental discoveries when others compensate for the player’s incompetence.
