# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 07: Might + Calculation + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a calculating enforcer-scout: a physically imposing, strategically brilliant investigator who uncovers hidden truths, dominates obstacles through force when needed, and uses perfect information + planning to achieve total self-reliance.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Iron Nomad (strategic variant)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (calculated variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward information dominance, strategic foresight, and physical enforcement.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward the combination of brute physical capability, cold strategic calculation, and relentless investigation. The player uncovers what others miss, plans the perfect moves, and physically enforces their will — then walks away from Concordia’s collapsing systems with superior knowledge and power.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Calculation + Investigation triad — combining physical dominance, strategic foresight, and relentless information gathering.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (mobile command lab) → NPC 9 (scouting expedition leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant massive information and tactical advantages while alienating districts that prefer secrets stay buried or controlled.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover the truth, calculate the perfect moves, physically enforce your will, and leave the ignorant and inefficient behind.
