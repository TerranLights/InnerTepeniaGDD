# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 07: Might + Calculation + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a **calculated truth-enforcer**: a physically overwhelming investigator who uncovers hidden truths, plans perfect strategic moves, and physically dominates obstacles while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Unbreakable Ronin (investigator variant)  
- Iron Nomad (strategic scout variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward information dominance, calculated precision, and physical enforcement.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine overwhelming physical presence, cold strategic calculation, and relentless investigation. The player is seen as a dangerous, highly competent truth-seeker who physically enforces their will and uncovers what others want hidden.
