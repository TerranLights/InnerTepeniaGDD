# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 17: Agility + Calculation + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a **phantom operative**: a lightning-fast, brilliantly calculating investigator who moves like a ghost, uncovers hidden truths others miss, and executes flawless plans with unmatched precision while rejecting Concordia’s failing systems.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Phantom Courier  
- Architect of Collapse (scout variant)  
- Iron Nomad (high-mobility scout variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward information dominance, graceful mobility, and calculated stealth.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine fluid high-speed movement, cold strategic foresight, and relentless investigation. The player is seen as an untouchable, highly competent shadow who uncovers secrets, calculates perfect moves, and vanishes before anyone can react.
