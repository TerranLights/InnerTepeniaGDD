# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 17: Agility + Calculation + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a phantom operative: a lightning-fast, brilliantly calculating investigator who moves like a ghost, uncovers hidden truths others miss, and executes perfect plans with unmatched precision while rejecting Concordia’s failing systems.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Phantom Courier  
- Architect of Collapse (scout variant)  
- Iron Nomad (high-mobility scout variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward information dominance, graceful mobility, and calculated stealth.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward fluid high-speed action, cold strategic foresight, and relentless investigation. The player darts through danger unseen, pieces together secrets with perfect clarity, and executes flawless plans — then vanishes with their knowledge and freedom, leaving the city to its ignorance.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Calculation + Investigation triad — blending graceful high-speed movement, strategic foresight, and relentless secret uncovering.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (mobile reconnaissance lab) → NPC 9 (scouting expedition leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant unmatched mobility, information dominance, and tactical advantages while alienating districts that prefer secrets stay buried.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you move unseen, uncover the truth, calculate the perfect path, and leave the blind and slow behind.
