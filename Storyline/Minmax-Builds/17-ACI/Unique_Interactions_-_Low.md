# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 17: Agility + Calculation + Investigation (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), and **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details).  
This creates a **clumsy, oblivious, terrible-planner**: a character who trips over everything, misses obvious clues, makes awful strategic decisions, and fails to connect basic information, often becoming a pitiable liability or accidental comic relief.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, investigative blindness, and poor planning. These limitations frequently cause awkward mishaps, missed opportunities, failed stealth attempts, and situations where the player is underestimated or pitied. This can lead to pity-based alliances, accidental discoveries when others compensate, or moments where the player’s incompetence inadvertently reveals information.
