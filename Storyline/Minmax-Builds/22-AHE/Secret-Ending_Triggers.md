# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 22: Agility + Humanity + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication).  
This creates a graceful guardian artisan: a lightning-fast, deeply empathetic leader who moves with fluid grace, forges protective gear and mobile sanctuaries on the move, and inspires loyalty in a chosen few while rejecting the failing systems of Concordia.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Iron Nomad (with loyal crew)  
- Eternal Engine (mobile artisan variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful high-mobility leadership, heartfelt care, and hands-on fabrication.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward fluid high-speed action, genuine emotional connection, and masterful on-the-move engineering. The player darts through danger with grace, builds real hope and protection for those they care about, and fabricates solutions in motion — then leads their chosen circle away from the collapsing city.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Humanity + Engine triad — blending graceful high-speed movement, genuine empathetic leadership, and masterful hands-on fabrication.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 7 (nomad convoy) → NPC 9 (caravan protector) → NPC 6 (guardian mobile habitat).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while rapidly alienating larger districts and central authority.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you move with grace, protect those you choose with heart and skill, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
