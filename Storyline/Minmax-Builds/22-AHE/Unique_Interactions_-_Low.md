# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 22: Agility + Humanity + Engine (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), and **Engine** (poor mechanical aptitude, inability to repair or craft effectively, tendency to break things).  
This creates a **clumsy, socially awkward, destructive tinker-failure**: a character who trips constantly, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, and breaks or ruins technology they try to fix, often becoming a walking disaster or pitiable burden.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, emotional tone-deafness, and mechanical incompetence. These limitations frequently cause awkward social mishaps, broken equipment, failed repairs, and situations where the player is seen as a liability or harmless fool. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others fix what the player broke.
