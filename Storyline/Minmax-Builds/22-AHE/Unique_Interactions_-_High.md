# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 22: Agility + Humanity + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication).  
This creates a **graceful guardian artisan**: a lightning-fast, deeply empathetic leader who moves with fluid grace, fabricates real protection and sanctuaries on the move, and inspires loyalty in a chosen few while rejecting the failing systems of Concordia.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Iron Nomad (with loyal crew)  
- Eternal Engine (mobile artisan variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful high-mobility leadership, heartfelt care, and hands-on fabrication.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine fluid high-speed movement, genuine emotional connection, and masterful on-the-move engineering. The player is seen as an elegant, trustworthy protector who can slip through danger, build real hope and safety for those they care about, and inspire others through both heart and skill.
