# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 19: Agility + Calculation + Engine (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), and **Engine** (poor mechanical aptitude, inability to repair or craft effectively, tendency to break things).  
This creates a **clumsy, inept tinker-failure**: a character who trips constantly, makes terrible plans, and breaks or ruins technology they try to fix, often becoming a walking disaster or pitiable burden.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, poor planning, and mechanical incompetence. These limitations frequently cause mishaps, broken equipment, failed repairs, and situations where the player is seen as a liability or harmless fool. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others step in to fix what the player broke.
