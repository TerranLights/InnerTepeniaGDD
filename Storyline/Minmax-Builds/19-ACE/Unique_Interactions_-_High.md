# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 19: Agility + Calculation + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication).  
This creates a **lightning-fast strategic artisan**: a ghost-like operative who moves with perfect grace, calculates optimal outcomes, and fabricates or modifies technology on the fly while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Phantom Courier  
- Iron Nomad (high-mobility variant)  
- Eternal Engine (speed artisan variant)  
- Architect of Collapse (mobile fabrication variant)  
- Forge Lord (lightning craftsman variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward high-speed fabrication, calculated mobility, and graceful self-sufficiency.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine fluid high-speed movement, cold strategic foresight, and masterful on-the-move engineering. The player is seen as an untouchable, highly competent fabricator who can turn scrap into superior solutions in seconds and vanish before anyone reacts.
