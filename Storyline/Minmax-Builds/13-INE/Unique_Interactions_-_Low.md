# Minmax Build 13: Investigation + Nerve + Engine (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Investigation (≤3)**, **low Nerve (≤3)**, and **low Engine (≤3)**. The player is perceptually oblivious (misses obvious details, clues, and environmental cues), mentally fragile (easily frightened, quick to panic or give up), and has extremely poor endurance — tires out almost immediately from any effort.

This creates a **"blind, fearful, and constantly exhausted"** underdog archetype. The character frequently misses critical information, panics under pressure, and burns out so quickly that they become a major burden. They are often pitied, protected, dismissed as useless, or overlooked entirely. Their triple limitations can open unique paths through sympathy, being ignored as non-threatening, accidental discoveries while panicking or resting, or reliance on other stats (especially Calculation, Humanity, Might, or Agility).

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize perceptual blindness, mental fragility, and rapid burnout. They should feel limiting and frustrating, but also create opportunities via pity, protective alliances, "coward's luck," or accidental discoveries from fumbling while tired. Many can lead to underdog questlines, "protected oblivious burden" dynamics, or unconventional routes toward minmax endings.

## Design Notes
- These interactions highlight the character's **perceptual blindness, mental fragility, and extremely poor stamina** — they miss the obvious, panic easily, tire out almost immediately, and are often a burden, strongly inviting pity and being underestimated.
- Many interactions can combine with high stats in Calculation, Humanity, Might, or Agility for strong contrast (e.g., a physically capable but oblivious, fearful, and quickly exhausted character).
- Questlines often lean into pity-based alliances, "oblivious fearful burden," or accidental discovery while hiding/resting from panic and fatigue.
