# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 13: Investigation + Nerve + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, secret uncovering, forensics, and pattern recognition), **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication).  
This creates a fearless relic-forger and shadow-scavenger: an ice-nerved detective-engineer who uncovers hidden truths and lost tech in the most dangerous places, endures lethal risks, and rebuilds ancient secrets into superior personal systems while completely abandoning the collapsing districts.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Architect of Collapse (reconstructionist variant)  
- Eternal Engine  
- Iron Nomad (explorer/scavenger variant)  
- Phantom Courier (stealth-relic variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward relentless secret-hunting, daring exploration, and personal technical resurrection of the past.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward sharp-eyed deduction, fearless dives into the unknown, and masterful hands-on reconstruction. The player pieces together forgotten history and tech through investigation, pushes through mortal danger with raw nerve, and forges lost relics into personal power — then vanishes with their hoard, leaving Concordia and its problems behind.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Investigation + Nerve + Engine triad — every challenge combines sharp deduction, fearless endurance under pressure, and hands-on technical reconstruction of the lost.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries archives) → NPC 6 (mobile fabrication lab).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant massive personal relic advantages and crafting depth while quickly alienating districts that prefer secrets stay buried or regulated.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover the truth, brave the deadliest places, rip the past out with your hands, rebuild it better for yourself alone, and leave the ignorant present to crumble.
