# Minmax Build 13: Investigation + Nerve + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines perception, deduction, clue-tracking, secret uncovering, forensics, and pattern recognition (**Investigation**), courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude (**Nerve**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at uncovering hidden truths in the deadliest places, enduring mortal peril without flinching, and reconstructing lost or broken tech into superior personal systems while pursuing total independence.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward sharp deduction paired with fearless dives into danger and masterful on-the-spot reconstruction. The player pieces together forgotten secrets, physically claims them under extreme risk, and rebuilds them better for themselves alone. Many open unique questlines involving deep relic hunting, forbidden archive reconstruction, high-risk infrastructure exploits, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Investigation + Nerve alongside Engine for dangerous clue chains and technical resurrection under pressure.
