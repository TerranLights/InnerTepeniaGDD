# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 06: Might + Calculation + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Calculation** (optimization, predictive modeling, strategic planning, hacking, resource efficiency, and pattern mastery), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication).  
This creates a strategic one-robot industrial singularity: a brutally strong optimizer who calculates perfect solutions, rips apart failing systems, rebuilds them into superior personal technology, and achieves total self-sufficiency while rejecting all external dependencies.

**Target Endings**:  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Eternal Engine  
- Forge Lord (lone variant)  
- Iron Nomad (heavily optimized/fortified variant)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (engineer-warrior variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated dominance, personal mega-engineering, and complete systemic abandonment.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward calculated brutality — using raw strength to break things open, precise optimization and prediction to solve complex problems, and masterful engineering to turn scrap into personal supremacy. The player designs their way out of every crisis, then leaves the rest of Concordia to rot.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Calculation + Engine triad — every challenge demands strategic foresight, raw physical power, and masterful fabrication working in perfect synergy.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries archives) → NPC 6 (mobile fabrication fortress).
- Reputation impact: These paths alienate districts reliant on central industry (especially Aries, Capricorn, Virgo) but grant powerful crafting/perk trees, unique optimized gear, and base-building dominance.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you calculate the optimal solution, tear down the old world with your hands, rebuild it better for yourself alone, and leave the inefficient masses behind.
