# Minmax Build 06: Might + Calculation + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), advanced optimization, predictive modeling, strategic planning, hacking, and pattern mastery (**Calculation**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at calculating perfect outcomes, physically tearing systems apart with brute force, and rebuilding them into hyper-efficient personal technology while rejecting all inefficient external dependencies.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward cold strategic foresight paired with raw physical dominance and masterful fabrication. The player models problems perfectly, executes with overwhelming efficiency, and turns scrap into optimized personal supremacy. Many open unique questlines involving hyper-optimized tech, strategic sabotage, personal mega-forges, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Calculation alongside Might/Engine for predictive modeling and complex engineering.
