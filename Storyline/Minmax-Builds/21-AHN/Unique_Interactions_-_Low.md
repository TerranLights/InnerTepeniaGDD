# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 21: Agility + Humanity + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **clumsy, socially awkward, panicky coward**: a character who trips constantly, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, panics and freezes under pressure, and struggles to connect with or inspire others, often becoming a pitiable liability.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, emotional tone-deafness, and easy panic. These limitations frequently cause awkward social mishaps, failed stealth or leadership attempts, and situations where the player is seen as a harmless burden. This can lead to pity-based alliances, protective behavior from others, or accidental usefulness when the player is dismissed as irrelevant.

## Design Notes
- These interactions heavily emphasize the player’s **clumsiness, social awkwardness, and easy panic** — the character is frequently a burden who trips, says the wrong thing, and freezes at critical moments.
