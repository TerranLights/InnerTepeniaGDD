# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 21: Agility + Humanity + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a swift-hearted fearless guardian: a lightning-fast, deeply empathetic leader who moves like a ghost, inspires loyalty through heart and moral courage, and endures the worst with unbreakable will while protecting a chosen few.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Phantom Courier (empathetic variant)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (swift guardian variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful high-speed leadership, heartfelt courage, and fearless protection.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward fluid high-speed action, genuine emotional connection, and courageous endurance under pressure. The player darts through danger with grace, inspires and protects those they care about, and holds unbreakable nerve through hell — then leads their small circle away from Concordia’s collapse.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Humanity + Nerve triad — blending graceful high-speed movement, genuine empathetic leadership, and courageous endurance under pressure.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (high-mobility guardian vehicle) → NPC 9 (caravan leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while rapidly alienating larger districts and central authority.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you move like lightning, protect those you choose with heart and unbreakable will, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
