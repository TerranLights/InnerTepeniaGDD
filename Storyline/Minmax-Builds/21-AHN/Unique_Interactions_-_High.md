# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 21: Agility + Humanity + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a **swift-hearted fearless guardian**: a lightning-fast, deeply empathetic leader who moves like a ghost, inspires loyalty through heart and moral courage, and endures the worst horrors with unbreakable will while protecting a chosen few.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Phantom Courier (empathetic variant)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (swift guardian variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful high-speed leadership, heartfelt courage, and fearless protection.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine fluid high-speed movement, genuine emotional connection, and courageous endurance under pressure. The player is seen as an elegant, inspiring protector who can slip through danger, stand firm when others break, and lead with both heart and unbreakable will.
