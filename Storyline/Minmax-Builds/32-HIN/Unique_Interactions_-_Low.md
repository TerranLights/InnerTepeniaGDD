# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 32: Humanity + Investigation + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **socially tone-deaf, oblivious, panicky coward**: a character who misses obvious clues, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, panics and freezes under pressure, and struggles to connect with or inspire others, often becoming a deeply pitiable liability.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s social awkwardness, investigative blindness, and easy panic. These limitations frequently cause awkward social mishaps, missed dangers, failed leadership attempts, and situations where the player is seen as a harmless, scared burden. This can lead to pity-based alliances, protective behavior from others, or accidental usefulness when the player is dismissed as irrelevant.
