# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 32: Humanity + Investigation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a fearless compassionate seeker: a deeply empathetic investigator who uncovers painful truths, inspires loyalty through heart and moral clarity, and endures the worst horrors with unbreakable will while protecting a chosen few.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Relic Hunter (empathetic variant)  
- Ghost of the Undergrid (moral variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward heartfelt truth-seeking, courageous leadership, and moral endurance.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward genuine emotional connection, relentless investigation, and fearless endurance under pressure. The player uncovers hidden darkness, inspires and protects those they care about with heart and courage, and holds unbreakable nerve through trauma — then leads their chosen circle away from Concordia’s lies and collapse.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Humanity + Investigation + Nerve triad — blending genuine empathetic leadership, relentless truth-seeking, and fearless endurance under pressure.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (guardian mobile sanctuary) → NPC 9 (caravan leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while alienating larger districts and central authorities.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover painful truths, protect those you choose with heart and courage, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
