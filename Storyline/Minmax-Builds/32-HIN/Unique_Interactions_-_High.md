# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 32: Humanity + Investigation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a **fearless compassionate seeker**: a deeply empathetic investigator who uncovers painful truths, inspires loyalty through heart and moral clarity, and endures the worst horrors with unbreakable will while protecting a chosen few.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Relic Hunter (empathetic variant)  
- Ghost of the Undergrid (moral variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward heartfelt truth-seeking, courageous leadership, and moral endurance.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine genuine emotional connection, relentless investigation, and fearless endurance under pressure. The player is seen as a strong, trustworthy protector who uncovers injustices others avoid and stands firm with both heart and unbreakable will.

## Design Notes
- These interactions position the player as a **fearless, moral truth-protector** who leads with heart, uncovers painful truths, and endures hell for those they choose.
- Many triggers can chain into larger independence or protective arcs focused on helping chosen people.
- Reputation tends to improve significantly with small independent groups and the vulnerable while worsening with corrupt authorities.
