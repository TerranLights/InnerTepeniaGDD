# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 06: Might + Calculation + Humanity

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, protection, and dominance), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, pattern recognition, and foresight), and **Humanity** (empathy, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding).  
This creates a calculated guardian-leader: a physically imposing, strategically brilliant protector who builds strong bonds with a chosen few, plans long-term survival, and ultimately rejects the collapsing systems of Concordia.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Iron Nomad (with loyal crew)  
- Beloved Exile  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward strategic leadership, protective strength, and calculated detachment from failing districts.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward the combination of physical capability, long-term strategic thinking, and genuine (but selective) care for others. The player builds, protects, and plans for a small trusted circle while rejecting the larger, inefficient, and collapsing society.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Calculation + Humanity triad — combining physical protection, strategic long-term planning, and genuine leadership/care for a chosen circle.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (mobile command base) → NPC 9 (caravan leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small groups while alienating larger districts that rely on central control or exploitation.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you protect and plan for those you choose to care about, then leave the failing systems behind.
