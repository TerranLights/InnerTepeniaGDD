# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 06: Might + Calculation + Humanity

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power and protection), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, and foresight), and **Humanity** (empathy, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding).  
This creates a **calculated guardian-leader** — a physically imposing, strategically brilliant protector who forms deep bonds with a chosen few and plans long-term survival for them.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine physical strength, long-term strategic thinking, and genuine (but selective) care for others. The player is seen as a reliable protector and planner, but also as someone who will eventually walk away from failing systems if they no longer serve their chosen circle.

## District Interactions

### Sagittarius District
1. **Frontier Scouts** show immediate respect when you physically clear dangerous terrain while calculating optimal long-term migration routes. They may offer rare scouting data or request your help planning a large-scale evacuation.
2. **Local leaders** approach you with offers to lead or advise on establishing a new independent outpost, recognizing your combination of strength and strategic foresight.
3. You can physically intimidate hostile groups while using calculated diplomacy to negotiate safe passage for refugees, earning unique reputation gains.
4. Special dialogue options appear when discussing long-term survival strategies — you can propose realistic, calculated plans that impress pragmatic NPCs.
5. You have the option to physically protect a key scout during a dangerous mission while calculating risk-reward ratios, unlocking a unique loyalty path.

### Cancer District
1. Safehouse residents instinctively look to you for leadership during crises due to your combination of physical presence and calm strategic thinking.
2. You can physically reinforce weak sections of the safehouse while calculating long-term structural improvements.
3. Special interactions allow you to mediate conflicts between residents with calculated empathy, often resulting in stronger group cohesion.
4. NPCs may confide in you about hidden vulnerabilities in the district, trusting your strategic mind and protective nature.
5. You gain unique options to organize and lead defensive training sessions that combine physical drills with tactical planning.

## Design Notes
- These interactions emphasize the player as a **strong, strategic, and selectively caring** leader.
- Many triggers can chain into larger questlines (e.g., protecting a group → leading them to independence).
- Reputation tends to improve significantly with small, independent groups while potentially worsening with large, controlling districts.
