# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 06: Might + Calculation + Humanity (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Might** (physical weakness, poor endurance, and limited protective capability), **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), and **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds).  
This creates a **frail, blunt, and socially awkward underdog**: a physically weak character who struggles with long-term planning and often says the wrong thing at the wrong time, yet can occasionally benefit from being underestimated or pitied.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s physical frailty, mental sluggishness in planning, and social clumsiness. These limitations often lead to being pitied, underestimated, or causing awkward but revealing situations. The player can sometimes turn these weaknesses into unexpected advantages through raw honesty, pity-based alliances, or being dismissed as harmless.

## Design Notes
- These interactions emphasize the player’s **physical weakness, poor planning, and social clumsiness** — they are often a burden or source of awkwardness, but this can lead to pity, being underestimated, or accidental usefulness.
- Many interactions highlight the contrast between the player’s frailty and the harsh world, creating opportunities for protective alliances or "harmless fool" advantages.
- Questlines often lean into underdog, pity-based, or accidental discovery archetypes.
