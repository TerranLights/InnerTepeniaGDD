# Minmax Build 09: Might + Nerve + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude (**Nerve**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at charging into lethal danger without flinching, ripping apart machines under extreme stress, and rebuilding them into superior death-defying gear or vehicles while rejecting every safety net.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward fearless adrenaline-fueled engineering — the player stares death in the face, physically dominates failing systems, and fabricates insane custom solutions on the fly. Many open unique questlines involving high-risk fabrication, overclocked personal tech, daredevil vehicles, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Nerve alongside Might/Engine for pushing through mortal peril and complex crafting under pressure.

## Design Notes
- These interactions emphasize synergy between raw power, fearless endurance, and hands-on mechanical genius under extreme pressure — the player is a walking adrenaline-fueled forge.
- Many can chain into larger high-stakes survival, vehicle-building, or overclock arcs that feed directly into minmax endings (Unbreakable Ronin, Iron Nomad, Eternal Engine, Phantom Courier, Architect of Collapse, etc.).
- Questlines often reward powerful risk-tolerant perks, overclocked custom gear/vehicles, fear-based reputation, or direct progress toward daredevil independence paths.
- Balance recommendation: High Nerve gates for surviving extreme consequences; Might + Engine for physical fabrication in chaos.
