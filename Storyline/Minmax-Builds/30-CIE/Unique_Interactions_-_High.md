# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 30: Calculation + Investigation + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication).  
This creates a **brilliant relic-forger genius**: a calculating, hyper-perceptive artisan who uncovers lost secrets, predicts optimal outcomes, and fabricates superior technology on the fly while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Eternal Engine (analytical variant)  
- Forge Lord (mastermind variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward information dominance, strategic fabrication, and calculated self-sufficiency.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine sharp deduction, cold strategic foresight, and masterful hands-on reconstruction. The player is seen as an untouchable genius who can uncover what others cannot, calculate perfect solutions, and immediately turn discoveries into superior personal systems.
