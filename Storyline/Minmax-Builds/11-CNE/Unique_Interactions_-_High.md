# Minmax Build 11: Calculation + Nerve + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines advanced optimization, predictive modeling, strategic planning, hacking, and pattern mastery (**Calculation**), courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude (**Nerve**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at coldly modeling chaos, pushing through lethal outcomes with unbreakable will, and turning failing systems into hyper-efficient personal technology on the fly.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward ice-cold strategic foresight paired with fearless endurance and masterful fabrication under extreme pressure. The player calculates perfect (often insane) solutions, endures the fire, and rebuilds the world better for themselves alone. Many open unique questlines involving hyper-optimized high-risk tech, strategic overclocks, calculated mega-forges, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Calculation + Nerve alongside Engine for predictive mastery and crafting in mortal peril.
