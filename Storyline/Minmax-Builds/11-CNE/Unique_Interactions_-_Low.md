# Minmax Build 11: Calculation + Nerve + Engine (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Calculation (≤3)**, **low Nerve (≤3)**, and **low Engine (≤3)**. The player is mentally slow (poor optimization, bad at logic/prediction, easily confused), mentally fragile (easily frightened, low stress tolerance, quick to panic or give up), and has extremely poor endurance — tires out almost immediately from any effort.

This creates a **"slow-thinking, fearful, and constantly exhausted"** underdog archetype. The character struggles to understand complex situations, panics under pressure, and burns out so quickly that they become a major burden. They are frequently pitied, protected, dismissed as useless, or overlooked. Their limitations can open unique paths through sympathy, being underestimated, accidental "simple-minded" insights, or reliance on other stats (especially Might, Agility, Humanity, or Investigation).

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize mental sluggishness, emotional/mental fragility, and rapid burnout. They should feel limiting and frustrating, but also create opportunities via pity, protective alliances, simplistic but sometimes profound honesty, or accidental discoveries while panicking or resting. Many can lead to underdog questlines, "protected simpleton" dynamics, or unconventional routes toward minmax endings.

## District-Specific Special/Unique Interactions (Minimum 5 per District)

- Panics and tires out instantly during blackouts or emergencies, freezing or hiding.
- Gets heavily pitied as "the poor slow, fearful one," receiving extra care or protected access.
- Rapid burnout leads to being carried or shielded, exposing private conversations or hidden areas.
- Mentally sluggish reactions to trauma create awkward but sometimes emotionally raw moments.

## Design Notes
- These interactions highlight the character's **mental sluggishness, emotional fragility, and extremely poor stamina** — they think slowly, panic easily, tire out almost immediately, and are often a burden, strongly inviting pity and being underestimated.
- Many interactions can combine with high stats in Might, Agility, Humanity, or Investigation for strong contrast (e.g., a physically strong but slow, fearful, and quickly exhausted character).
- Questlines often lean into pity-based alliances, "slow and fearful burden," or accidental simple insights.
