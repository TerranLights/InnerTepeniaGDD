# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 18: Agility + Calculation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a lightning-fast strategic daredevil: a ghost-like operative who calculates perfect high-risk outcomes, executes them with unmatched speed and precision, and holds unbreakable nerve through chaos while rejecting all external dependencies.

**Target Endings**:  
- Phantom Courier  
- Unbreakable Ronin  
- Architect of Collapse (scout variant)  
- Iron Nomad (high-mobility variant)  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated high-speed execution, fearless precision, and strategic detachment.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward fluid high-speed action, cold strategic foresight, and fearless execution under lethal pressure. The player moves like lightning, calculates optimal (often insane) solutions, holds their nerve through cascading disasters, and vanishes before the consequences catch up — leaving Concordia to its fate.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Calculation + Nerve triad — blending graceful high-speed movement, strategic foresight, and unbreakable will under extreme pressure.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (high-speed reconnaissance rig) → NPC 9 (scouting group leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant unmatched mobility and tactical precision while alienating districts reliant on slow planning or brute force.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you move like lightning, calculate the perfect outcome, endure the fire with unbreakable nerve, and vanish before the city can drag you down.
