# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 18: Agility + Calculation + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **clumsy, panicky, terrible-planner**: a character who trips over everything, makes awful strategic decisions, freezes or panics under pressure, and often becomes a pitiable liability or accidental distraction.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, poor planning, and easy panic. These limitations frequently cause mishaps, failed stealth attempts, bad decisions, and situations where the player is seen as a burden or harmless fool. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others compensate for the player’s obvious incompetence and fear.

## Design Notes
- These interactions heavily emphasize the player’s **clumsiness, poor planning, and easy panic** — the character is frequently a liability who trips, misses everything, freezes, and makes bad decisions.
