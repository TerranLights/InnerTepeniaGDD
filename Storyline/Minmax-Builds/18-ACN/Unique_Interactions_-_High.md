# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 18: Agility + Calculation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a **lightning-fast strategic daredevil**: a ghost-like operative who moves with perfect grace, calculates flawless high-risk outcomes, and executes them with unbreakable nerve under extreme pressure while rejecting all external dependencies.

**Target Endings**:  
- Phantom Courier  
- Unbreakable Ronin  
- Architect of Collapse (scout variant)  
- Iron Nomad (high-mobility variant)  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated high-speed execution and fearless precision.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine fluid high-speed action, cold strategic foresight, and fearless execution under lethal pressure. The player is seen as an untouchable, highly competent shadow who calculates the perfect move and strikes before anyone can react.
