# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 23: Agility + Investigation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a **fearless shadow-scout**: a lightning-fast, hyper-perceptive operative who moves like a ghost, uncovers hidden truths in the deadliest places, and holds unbreakable nerve through mortal danger while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Phantom Courier  
- Unbreakable Ronin (scout variant)  
- Architect of Collapse (infiltration variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward high-speed investigation, fearless exploration, and silent precision.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine fluid high-speed movement, relentless investigation, and fearless execution under extreme pressure. The player is seen as an untouchable phantom who uncovers what others fear to touch and strikes before anyone can react.
