# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 23: Agility + Investigation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a fearless shadow-scout: a lightning-fast, hyper-perceptive operative who moves like a ghost, uncovers hidden truths in the deadliest places, and holds unbreakable nerve through mortal danger while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Phantom Courier  
- Unbreakable Ronin (scout variant)  
- Architect of Collapse (infiltration variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward high-speed investigation, fearless exploration, and silent precision.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward fluid high-speed movement, sharp deduction, and courageous execution under extreme pressure. The player darts through danger unseen, pieces together secrets others miss, and pushes through mortal peril with raw nerve — then vanishes with their discoveries, leaving Concordia to its ignorance.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Investigation + Nerve triad — blending graceful high-speed movement, relentless truth-seeking, and unbreakable will under mortal danger.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (mobile reconnaissance lab) → NPC 9 (scouting expedition leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant unmatched mobility, information dominance, and tactical precision while alienating districts that prefer secrets stay buried.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you move unseen, uncover the truth, brave the deadliest places, and vanish before the city can react.
