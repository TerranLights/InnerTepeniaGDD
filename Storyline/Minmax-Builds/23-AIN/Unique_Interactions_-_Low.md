# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 23: Agility + Investigation + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **clumsy, oblivious, panicky weakling**: a character who trips constantly, misses obvious clues, panics and freezes under pressure, and fails to connect basic information, often becoming a pitiable liability or accidental distraction.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, investigative blindness, and easy panic. These limitations frequently cause mishaps, missed dangers, failed stealth attempts, and situations where the player is seen as a harmless burden. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others compensate for the player’s obvious incompetence and fear.
