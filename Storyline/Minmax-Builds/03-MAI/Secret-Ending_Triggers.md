# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 03: Might + Agility + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Agility** (speed, mobility, precision, stealth), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, secret uncovering, forensics, and pattern recognition).  
This creates a relentless lone explorer and relic hunter: an unstoppable force who can break into anywhere, move like a ghost, and piece together hidden truths while completely abandoning the collapsing districts and their problems.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Phantom Courier  
- Eternal Seeker  
- Unbreakable Ronin (scout/infiltrator variant)  
- Iron Nomad (explorer variant)  
- Architect of Collapse (knowledge/secrets variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward relentless discovery, infiltration, and personal knowledge hoarding over helping society.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward sharp-eyed exploration, stealthy or brute-force entry, and deductive reasoning. The player uncovers lost knowledge, hidden routes, and powerful relics through superior physical capability and investigative skill, then chooses to keep the secrets for themselves and walk away — leaving Concordia’s problems behind.

## World / Environmental Triggers (7)

1. **Abandoned High-Speed Maintenance Rail (Virgo Undergrid)**  
   Collapsed rail system. Player uses Agility + Might to navigate debris and Investigation to decode old route markers, revealing hidden exit vectors and long-lost supply caches.  
   **Strongly supports**: Phantom Courier, Ghost of the Undergrid, Iron Nomad.

2. **Glitching Pre-War Military Exosuit Prototype (Aries restricted ruins)**  
   Interactive wreckage. Requires Might to access sealed sections, Agility to traverse unstable areas, and Investigation to piece together activation logs and hidden upgrade data.  
   **Strongly supports**: Relic Hunter, Unbreakable Ronin, Eternal Seeker.

3. **Encrypted Overland Route Data Cache (Sagittarius frostline marker)**  
   Weather-beaten terminal. High Investigation to decrypt layered logs; Might and Agility to reach and defend the site, unlocking solo or small-team migration vectors.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Relic Hunter, Phantom Courier.

4. **Collapsed but Hackable Aries Micro-Reactor (hidden backup node)**  
   Environmental puzzle: physically stabilize failing systems (Might + Agility) while investigating old maintenance logs (Investigation) to extract portable power blueprints.  
   **Strongly supports**: Architect of Collapse, Relic Hunter, Iron Nomad.

5. **Ancient Mobility Rig Testing Ground (outer Capricorn scrap fields)**  
   Ruined proving grounds. Completing obstacle courses with Might and Agility while investigating scattered data pads unlocks blueprints for advanced solo exploration frames.  
   **Strongly supports**: Unbreakable Ronin, Relic Hunter, Phantom Courier.

6. **Hidden Server Vault with Predictive Survival Algorithms**  
   Sealed Gemini ruin. Agility to bypass security, Might to force entry, and Investigation to reconstruct and adapt ancient lone-survivor knowledge databases.  
   **Strongly supports**: Eternal Seeker, Architect of Collapse, Ghost of the Undergrid.

7. **Derelict Armored Nomad Convoy Wreck (Frostlands edge)**  
   Multi-stage environmental site. Requires Might to clear wreckage, Agility to explore compartments, and Investigation to decode logs and salvage advanced navigation/relic systems.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Relic Hunter, Phantom Courier.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Agility + Investigation triad — every challenge combines physical prowess, graceful movement, and sharp deductive work.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route cache) → NPC 1 (Scout quest) → World Trigger 7 (nomad convoy).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant massive personal knowledge and relic advantages while quickly alienating districts that prefer secrets stay buried.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover the truth, claim what you need, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
