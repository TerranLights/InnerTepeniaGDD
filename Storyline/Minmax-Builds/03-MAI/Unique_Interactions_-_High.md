# Minmax Build 03: Might + Agility + Investigation - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), graceful speed and precision movement (**Agility**), and keen perception, deduction, clue-tracking, secret uncovering, forensics, and pattern recognition (**Investigation**). The player excels at breaking into forbidden areas, piecing together hidden truths, moving like a ghost, and physically dominating obstacles to claim relics, knowledge, or advantages while pursuing extreme independence.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward sharp-eyed deduction paired with agile infiltration and brute-force access. The player uncovers what others miss, physically reaches the unreachable, and uses gathered intel for personal gain or hidden paths. Many open unique questlines involving relic hunts, lost archives, conspiracy exposure, or direct routes toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Investigation alongside Might/Agility for deep exploration and physical execution.

## Design Notes

- Balance recommendation: High Investigation gates for deep clue chains; Might + Agility for reaching and claiming the secrets.
