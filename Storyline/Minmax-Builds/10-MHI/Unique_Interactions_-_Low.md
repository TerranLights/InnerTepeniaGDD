# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 10: Might + Humanity + Investigation (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Might** (physical weakness, poor endurance, and limited protective capability), **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), and **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details).  
This creates a **frail, socially awkward, oblivious weakling**: a physically weak character who misses obvious clues, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, and struggles to connect with others, often appearing pitiable or harmless.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s physical frailty, social clumsiness, and investigative blindness. These limitations frequently cause awkward moments, missed opportunities, and being underestimated or pitied. This can lead to pity-based alliances, accidental discoveries, or situations where others compensate for the player’s obvious weaknesses.
