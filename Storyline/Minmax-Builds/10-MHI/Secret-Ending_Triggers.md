# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 10: Might + Humanity + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a compassionate truth-seeker protector: a physically imposing investigator who uncovers hidden truths, deeply cares for a chosen few, and uses both moral leadership and raw strength to shield them while rejecting the lies and failures of Concordia.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Ghost of the Undergrid (empathetic variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward truth-seeking, protective leadership, and moral investigation.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward the combination of physical strength, genuine emotional connection, and relentless investigation. The player uncovers secrets others miss, protects those they choose to care about with both heart and force, and ultimately walks away from the city’s deceptions and collapsing systems.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Humanity + Investigation triad — blending physical protection, genuine emotional leadership, and relentless truth-seeking.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 7 (nomad convoy) → NPC 9 (caravan protector) → NPC 6 (guardian mobile sanctuary).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while alienating larger districts and central authorities.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover the truth, protect those you choose with heart and strength, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
