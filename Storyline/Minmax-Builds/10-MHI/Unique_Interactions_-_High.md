# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 10: Might + Humanity + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a **compassionate truth-enforcer**: a physically imposing investigator who uncovers hidden truths, protects the vulnerable with both heart and strength, and uses moral leadership combined with raw power while rejecting Concordia’s lies and failures.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Relic Hunter (empathetic variant)  
- Ghost of the Undergrid (protector variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward heartfelt truth-seeking, protective leadership, and moral investigation.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine overwhelming physical presence, genuine empathy, and relentless investigation. The player is seen as a strong, trustworthy protector who uncovers injustices and physically shields those they choose to care about.
