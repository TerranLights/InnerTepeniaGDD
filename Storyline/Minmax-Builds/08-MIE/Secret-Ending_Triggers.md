# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 08: Might + Investigation + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, secret uncovering, forensics, and pattern recognition), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication).  
This creates a relentless relic-forger and scavenger-savant: an unstoppable force who tears open sealed ruins, deciphers forgotten history and tech, rebuilds lost wonders into superior personal gear, and achieves total self-sufficiency while abandoning the collapsing districts.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Iron Nomad (heavily customized explorer variant)  
- Eternal Engine  
- Architect of Collapse (reconstructionist variant)  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Forge Lord (lone scavenger variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward deep scavenging, technical reconstruction, and personal knowledge/gear hoarding.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward brutal entry combined with sharp deduction and masterful fabrication. The player smashes through barriers, pieces together lost secrets, and turns ancient scrap into cutting-edge personal technology — then walks away with their hoard, leaving Concordia’s problems and people behind.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Investigation + Engine triad — every challenge combines raw physical power, sharp deductive work, and hands-on technical reconstruction.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries archives) → NPC 6 (mobile fabrication lab).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant massive personal relic advantages and crafting depth while quickly alienating districts that want lost tech kept buried or regulated.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover the past, rip it out with your hands, rebuild it better for yourself alone, and leave the ignorant present to crumble.
