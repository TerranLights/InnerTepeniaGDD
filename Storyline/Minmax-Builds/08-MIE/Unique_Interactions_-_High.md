# Minmax Build 08: Might + Investigation + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), perception, deduction, clue-tracking, secret uncovering, forensics, and pattern recognition (**Investigation**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at investigating hidden truths, physically breaking into unreachable places, and reconstructing lost or broken tech into superior personal systems while pursuing extreme independence.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward sharp deduction paired with brute-force access and hands-on reconstruction. The player uncovers what others cannot, physically claims it, and rebuilds it better for themselves. Many open unique questlines involving relic hunting, lost archive reconstruction, hidden infrastructure exploits, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Investigation alongside Might/Engine for deep clue chains and technical resurrection.

## Design Notes
- These interactions emphasize synergy between deduction, physical dominance, and technical reconstruction — the player is a relentless relic-forger and scavenger-savant.
- Many can chain into larger relic-hunting or lost-tech resurrection arcs that feed directly into minmax endings (Relic Hunter, Ghost of the Undergrid, Architect of Collapse, Iron Nomad, Eternal Engine, etc.).
- Questlines often reward unique knowledge perks, powerful restored relics, hidden workshops, or direct progress toward explorer-style independence paths.
- Balance recommendation: High Investigation gates for deep clue and pattern chains; Might + Engine for physical access and reconstruction scale.
