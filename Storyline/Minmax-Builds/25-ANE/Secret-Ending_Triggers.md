# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 10: Agility + Nerve + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, mobility, precision, stealth), **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication).  
This creates a lightning-fast daredevil speed-forged: an untouchable blur of motion who performs death-defying maneuvers, rebuilds and overclocks vehicles/machines on the fly under extreme stress, and outruns every problem while rejecting all central infrastructure and obligations.

**Target Endings**:  
- Phantom Courier  
- Iron Nomad (high-mobility variant)  
- Eternal Engine (speed-core variant)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (agile daredevil variant)  
- Architect of Collapse (hit-and-run fabrication variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward extreme mobility, fearless risk-taking, and personal high-speed engineering dominance.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward graceful high-speed action, balls-to-the-wall courage, and masterful on-the-move engineering. The player dodges danger with precision, pushes machines (and themselves) to the limit, builds custom speed machines in the heat of chaos, then vanishes — leaving Concordia’s collapsing systems in the dust.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Nerve + Engine triad — every challenge combines graceful high-speed movement, fearless risk-taking, and hands-on mechanical genius performed in motion.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal speed reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries schematics) → NPC 6 (lightning nomad rig).
- Reputation impact: These paths destroy district relations extremely quickly but grant powerful mobility perks, unique overclocked vehicles, and unmatched escape/evasion capabilities.
- Tone: Every trigger screams "Not My Fucking Problem" — you blur past danger at impossible speeds, rebuild machines while laughing in the face of death, take what you need, and leave the slow and the stuck behind in the dust.
