# Minmax Build 10: Agility + Nerve + Engine (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Agility (≤3)**, **low Nerve (≤3)**, and **low Engine (≤3)**. The player is clumsy and uncoordinated, mentally fragile (easily frightened, low stress tolerance, quick to panic or give up), and has extremely poor endurance — tires out almost immediately from any effort.

This creates a **"clumsy, fearful, and constantly exhausted"** underdog archetype. The character moves awkwardly, panics under pressure, and burns out so quickly that they become a major liability in almost any demanding situation. They are frequently pitied, protected, dismissed as useless, or used as bait. Their obvious vulnerability can open unique paths through sympathy, being overlooked as non-threatening, hiding in fear, or accidental outcomes while panicking or resting.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize clumsiness, mental fragility, and rapid burnout. They should feel limiting, stressful, and exhausting, but also create opportunities via pity, protective alliances, "coward's luck," or accidental discoveries while fleeing or hiding. Many can lead to underdog questlines, "protected burden" dynamics, or unconventional routes toward minmax endings.

## District-Specific Special/Unique Interactions (Minimum 5 per District)

### 1. Cancer (Nurturing/Protective Sanctuary)
- Clumsy and weak movement causes frequent trips and drops while trying to help; panics and freezes during blackouts.
- Rapid exhaustion forces constant rests, leading to being carried or shielded by staff.
- Gets heavily pitied as "the poor clumsy, fearful one," receiving extra protection or restricted access.
- Fearful panic during crises causes chaotic but revealing accidents (knocking over equipment, revealing hidden areas).
- Overwhelmed by emotional stress, breaking down in ways that trigger protective or confessional responses.

### 2. Taurus (Residential/Stability)
- Clumsy navigation in tight residential spaces leads to constant bumping and minor damage.
- Panics and tires out instantly during disputes or raids, hiding or fleeing.
- Gets treated as a helpless, fearful burden, earning strong protective behavior from families.
- Rapid burnout during neighborhood tasks leads to resting in private homes and overhearing secrets.
- Fearful clumsiness accidentally alerts neighbors or reveals hidden storage.

### 3. Leo (Cultural/Performative)
- Too clumsy for stage work and panics under crowds or failing spectacles.
- Rapid exhaustion forces hiding in backstage areas, accidentally discovering secrets.
- Gets pitied or used as comic relief, granting protected backstage access.
- Fearful reactions disrupt performances in chaotic but revealing ways.
- Overwhelmed by loud chaos, leading to hiding in restricted VIP zones.

### 4. Scorpio (Psychological/Transformative)
- Clumsy and fearful during intense rituals; burns out and panics quickly.
- Gets pitied as too fragile for real shadow-work, allowing observational access.
- Fear-driven clumsiness accidentally triggers safety systems or hidden mechanisms.
- Rapid exhaustion leads to emotional vulnerability and unexpected bonds through pity.
- Overwhelmed by taboo environments, causing the player to hide in forbidden chambers.

### 5. Aries (Power Core / Raw Survival)
- Clumsy movement in dangerous tunnels and instant exhaustion near heat/radiation.
- Panics near failing reactors, freezing or fleeing and creating opportunities for others to intervene.
- Gets heavily protected or dismissed by engineers, allowing passive observation.
- Fearful clumsiness accidentally triggers safety systems or exposes hidden nodes.
- Rapid burnout forces being carried through restricted areas.

### 6. Capricorn (Industrial/Ambition)
- Tires out immediately during labor and panics around heavy machinery.
- Clumsy fear responses cause frequent mistakes that reveal hidden operations.
- Gets pitied and assigned "safe" tasks, leading to observational discoveries.
- Rapid burnout in forges forces protective intervention from workers.
- Overwhelmed by noise and danger, leading to hiding in storage zones.

### 7. Aquarius (Visionary/Experimental)
- Clumsy handling of equipment and quick panic during failures or leaks.
- Gets pitied as too frail and fearful, granting safe observational access.
- Fear-driven clumsiness accidentally triggers prototypes or reveals data.
- Rapid exhaustion leads to hiding in restricted or quarantine areas.
- Overwhelmed by stress, creating moments of vulnerability with researchers.

### 8. Libra (Diplomatic/Balance)
- Tires quickly in sessions and panics under political pressure.
- Clumsy, fearful reactions disrupt formal settings in revealing ways.
- Gets dismissed as frail and useless, allowing passive observation of backroom deals.
- Rapid burnout leads to hiding in secure spires.
- Overwhelmed by tension, causing accidental eavesdropping.

### 9. Gemini (Data/Communication Nexus)
- Clumsy navigation of holographic environments and rapid exhaustion.
- Panics during system failures or pursuits, freezing or fleeing.
- Gets treated as harmless and fearful, allowing access to private nodes.
- Clumsy panic triggers glitches or exposes hidden data.
- Rapid burnout creates hiding opportunities in quiet terminals.

### 10. Pisces (Black Market/Underworld)
- Clumsy and fearful in dangerous dealings; tires out during raids.
- Gets pitied or dismissed as useless, allowing access to hidden operations.
- Fearful clumsiness disrupts deals in chaotic but revealing ways.

### 11. Sagittarius (Frostlands/Frontier)

- Gets heavily pitied by settlers, who offer strong protection.

- Rapid burnout forces rest in hidden outposts.
- Overwhelmed by conditions, leading to hiding in abandoned structures.

### 12. Virgo (Undergrid/Maintenance)

## Design Notes
- These interactions highlight the character's **clumsiness, mental fragility, and extremely poor stamina** — they panic easily, move awkwardly, tire out almost immediately, and are often a major liability, strongly inviting pity and protection.
- Many interactions can combine with high stats in Calculation, Investigation, Humanity, or Might for strong contrast (e.g., a brilliant mind trapped in a clumsy, fearful, quickly exhausted body).
- Questlines often lean heavily into pity-based alliances, "protected clumsy coward," or accidental discovery while hiding/resting from fear and fatigue.
