# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 20: Agility + Humanity + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a graceful empathetic seeker: a lightning-fast, deeply compassionate investigator who moves like a ghost, uncovers hidden truths, and inspires loyalty in a chosen few while rejecting Concordia’s lies and failures.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Relic Hunter (empathetic variant)  
- Ghost of the Undergrid (charismatic variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful truth-seeking, heartfelt leadership, and moral investigation.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward fluid high-speed movement, genuine emotional connection, and relentless investigation. The player darts through danger unseen, uncovers secrets others miss, and builds real bonds with those they choose to protect — then leads their small circle away from the city’s deceptions and collapse.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Humanity + Investigation triad — blending graceful high-speed movement, genuine empathetic leadership, and relentless truth-seeking.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (high-mobility sanctuary vehicle) → NPC 9 (caravan leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while alienating larger districts and central authorities.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you move unseen, uncover the truth, protect those you choose with heart and grace, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
