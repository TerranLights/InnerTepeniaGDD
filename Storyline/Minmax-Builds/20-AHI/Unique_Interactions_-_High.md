# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 20: Agility + Humanity + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a **graceful empathetic seeker**: a lightning-fast, deeply compassionate investigator who moves like a ghost, uncovers hidden truths, inspires loyalty through heart and moral clarity, and protects a chosen few while rejecting Concordia’s lies and failures.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Relic Hunter (empathetic variant)  
- Ghost of the Undergrid (charismatic variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful truth-seeking, heartfelt leadership, and moral investigation.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine fluid high-speed movement, genuine emotional connection, and relentless investigation. The player is seen as an elegant, trustworthy protector who can slip through danger, uncover injustices, and inspire others to follow with both heart and grace.
