# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 20: Agility + Humanity + Investigation (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), and **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details).  
This creates a **clumsy, socially awkward, oblivious investigator**: a character who trips constantly, misses obvious clues, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, and struggles to read people or situations, often becoming a pitiable burden or accidental distraction.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, social tone-deafness, and investigative blindness. These limitations frequently cause awkward mishaps, missed opportunities, failed stealth attempts, and situations where the player is underestimated or pitied. This can lead to pity-based alliances, accidental discoveries when others compensate, or moments where the player’s incompetence inadvertently reveals information.
