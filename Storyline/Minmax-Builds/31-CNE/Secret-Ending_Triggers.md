# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 11: Calculation + Nerve + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (optimization, predictive modeling, strategic planning, hacking, resource efficiency, and pattern mastery), **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication).  
This creates a ice-cold strategic overclocker: a calculating risk-taker who perfectly models chaos, endures the worst outcomes, and rebuilds machines into superior optimized systems under lethal pressure while completely rejecting all external dependencies and societal burdens.

**Target Endings**:  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Eternal Engine  
- Forge Lord (strategic variant)  
- Iron Nomad (optimized fortress variant)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (calculated risk-master variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward perfect foresight, unbreakable will under fire, and personal mega-engineering dominance.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward cold strategic prediction, willingness to push limits, and masterful fabrication. The player calculates optimal (often insane) solutions, holds their nerve through cascading disasters, and turns scrap into hyper-efficient personal technology — then executes the perfect exit, leaving Concordia to its inefficient fate.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Calculation + Nerve + Engine triad — every challenge demands perfect strategic foresight, unbreakable willpower under pressure, and masterful on-the-spot fabrication.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries archives) → NPC 6 (strategic mobile forge).
- Reputation impact: These paths alienate districts reliant on central planning and industry (especially Aries, Gemini, Capricorn) but grant powerful optimization perks, unique calculated gear, and self-sufficient base-building dominance.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you calculate the perfect outcome, endure the fire, rebuild the world better for yourself alone, and leave the inefficient and emotional masses to collapse.
