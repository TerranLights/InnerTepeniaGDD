# Minmax Build 05: Might + Agility + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), graceful speed and precision movement (**Agility**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at ripping apart failing machines with bare hands, rebuilding them better on the fly, moving fluidly through danger while carrying or modifying heavy tech, and achieving total personal self-sufficiency.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward brutal physical intervention paired with graceful mobility and hands-on engineering genius. The player tears down problems, rebuilds them into personal advantages, and walks away with custom gear or vehicles. Many open unique questlines involving personal fabrication, vehicle construction, decentralized tech, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Engine alongside Might/Agility for complex on-the-spot crafting and physical execution.
