# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 05: Might + Agility + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Agility** (speed, mobility, precision, stealth), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle building, power systems, and hands-on fabrication).  
This creates a one-robot wrecking crew and mobile forge: an unstoppable tinkerer who can rip apart machines with bare hands, rebuild them better on the fly, and maintain perfect personal mobility while rejecting all central infrastructure.

**Target Endings**:  
- Iron Nomad  
- Eternal Engine  
- Architect of Collapse (tech-fabrication variant)  
- Forge Lord (lone variant)  
- Phantom Courier (custom vehicle focus)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (heavily customized frame)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward personal engineering dominance and total self-sufficiency.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward brutal physical intervention paired with graceful movement and masterful on-the-spot engineering. The player tears down failing systems, rebuilds them into personal advantages, and then abandons the city — taking their custom-built freedom with them.

## World / Environmental Triggers (7)

1. **Abandoned High-Speed Maintenance Rail (Virgo Undergrid)**  
   Collapsed rail system. Player uses Might to clear massive debris, Agility to access elevated sections, and Engine to repair and repurpose rail components into a personal high-mobility transport.  
   **Strongly supports**: Phantom Courier, Iron Nomad, Eternal Engine.

2. **Glitching Pre-War Military Exosuit Prototype (Aries restricted ruins)**  
   Dangerous wreckage. Requires Might to pry open, Agility to navigate failing systems, and Engine to fully restore and customize it into a personal power-armor frame.

3. **Encrypted Overland Route Data Cache (Sagittarius frostline marker)**

4. **Collapsed but Hackable Aries Micro-Reactor (hidden backup node)**  
   Environmental puzzle: physically stabilize the failing core (Might + Agility) while using Engine mastery to convert it into a compact, portable personal power plant.  
   **Strongly supports**: Eternal Engine, Architect of Collapse, Iron Nomad.

5. **Ancient Mobility Rig Testing Ground (outer Capricorn scrap fields)**  
   Ruined proving grounds filled with scrap. Completing challenges with Might and Agility while using Engine to repair and integrate test components unlocks blueprints for elite custom vehicles.

6. **Hidden Server Vault with Predictive Survival Algorithms**  
   Sealed Gemini ruin with failing environmental controls. Agility to bypass hazards, Might to force entry, and Engine to repair and adapt the vault’s systems into portable survival tech.  
   **Strongly supports**: Eternal Engine, Architect of Collapse.

7. **Derelict Armored Nomad Convoy Wreck (Frostlands edge)**  
   Multi-stage wreckage site. Requires Might to shift heavy armor plating, Agility to reach buried compartments, and Engine to salvage and fully restore a functional mobile base.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Eternal Engine, Phantom Courier.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Agility + Engine triad — every challenge combines raw physical power, graceful movement, and hands-on mechanical genius.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries schematics) → NPC 6 (mobile command forge).
- Reputation impact: These paths rapidly destroy relations with districts (especially Aries and Capricorn) but grant powerful crafting perks, unique custom gear, and self-sufficient base-building options.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you rip apart the old world, rebuild it better for yourself, and leave everyone else to deal with the wreckage.
