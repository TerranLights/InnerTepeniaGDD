# Minmax Build 05: Might + Agility + Engine (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Might (≤3)**, **low Agility (≤3)**, and **low Engine (≤3)**. The player is physically weak, clumsy and uncoordinated, and has extremely poor endurance — tires out almost immediately, operates inefficiently, and burns out quickly from any sustained effort.

This creates a "frail, clumsy, and constantly exhausted" underdog archetype. The character struggles with almost any physical task, moves awkwardly, and fatigues so rapidly that they become a burden in demanding situations. They are frequently pitied, protected, or dismissed as useless, but this can open unique paths through sympathy, being overlooked, or forcing creative/non-physical solutions.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize physical weakness, clumsiness, and rapid burnout. They should feel limiting and exhausting for the player, but also create opportunities via pity, protective alliances, accidental discoveries from fumbling, or reliance on other stats (especially Calculation, Humanity, Investigation, or Nerve). Many can lead to underdog questlines, "burden" dynamics, or unconventional routes toward minmax endings.

## District-Specific Special/Unique Interactions (Minimum 5 per District)

### 1. Cancer (Nurturing/Protective Sanctuary)
- Tires out almost instantly when trying to help carry patients or supplies, forcing frequent breaks or abandonment of tasks.
- Clumsy and weak movements cause frequent drops or minor accidents with medical equipment.
- Gets heavily pitied by caregivers, who assign "safe, low-effort" tasks or offer extra assistance.
- Rapid burnout during emergencies leads to being carried or protected by others, revealing hidden areas or conversations.
- Struggles with sustained focus or movement in large care facilities, missing details but gaining sympathy-based access.

### 2. Taurus (Residential/Stability)
- Too weak and clumsy to assist with lifting, repairs, or moving belongings; exhausts quickly during any manual labor.
- Frequent fatigue forces the player to sit out neighborhood work, leading to observational or gossip-heavy interactions.
- Clumsy exhaustion-related accidents damage property or alert neighbors.
- Gets treated as a frail burden, earning protective behavior or pity-based alliances from residents.
- Rapid burnout during resource disputes or patrols creates opportunities for others to step in and share secrets.

### 3. Leo (Cultural/Performative)

## Design Notes
- These interactions highlight the character's **physical weakness, clumsiness, and extremely poor stamina** — they tire out almost immediately and become a burden, inviting pity, protection, and being underestimated.
- Many interactions can combine with high stats in Calculation, Humanity, Investigation, or Nerve for strong contrast (e.g., a brilliant mind trapped in a frail, clumsy, quickly exhausted body).
- Questlines often lean into pity-based alliances, "burden" dynamics, or accidental discovery through exhaustion-related hiding/resting.
