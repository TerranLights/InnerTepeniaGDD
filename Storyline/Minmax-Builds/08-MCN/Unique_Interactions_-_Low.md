# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 08: Might + Calculation + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Might** (physical weakness, poor endurance, and limited protective capability), **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **frail, panicky coward**: a physically weak character who makes terrible plans, freezes or panics under pressure, and often becomes a liability that others must protect or work around.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s physical frailty, poor planning, and tendency to panic or freeze. These limitations frequently cause the player to become a burden, make poor decisions under stress, or appear completely harmless. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others compensate for the player’s obvious weakness and fear.
