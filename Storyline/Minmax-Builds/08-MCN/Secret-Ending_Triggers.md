# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 08: Might + Calculation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates an ice-cold strategic enforcer: a physically overwhelming force who calculates perfect outcomes under extreme pressure, holds unbreakable nerve through chaos, and physically executes their will while rejecting all external dependencies.

**Target Endings**:  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Unbreakable Ronin  
- Iron Nomad (strategic variant)  
- Eternal Engine (calculated variant)  
- Forge Lord (enforcer variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated dominance, unbreakable will under fire, and strategic physical enforcement.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward cold strategic foresight, fearless execution under lethal pressure, and raw physical dominance. The player calculates optimal (often brutal) solutions, holds their nerve through cascading disasters, and physically enforces their independence — then leaves Concordia to its inefficient fate.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Calculation + Nerve triad — combining physical dominance, strategic foresight, and unbreakable will under extreme pressure.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries archives) → NPC 6 (strategic mobile rig).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant massive tactical and combat advantages while alienating districts reliant on central planning or emotional appeals.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you calculate the perfect outcome, endure the fire with unbreakable will, physically enforce your independence, and leave the rest behind.
