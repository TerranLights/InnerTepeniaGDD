# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 08: Might + Calculation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates an **unbreakable strategic enforcer**: a physically overwhelming force who calculates perfect outcomes under extreme pressure and executes them with ice-cold nerve while rejecting all external dependencies.

**Target Endings**:  
- Unbreakable Ronin  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Iron Nomad (strategic enforcer variant)  
- Eternal Engine (calculated variant)  
- Forge Lord (enforcer variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated dominance, unbreakable will under fire, and strategic physical enforcement.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine overwhelming physical presence, cold strategic calculation, and fearless execution under lethal pressure. The player is seen as an unstoppable, highly competent operator who calculates the perfect move and physically enforces it no matter the cost.
