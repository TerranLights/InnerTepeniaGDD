# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 07: Might + Humanity + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, bonding, moral intuition, leadership, and selective care), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication).
This creates a powerful guardian-forge master: an incredibly strong and empathetic builder who forges protective gear, mobile sanctuaries, and life-saving tech for a chosen few while completely rejecting the failing central systems and their endless demands.

**Target Endings**:
- Iron Nomad (with loyal small crew)
- Last Shepherd
- Wandering Guardian
- Eternal Engine (protective variant)
- Beloved Exile
- Forge Lord (guardian variant)
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward personal fabrication, heartfelt loyalty to a small circle, and abandonment of the wider collapsing society.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward heroic physical intervention paired with genuine emotional connection and masterful hands-on engineering. The player builds real solutions for people they care about, uses raw strength and technical genius to protect them, then walks away from the rest of Concordia with their chosen few — telling the larger system "Not My Fucking Problem."

## World / Environmental Triggers (7)

1. **Abandoned High-Speed Maintenance Rail (Virgo Undergrid)**
   Collapsed rail system. Player uses Might to clear wreckage, Humanity-aligned choices (e.g., aiding trapped survivors), and Engine to repurpose components into a reinforced small-group transport sanctuary.
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Wandering Guardian, Last Shepherd.

2. **Glitching Pre-War Military Exosuit Prototype (Aries restricted ruins)**
   Interactive wreckage. Requires Might to access, Humanity decisions (e.g., choosing to save embedded data logs of past guardians), and Engine to restore it into protective guardian-class armor.
   **Strongly supports**: Unbreakable Ronin, Wandering Guardian, Beloved Exile.

3. **Encrypted Overland Route Data Cache (Sagittarius frostline marker)**
   Weather-beaten terminal. Might and Engine to repair the system; Humanity checks on personal survivor messages unlock safe small-group migration vectors and habitat schematics.
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Last Shepherd, Wandering Guardian.

4. **Collapsed but Hackable Aries Micro-Reactor (hidden backup node)**
   Environmental puzzle: physically stabilize (Might) while making moral choices about power distribution to nearby survivors and engineering a compact portable core suited for a loyal group.
   **Strongly supports**: Eternal Engine, Iron Nomad, Last Shepherd.

5. **Ancient Mobility Rig Testing Ground (outer Capricorn scrap fields)**
   Ruined proving grounds. Completing strength trials (Might) while choosing to help stranded test AIs/NPCs (Humanity) and integrating components (Engine) unlocks blueprints for crew-capable guardian vehicles.
   **Strongly supports**: Forge Lord, Iron Nomad, Wandering Guardian.

6. **Hidden Server Vault with Predictive Survival Algorithms**
   Sealed Gemini ruin. Might to force entry, Humanity to interpret ethical "protect your chosen" subroutines, and Engine to adapt the systems into portable life-support tech.
   **Strongly supports**: Eternal Engine, Beloved Exile, Last Shepherd.

7. **Derelict Armored Nomad Convoy Wreck (Frostlands edge)**
   Multi-stage site. Requires Might to clear heavy plating, Humanity interactions with survivor logs, and Engine to salvage and fully restore a functional small-crew mobile sanctuary.
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Wandering Guardian, Eternal Engine.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Humanity + Engine triad — every challenge blends raw physical power, meaningful empathetic/leadership moments, and hands-on fabrication.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 7 (nomad convoy) → NPC 9 (caravan protector) → NPC 6 (guardian forge vehicle).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with a small number of companions while rapidly destroying relations with larger districts and central authority.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you forge strength and safety for those you choose to protect, then leave the rest of the dying city and its ungrateful systems behind.
