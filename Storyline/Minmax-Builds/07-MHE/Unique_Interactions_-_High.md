# Minmax Build 07: Might + Humanity + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), empathy, charisma, moral intuition, bonding, and selective leadership (**Humanity**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at heroic physical intervention, forging protective gear and mobile sanctuaries, and inspiring/protecting a chosen few with genuine care while rejecting the larger failing systems.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward heartfelt leadership paired with raw strength and masterful engineering. The player physically protects people they care about, builds real solutions for them under pressure, and leads their small circle away from the collapsing city. Many open unique questlines involving loyal companion protection, custom sanctuary vehicles, moral high-risk fabrication, or direct paths toward guardian-style "Not My Fucking Problem" endings. Gates often require high Humanity alongside Might/Engine for trust-building and large-scale crafting.
