# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 12: Humanity + Nerve + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, bonding, moral intuition, leadership, and selective care), **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication).  
This creates a bold-hearted guardian forger: a deeply empathetic yet unbreakable leader who forges life-saving tech and mobile sanctuaries under extreme pressure, protects a chosen few with raw courage and mechanical genius, and ultimately rejects the failing city’s endless demands.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Iron Nomad (with loyal small crew)  
- Eternal Engine (protective variant)  
- Forge Lord (guardian variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward heartfelt leadership, fearless action under fire, and personal fabrication for a chosen circle.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward genuine emotional connection, courageous stands in crisis, and masterful hands-on engineering. The player builds real hope and protection for people they care about, endures the worst with unbreakable will, then leads their chosen few away — telling the rest of Concordia “Not My Fucking Problem.”

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Humanity + Nerve + Engine triad — every challenge blends meaningful empathetic/leadership moments, courageous endurance under pressure, and hands-on fabrication.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 7 (nomad convoy) → NPC 9 (caravan protector) → NPC 6 (guardian forge vehicle).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with a small number of companions while rapidly destroying relations with larger districts and central authority.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you forge strength and safety for those you choose to protect with heart, courage, and skill, then leave the rest of the dying city behind.
