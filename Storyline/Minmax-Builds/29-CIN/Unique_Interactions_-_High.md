# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 29: Calculation + Investigation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a **cold analytical specter**: a hyper-perceptive, brilliantly calculating operative who uncovers forbidden truths in the deadliest places, predicts perfect outcomes, and executes them with unbreakable nerve while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Unbreakable Ronin (analytical variant)  
- Phantom Courier (strategic variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward information dominance, calculated precision, and fearless execution.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine sharp deduction, cold strategic foresight, and unbreakable will under extreme pressure. The player is seen as a dangerous, highly competent force who uncovers what others fear to touch, calculates the perfect move, and strikes with ice-cold resolve.
