# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 29: Calculation + Investigation + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **panicky, oblivious, terrible-planner**: a character who misses obvious clues, makes awful strategic decisions, panics and freezes under pressure, and fails to connect basic information, often becoming a pitiable liability or accidental distraction.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s poor planning, investigative blindness, and easy panic. These limitations frequently cause awkward mishaps, missed dangers, failed decision-making, and situations where the player is seen as a harmless burden. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others compensate for the player’s obvious incompetence and fear.
