# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 29: Calculation + Investigation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a cold analytical specter: a hyper-perceptive, brilliantly calculating operative who uncovers hidden truths in the deadliest places, predicts perfect outcomes, and executes them with unbreakable nerve while rejecting all external dependencies.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Architect of Collapse  
- Unbreakable Ronin (analytical variant)  
- Phantom Courier (strategic variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward information dominance, calculated precision, and fearless execution.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward sharp deduction, cold strategic foresight, and unbreakable will under extreme pressure. The player uncovers what others cannot, calculates the perfect moves, and holds their nerve through mortal danger — then vanishes with superior knowledge and freedom, leaving Concordia to its ignorance and collapse.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Calculation + Investigation + Nerve triad — blending strategic foresight, relentless truth-seeking, and unbreakable will under mortal danger.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (strategic analysis lab) → NPC 9 (scouting expedition leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant massive information dominance and tactical precision while alienating districts that prefer secrets stay buried or controlled.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover the truth, calculate the perfect moves, endure hell with unbreakable nerve, and vanish before the city can react.
