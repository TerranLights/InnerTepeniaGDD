# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 13: Might + Investigation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a **fearless relic-enforcer**: a physically overwhelming investigator who uncovers forbidden truths in the deadliest places, holds unbreakable nerve through horror, and physically dominates anything that stands in their way while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Unbreakable Ronin  
- Architect of Collapse (enforcer variant)  
- Iron Nomad (relentless scout variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward relentless truth-seeking, fearless exploration, and overwhelming physical enforcement.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine overwhelming physical presence, relentless investigation, and unbreakable will under extreme pressure. The player is seen as a dangerous, highly competent force who uncovers what others fear to touch and physically claims or destroys it.
