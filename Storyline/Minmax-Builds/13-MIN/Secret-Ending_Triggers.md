# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 13: Might + Investigation + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a fearless relic-hunter enforcer: a physically overwhelming investigator who uncovers forbidden truths in the deadliest places, endures mortal peril without flinching, and physically dominates obstacles while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Unbreakable Ronin  
- Architect of Collapse (reconstructionist variant)  
- Iron Nomad (explorer variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward relentless secret-hunting, daring physical enforcement, and unbreakable will in the face of danger.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward sharp-eyed deduction, fearless dives into the unknown, and raw physical dominance under extreme pressure. The player uncovers what others cannot, holds their nerve through lethal situations, and physically claims or destroys what they find — then vanishes with their hoard, leaving Concordia to its ignorance and collapse.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Investigation + Nerve triad — combining physical dominance, relentless truth-seeking, and unbreakable will under mortal danger.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries archives) → NPC 6 (mobile relic lab).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant massive personal relic and tactical advantages while alienating districts that prefer secrets stay buried or regulated.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover the truth, brave the deadliest places, physically claim what you find, and leave the ignorant present to crumble.
