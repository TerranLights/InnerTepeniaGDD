# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 13: Might + Investigation + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Might** (physical weakness, poor endurance, and limited protective capability), **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **frail, oblivious panicky weakling**: a physically weak character who constantly misses obvious clues, panics and freezes under pressure, and fails to connect basic information, often becoming a pitiable liability.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s physical frailty, investigative blindness, and easy panic. These limitations frequently cause the player to overlook dangers, freeze at critical moments, and appear completely harmless or useless. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, accidental discoveries when others compensate, or situations where the player’s incompetence inadvertently reveals information.
