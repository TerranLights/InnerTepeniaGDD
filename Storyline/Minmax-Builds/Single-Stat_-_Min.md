# Low MACHINE Stat Interactions in Inner Tepenia

**Overview**  
This document outlines "low-stat" interactions for the MACHINE system when a stat is set to **3 or below**. These are inspired by classic Fallout low-Intelligence dialogue and events but expanded across all seven MACHINE stats.  

Low-stat interactions should feel flavorful, occasionally humorous, occasionally tragic or frustrating, and mechanically relevant (affecting dialogue, skill checks, environmental interactions, combat, crafting, social dynamics, etc.). They help reinforce character identity and can open (or close) unique questlines, reputation paths, or roleplaying opportunities.

Each stat section includes:
- A brief recap of the stat
- At least **7 different types** of unique interactions
- At least **3 sorts of immediate settings** where these interactions can occur

---

## Low Might (3 or below)

**Stat Recap**: Raw physical power, strength, and the ability to exert force. Governs melee damage, carrying capacity, breaking objects, and intimidation through sheer presence.

**Interaction Types**
1. **Physical Inadequacy**: Cannot lift or move heavy objects, crates, or debris that average characters handle easily. Must ask for help or find creative (often slower) workarounds.
2. **Being Physically Overpowered**: NPCs (even non-combatants) can easily restrain, push, or manhandle the player. Guards or thugs treat the player as non-threatening.
3. **Poor Melee Performance**: Struggles significantly in hand-to-hand or weapon-based combat; weapons feel "too heavy" and swings are weak or slow.
4. **Failure to Break or Force**: Cannot smash locked doors, weak walls, or containers that stronger characters can brute-force. Must rely on tools or hacking.
5. **Low Carrying Capacity & Encumbrance**: Becomes overburdened extremely quickly. Even moderate loot forces the player to drop items or move very slowly.
6. **Social Mockery or Pity**: NPCs comment on the player's weakness ("You couldn't lift a feather, could you?"). Can trigger bullying, pity, or exclusion from physical jobs.
7. **Environmental Failures**: Cannot climb certain ledges, push heavy machinery, or hold open heavy doors. Often requires companions or gadgets.
8. **Intimidation Backfire**: Attempts to intimidate through size/strength are laughed off or provoke stronger opponents.

**Immediate Settings**
- **Exploration & Environment**: Lifting debris, climbing, forcing doors, carrying loot in ruins or collapsed areas.
- **Combat & Confrontations**: Melee fights, being grappled or overpowered by enemies, failed intimidation checks.
- **Social & Roleplaying**: Physical labor jobs, being mocked in bars or by stronger NPCs, exclusion from "tough guy" groups.

## Low Agility (3 or below)

**Stat Recap**: Speed, reflexes, coordination, and fine motor control. Affects dodging, stealth, lockpicking, acrobatics, and quick actions.

**Interaction Types**
1. **Frequent Tripping & Falling**: Trips over small obstacles, own feet, or uneven ground. Can cause minor damage or alert enemies.
2. **Poor Dodging & Evasion**: Struggles to avoid attacks, traps, or projectiles. Often gets hit even when trying to dodge.
3. **Clumsy Fine Motor Tasks**: Fumbles tools, drops items, fails delicate actions like lockpicking or precise repairs.
4. **Bad Stealth & Sneaking**: Makes noise easily, bumps into things, or moves too slowly to stay hidden.
5. **Slow Reaction Time**: Takes longer to react to sudden events (explosions, ambushes, falling objects).
6. **Difficulty with Acrobatics/Parkour**: Cannot jump gaps, climb ropes, or navigate complex terrain that others can.
7. **Weapon & Tool Mishandling**: Drops weapons mid-fight, fumbles reloads, or accidentally discharges weapons.
8. **Coordination Failures in Group Actions**: Struggles to keep pace with agile allies or perform coordinated tasks.

**Immediate Settings**
- **Exploration & Movement**: Navigating ruins, rooftops, or tight spaces; avoiding environmental hazards.
- **Combat & Action**: Dodging attacks, stealth approaches, quick-time reactions during fights.
- **Crafting & Fine Work**: Lockpicking, delicate repairs, handling fragile tech or traps.

## Low Calculation (3 or below)

**Stat Recap**: Logical processing, computation, optimization, problem-solving, and foresight. The closest equivalent to low Intelligence.

**Interaction Types**
1. **Misunderstanding Instructions**: Frequently misinterprets orders, plans, or technical explanations. NPCs have to repeat things slowly.
2. **Poor Problem-Solving**: Cannot solve puzzles, optimize systems, or figure out how machines work without heavy guidance.
3. **Bad Decision-Making**: Makes obviously poor choices in dialogue or strategy that smarter characters avoid.
4. **Social Naivety**: Easily tricked, manipulated, or talked into bad deals. Gullible in conversations.
5. **Difficulty with Tech & Systems**: Struggles to operate computers, hack terminals, or understand complex machinery.
6. **Memory & Planning Issues**: Forgets important details, plans poorly, or misses obvious connections.
7. **Being Talked Down To**: NPCs (especially high-Calculation ones) treat the player like a child or idiot. Can trigger condescending dialogue.
8. **Failure in Optimization Tasks**: Cannot improve systems, find efficient routes, or calculate risks accurately.

**Immediate Settings**
- **Dialogue & Social**: Being manipulated, receiving condescending explanations, failing persuasion through logic.
- **Exploration & Puzzles**: Failing to solve environmental or terminal puzzles, missing clues.
- **Crafting & Tech**: Operating machinery, hacking, optimizing equipment, or understanding schematics.

## Low Humanity (3 or below)

**Stat Recap**: Emotional intelligence, empathy, social awareness, and connection with others. Affects persuasion, reading people, building rapport, and companion influence.

**Interaction Types**
1. **Social Awkwardness**: Says the wrong thing at the wrong time, offends people unintentionally, or fails to read the room.
2. **Poor Empathy & Understanding**: Misreads emotions, gives tone-deaf advice, or fails to comfort others.
3. **Difficulty Building Trust**: NPCs are wary or dismissive. Harder to recruit companions or gain faction favor.
4. **Manipulation Vulnerability**: Easily deceived by charismatic or deceptive NPCs.
5. **Bad at Leadership or Inspiration**: Fails to rally people or inspire loyalty. Companions may question or leave the player.
6. **Social Isolation**: Struggles to make friends or allies. Often left out of group conversations or plans.
7. **Offensive or Creepy Behavior**: Unintentionally comes across as rude, creepy, or emotionally distant.
8. **Failure in Emotional Persuasion**: Cannot calm angry NPCs or appeal to shared values.

**Immediate Settings**
- **Dialogue & Social Encounters**: Persuasion checks, companion conversations, faction negotiations.
- **Group Dynamics**: Rallying people, resolving conflicts, building alliances.
- **Roleplaying & Emotional Moments**: Comforting NPCs, reading intentions, handling sensitive topics.

## Low Investigation (3 or below)

**Stat Recap**: Pattern recognition, information gathering, deduction, and noticing details. Affects perception, finding clues, and uncovering secrets.

**Interaction Types**
1. **Missing Obvious Clues**: Walks past important items, notes, or environmental details that others notice immediately.
2. **Poor Deduction**: Jumps to wrong conclusions or fails to connect obvious dots.
3. **Bad at Searching**: Misses hidden compartments, loot, or secret passages.
4. **Easily Fooled by Misdirection**: Falls for red herrings or deceptive setups.
5. **Difficulty Reading Situations**: Misses social cues, lies, or hidden tensions in conversations.
6. **Overlooking Patterns**: Fails to notice recurring threats, cycles, or anomalies.
7. **Bad Forensics & Analysis**: Cannot properly examine bodies, scenes, or tech for clues.
8. **Information Overload or Blindness**: Either misses key details or gets overwhelmed by too much information.

**Immediate Settings**
- **Exploration & Investigation**: Searching rooms, examining scenes, finding hidden paths.
- **Dialogue & Interrogation**: Reading lies, noticing inconsistencies, gathering intel from NPCs.
- **Combat & Survival**: Spotting ambushes, noticing traps, analyzing enemy patterns.

## Low Nerve (3 or below)

**Stat Recap**: Mental resilience, willpower, resistance to stress/fear, and influence over companions.

**Interaction Types**
1. **Easily Panicked or Frightened**: Freezes, runs away, or makes poor decisions under pressure.
2. **Poor Stress Management**: Suffers breakdowns or loses composure in dangerous or emotional situations.
3. **Weak Companion Influence**: Companions are harder to control or more likely to disobey or leave.
4. **Vulnerable to Fear Effects**: More susceptible to intimidation, horror, or psychological attacks.
5. **Bad at Resisting Manipulation**: Easier to break or turn against allies under duress.
6. **Giving Up Easily**: Quits challenging tasks or loses motivation quickly.
7. **Overwhelmed by High-Stakes Decisions**: Freezes or makes rash choices when lives are on the line.
8. **Socially Weak-Willed**: Easily pushed around or convinced to do things against their better judgment.

**Immediate Settings**
- **Combat & High-Stress Situations**: Fights, ambushes, moral dilemmas under fire.
- **Horror or Psychological Encounters**: Facing terrifying events, interrogation, or mind-affecting threats.
- **Companion & Leadership Moments**: Managing followers during crises, resisting intimidation.

---

## Low Engine (3 or below)

**Stat Recap**: Operational endurance, efficiency, resistance to burnout, and long-term performance sustainability.

**Interaction Types**
1. **Quick Fatigue**: Tires out extremely fast during physical or prolonged activities.
2. **Inefficient Actions**: Wastes energy and resources. Actions take longer or cost more stamina.
3. **Frequent Burnout**: Needs frequent rest or suffers penalties after sustained effort.
4. **Poor Long-Duration Performance**: Struggles with extended exploration, travel, or crafting sessions.
5. **Overheating or System Strain**: (Thematically) "burns out" faster when using abilities or tech.
6. **Bad at Sustained Focus**: Loses concentration or effectiveness during long tasks.
7. **Higher Resource Consumption**: Uses more food, water, or energy for the same amount of work.
8. **Difficulty with Endurance-Based Tasks**: Fails at long marches, prolonged combat, or marathon crafting.

**Immediate Settings**
- **Exploration & Travel**: Long journeys, dungeon crawls, or sustained movement.
- **Crafting & Repair**: Extended repair or fabrication sessions.
- **Combat & Survival**: Prolonged fights or survival situations where stamina matters.

## General Notes
- Low-stat interactions should feel **distinct per stat** while still allowing creative overlap when multiple stats are low.
- These can be used for dialogue flags, skill check modifiers, environmental interactions, companion reactions, and quest design.
- Consider combining low stats for even more flavorful (and punishing) moments (e.g., low Humanity + low Nerve = socially anxious coward).
- Future documents can expand these into full dialogue trees, specific NPC reactions, or quest hooks.
