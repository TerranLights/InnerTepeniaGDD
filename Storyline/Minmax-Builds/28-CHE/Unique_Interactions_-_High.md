# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 28: Calculation + Humanity + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication).  
This creates a **brilliant guardian artisan**: a calculating, deeply empathetic leader who plans long-term solutions, inspires loyalty through heart and moral clarity, and fabricates real protection and sanctuaries with their own hands while rejecting the failing systems of Concordia.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Iron Nomad (with loyal crew)  
- Eternal Engine (empathetic artisan variant)  
- Forge Lord (guardian craftsman variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated moral leadership, heartfelt care, and masterful fabrication.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine strategic foresight, genuine emotional connection, and hands-on engineering mastery. The player is seen as a wise, trustworthy builder-leader who calculates the best path, cares deeply for their chosen few, and turns scrap into real hope and safety.
