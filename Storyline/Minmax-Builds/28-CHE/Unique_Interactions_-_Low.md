# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 28: Calculation + Humanity + Engine (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), and **Engine** (poor mechanical aptitude, inability to repair or craft effectively, tendency to break things).  
This creates a **socially tone-deaf, oblivious, destructive tinker-failure**: a character who makes awful strategic decisions, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, and breaks or ruins technology they touch, often becoming a pitiable burden or walking disaster.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s poor planning, emotional tone-deafness, and mechanical incompetence. These limitations frequently cause awkward social mishaps, broken equipment, failed repairs, and situations where the player is seen as a liability or harmless fool. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others fix what the player broke.
