# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 28: Calculation + Humanity + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication).  
This creates a brilliant guardian artisan: a calculating, deeply empathetic leader who plans long-term solutions, inspires loyalty in a chosen few, and fabricates real protection and sanctuaries with their own hands while rejecting the failing systems of Concordia.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Iron Nomad (with loyal crew)  
- Eternal Engine (empathetic variant)  
- Forge Lord (guardian artisan variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated leadership, heartfelt care, and masterful fabrication.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward strategic foresight, genuine emotional connection, and hands-on engineering. The player calculates optimal long-term solutions, builds real hope and safety for those they care about, and fabricates the tools needed to survive independently — then leads their chosen circle away from the collapsing city.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Calculation + Humanity + Engine triad — blending strategic foresight, genuine empathetic leadership, and masterful hands-on fabrication.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (guardian command forge) → NPC 9 (caravan leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while alienating larger districts reliant on central planning or exploitation.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you calculate the best path with heart and intellect, build real protection with your hands, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
