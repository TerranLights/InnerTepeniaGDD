# Minmax Build 08: Might + Investigation + Engine (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Might (≤3)**, **low Investigation (≤3)**, and **low Engine (≤3)**. The player is physically weak, perceptually oblivious (misses obvious details and clues), and has extremely poor endurance — tires out almost immediately from any sustained effort.

This creates a **"frail, blind, and constantly exhausted"** underdog archetype. The character is physically limited, frequently misses critical information that others see, and burns out so quickly that they become a major burden. They are often pitied, protected, dismissed as useless, or overlooked entirely. Their triple limitations can open unique paths through sympathy, being ignored as non-threatening, accidental discoveries from fumbling while tired, or reliance on other stats (especially Calculation, Humanity, Nerve, or Agility).

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize physical weakness, perceptual blindness, and rapid burnout. They should feel limiting and frustrating, but also create opportunities via pity, protective alliances, being underestimated, or "dumb luck" from exhaustion-induced mistakes. Many can lead to underdog questlines, "harmless burden" dynamics, or unconventional routes toward minmax endings.

## District-Specific Special/Unique Interactions (Minimum 5 per District)

### 1. Cancer (Nurturing/Protective Sanctuary)
- Too weak to carry patients and exhausts instantly; misses obvious signs of distress or hidden injuries.
- Gets heavily pitied as "the poor frail, unobservant one," receiving extra help or protected access to areas.
- Clumsy, tired movements cause accidents that accidentally reveal hidden compartments or data shards.
- Fails to notice subtle medical cues or dangers, leading to being shielded by staff during crises.
- Rapid burnout forces frequent rests in private rooms, overhearing confidential conversations.

### 2. Taurus (Residential/Stability)

- Exhausts quickly during any task, becoming a burden that earns protective behavior from residents.
- Clumsy exhaustion accidents damage property or alert people, creating apologetic social opportunities.
- Fails to notice social tensions or hidden entrances, but gains access through pity.
- Rapid burnout during neighborhood work leads to resting in private homes and overhearing secrets.

### 3. Leo (Cultural/Performative)

## Design Notes
- These interactions highlight the character's **physical weakness, perceptual blindness, and extremely poor stamina** — they miss the obvious, tire out quickly, and are often a burden, strongly inviting pity and being underestimated.
- Many interactions can combine with high stats in Calculation, Humanity, Nerve, or Agility for strong contrast (e.g., a brilliant strategist trapped in a weak, blind, quickly exhausted body).
- Questlines often lean into pity-based alliances, "harmless unobservant burden," or accidental discovery archetypes.
