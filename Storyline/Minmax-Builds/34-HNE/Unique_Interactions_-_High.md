# Minmax Build 12: Humanity + Nerve + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines deep empathy, charisma, moral intuition, bonding, and selective leadership (**Humanity**), courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and risk-taking (**Nerve**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at forging protective gear and mobile sanctuaries under extreme pressure, inspiring and protecting a chosen few with heartfelt leadership while rejecting the failing central systems.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward genuine emotional connection, courageous stands in crisis, and masterful hands-on engineering. The player builds real hope and safety for people they care about, endures the worst with unbreakable will, and leads their chosen circle away from the collapsing city. Many open unique questlines involving loyal companion protection, small-crew sanctuaries, moral high-risk fabrication, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Humanity + Nerve alongside Engine for trust-building and crafting under duress.
