# Minmax Build 12: Humanity + Nerve + Engine (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Humanity (≤3)**, **low Nerve (≤3)**, and **low Engine (≤3)**. The player is emotionally/socially tone-deaf (blunt, awkward, low empathy), mentally fragile (easily frightened, quick to panic or give up), and has extremely poor endurance — tires out almost immediately from any effort.

This creates a **"frail, socially awkward, fearful, and constantly exhausted"** underdog archetype. The character is physically limited, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, panics under pressure, and burns out so quickly that they become a major burden. They are frequently pitied, protected, dismissed as rude/useless, or treated as a harmless but annoying burden. Their limitations can open unique paths through sympathy, accidental blunt honesty, protective alliances, or "coward's luck" moments.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize emotional/social clumsiness, mental fragility, and rapid burnout. They should feel limiting, awkward, stressful, and exhausting, but also create opportunities via pity, protective behavior, raw (sometimes brutally honest) moments, or accidental discoveries while panicking or resting. Many can lead to underdog questlines, "awkward protected burden" dynamics, or unconventional routes toward minmax endings.

## District-Specific Special/Unique Interactions (Minimum 5 per District)

### 1. Cancer (Nurturing/Protective Sanctuary)
- Makes blunt, tone-deaf comments to grieving or traumatized patients, causing offense or awkward silence.
- Panics and tires out instantly during blackouts or medical emergencies, freezing or hiding.
- Gets heavily pitied as "the poor frail, rude one," receiving extra care or protected access out of sympathy.
- Rapid exhaustion leads to being carried or shielded, exposing private conversations or hidden areas.
- Socially clumsy emotional reactions create uncomfortable but sometimes cathartic moments.

### 2. Taurus (Residential/Stability)
- Makes blunt, awkward comments about family tensions or resource hoarding, offending neighbors.
- Panics and exhausts quickly during disputes or raids, becoming a burden that earns reluctant protection.
- Gets treated as socially inept and helpless, leading to pity-based shelter or gossip.
- Clumsy exhaustion from simple tasks creates apologetic social situations.
- Tone-deaf honesty accidentally exposes hidden issues or neighbor secrets.

### 3. Leo (Cultural/Performative)
- Makes socially disastrous, blunt comments about performances or artists, offending some while amusing others.
- Panics under crowd pressure or failing spectacles, freezing or fleeing.
- Gets pitied or mocked as the "exhausted, awkward one," granting backstage access out of sympathy.
- Rapid burnout during events forces hiding in quiet areas, accidentally overhearing rivalries.
- Tone-deaf emotional reactions disrupt shows in chaotic but revealing ways.

### 4. Scorpio (Psychological/Transformative)
- Makes callous or overly blunt comments during sensitive shadow-work sessions.
- Panics and burns out quickly during intense rituals, becoming a liability.
- Gets pitied as too frail and socially inept for real transformation work.
- Fearful clumsiness accidentally triggers safety systems or hidden revelations.
- Rapid exhaustion leads to emotional vulnerability and unexpected (pity-based) bonds.

### 5. Aries (Power Core / Raw Survival)
- Makes blunt, tactless comments to stressed engineers or soldiers.
- Panics near heat/radiation or failing machinery and tires out almost instantly.
- Gets heavily protected or dismissed by staff, allowing passive observation.
- Fearful exhaustion causes clumsy accidents that reveal hidden nodes.
- Tone-deaf reactions to danger create tension but also unexpected honesty.

### 7. Aquarius (Visionary/Experimental)

- Panics during failures or leaks and burns out quickly.

### 8. Libra (Diplomatic/Balance)

- Tires quickly in long sessions and panics under pressure.

## Design Notes
- These interactions highlight the character's **emotional/social awkwardness, mental fragility, and extremely poor stamina** — they say the wrong thing, panic easily, tire out almost immediately, and are often a burden, strongly inviting pity mixed with frustration.
- Many interactions can combine with high stats in Calculation, Investigation, Might, or Agility for strong contrast (e.g., a physically capable but blunt, fearful, and quickly exhausted character).
- Questlines often lean into pity-based alliances, "awkward fearful burden," or accidental blunt honesty archetypes.
