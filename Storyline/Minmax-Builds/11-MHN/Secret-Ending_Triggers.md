# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 11: Might + Humanity + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a bold-hearted unbreakable guardian: a physically imposing, deeply empathetic leader who endures the worst horrors with unbreakable will, protects a chosen few with raw courage and strength, and ultimately rejects the failing city’s endless demands.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Iron Nomad (with loyal crew)  
- Unbreakable Ronin (guardian variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward heartfelt leadership, fearless endurance under fire, and protective physical dominance.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward genuine emotional connection, courageous stands in crisis, and overwhelming physical presence. The player inspires loyalty through heart and unbreakable will, physically shields their chosen circle through hell, and leads them away from Concordia’s collapse.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Humanity + Nerve triad — every challenge blends meaningful empathetic/leadership moments, courageous endurance under pressure, and raw physical dominance.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 7 (nomad convoy) → NPC 9 (caravan protector) → NPC 6 (guardian mobile sanctuary).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with a small chosen circle while rapidly destroying relations with larger districts and central authority.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you endure hell with unbreakable will, protect those you choose with heart and strength, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
