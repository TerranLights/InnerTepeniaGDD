# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 11: Might + Humanity + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power, dominance, and protection), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a **fearless compassionate guardian**: a physically overwhelming leader who protects a chosen few with raw strength and unbreakable will, inspires loyalty through heart and moral clarity, and endures the worst horrors without flinching.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Unbreakable Ronin (guardian variant)  
- Iron Nomad (with loyal crew)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward heartfelt courageous leadership and protective endurance.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine overwhelming physical presence, genuine empathy, and fearless endurance under pressure. The player is seen as a strong, trustworthy protector who stands firm when others break and inspires deep loyalty in those they choose to shield.
