# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 11: Might + Humanity + Nerve (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Might** (physical weakness, poor endurance, and limited protective capability), **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), and **Nerve** (cowardice, panic under stress, low willpower, and mental fragility).  
This creates a **frail, socially clumsy panicky coward**: a physically weak character who says the wrong thing at the wrong time, panics easily under pressure, and struggles to connect with or inspire others, often becoming a pitiable burden.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s physical frailty, emotional awkwardness, and tendency to panic or freeze. These limitations frequently cause awkward social moments, failed leadership attempts, and being seen as harmless or pitiable. This can lead to pity-based alliances, protective behavior from others, or accidental usefulness when the player is dismissed as irrelevant.
