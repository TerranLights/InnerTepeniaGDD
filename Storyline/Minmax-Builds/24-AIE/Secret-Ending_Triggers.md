# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 24: Agility + Investigation + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Agility** (speed, reflexes, coordination, stealth, and graceful movement), **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication).  
This creates a phantom relic-forger: a lightning-fast, hyper-perceptive artisan who moves like a ghost, uncovers lost secrets in the most dangerous places, and rebuilds ancient tech into superior personal systems on the fly while pursuing total independence.

**Target Endings**:  
- Relic Hunter  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Phantom Courier  
- Architect of Collapse (mobile reconstruction variant)  
- Iron Nomad (high-mobility scavenger variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward graceful exploration, relentless truth-seeking, and masterful on-the-move fabrication.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward fluid high-speed movement, sharp deduction, and masterful hands-on reconstruction. The player darts through danger unseen, pieces together forgotten knowledge, and fabricates superior solutions in motion — then vanishes with their hoard, leaving Concordia to its ignorance and collapse.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Agility + Investigation + Engine triad — blending graceful high-speed movement, relentless truth-seeking, and masterful on-the-move fabrication.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (mobile fabrication lab) → NPC 9 (scavenging expedition leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths grant unmatched mobility, information dominance, and personal crafting depth while alienating districts that prefer secrets stay buried or regulated.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you move unseen, uncover the truth, rip the past out with your hands, rebuild it better for yourself, and vanish before the city can react.
