# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 24: Agility + Investigation + Engine (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Agility** (clumsiness, poor reflexes, bad coordination, terrible stealth/movement), **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details), and **Engine** (poor mechanical aptitude, inability to repair or craft effectively, tendency to break things).  
This creates a **clumsy, oblivious, destructive tinker-failure**: a character who trips constantly, misses obvious clues, breaks or ruins technology they touch, and makes terrible plans, often becoming a walking disaster or pitiable burden.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s clumsiness, investigative blindness, and mechanical incompetence. These limitations frequently cause mishaps, broken equipment, missed dangers, and situations where the player is seen as a liability or harmless fool. This can lead to pity-based alliances, being dismissed as irrelevant, or accidental usefulness when others fix what the player broke.

2. Misses obvious trail markers and raider signs due to poor perception.

4. Gets heavily pitied as “the clumsy one who breaks everything,” earning reluctant protective escorts.
