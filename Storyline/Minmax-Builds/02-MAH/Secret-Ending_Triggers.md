# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 02: Might + Agility + Humanity

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Agility** (speed, mobility, precision, stealth), and **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, bonding, moral intuition, leadership, and selective care).  
This creates a charismatic lone guardian or small-pack leader: an incredibly strong and mobile survivor who uses deep personal connections and moral conviction to protect a chosen few while completely rejecting the failing districts and their burdens.

**Target Endings**:  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Last Shepherd  
- Beloved Exile  
- Unbreakable Ronin (charismatic/lone wolf variant)  
- Iron Nomad (with small loyal crew)  
- Eternal Seeker (guided by personal bonds)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward personal loyalty, protective strength, and selective humanity over systemic salvation.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward physical heroism, graceful intervention, and genuine (but limited) human connection. The player solves problems through superior capability and charisma, forms tight bonds with a small number of NPCs, then chooses to walk away from the larger collapsing society — protecting their chosen few while telling the rest of Concordia "Not My Fucking Problem."

## World / Environmental Triggers (7)

1. **Abandoned High-Speed Maintenance Rail (Virgo Undergrid)**  
   Collapsed but repairable rail. Player uses Might + Agility to clear paths and Humanity-aligned choices (e.g., helping trapped maintenance workers) to unlock hidden exit routes and small-group transport schematics.  
   **Strongly supports**: Wandering Guardian, Phantom Courier, Iron Nomad.

2. **Glitching Pre-War Military Exosuit Prototype (Aries restricted ruins)**  
   Interactive wreckage. Requires Might to access, Agility to navigate hazards, and Humanity-driven decisions (e.g., choosing to save a trapped NPC) to fully restore it into guardian-class mobility armor.  
   **Strongly supports**: Unbreakable Ronin, Wandering Guardian, Beloved Exile.

3. **Encrypted Overland Route Data Cache (Sagittarius frostline marker)**  
   Weather-beaten terminal. High Agility and Might to reach/defend; Humanity checks reveal personal messages from past guardians, unlocking viable small-group migration paths.  
   **Strongly supports**: Last Shepherd, Iron Nomad, Wandering Guardian.

4. **Collapsed but Hackable Aries Micro-Reactor (hidden backup node)**  
   Environmental puzzle: physically stabilize (Might + Agility) while making moral choices about power distribution to nearby survivors, yielding a portable core suited for a small nomadic group.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Last Shepherd.

5. **Ancient Mobility Rig Testing Ground (outer Capricorn scrap fields)**  
   Ruined proving grounds. Completing strength and agility trials while choosing to aid or bond with stranded test AIs/NPCs unlocks blueprints for crew-capable high-mobility frames.  
   **Strongly supports**: Unbreakable Ronin, Wandering Guardian.

6. **Hidden Server Vault with Predictive Survival Algorithms**  
   Sealed Gemini ruin. Agility to bypass traps, Might to force entry, and Humanity to interpret "protect your chosen few" ethical subroutines in lone-survivor AIs.  
   **Strongly supports**: Eternal Seeker, Beloved Exile, Last Shepherd.

7. **Derelict Armored Nomad Convoy Wreck (Frostlands edge)**  
   Multi-stage site. Might to clear wreckage, Agility to explore, and Humanity interactions with surviving logs or stranded individuals to salvage a functional small-crew mobile base.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Wandering Guardian.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Agility + Humanity triad — physical heroism and graceful action are always paired with meaningful empathetic or leadership moments.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 7 (nomad convoy) → NPC 9 (caravan protector) → NPC 6 (mobile habitat).
- Reputation impact: These paths build strong loyalty with a small number of NPCs/companions while rapidly destroying relations with districts and large factions.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you save and bond with your chosen few through superior strength, speed, and heart, then abandon the rest of the failing city.
