# Minmax Build 02: Might + Agility + Humanity - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), graceful speed and precision movement (**Agility**), and deep empathy, charisma, moral intuition, bonding, and selective leadership (**Humanity**). The player excels at heroic physical intervention, moving fluidly through danger, and forming genuine (but limited) connections with a chosen few. They become a charismatic guardian who protects small groups while rejecting the larger failing systems.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward heroic rescues, empathetic leadership, and moral choices paired with physical excellence. Many open unique questlines involving loyalty, small-crew protection, hidden sanctuaries, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Humanity alongside Might/Agility for emotional depth and trust-building.
