# Minmax Build 02: Might + Agility + Humanity (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Might (≤3)**, **low Agility (≤3)**, and **low Humanity (≤3)**. The player is physically weak, clumsy and uncoordinated, and emotionally/socially tone-deaf — blunt, awkward, and often unintentionally offensive or oblivious to others' feelings.

This creates a highly vulnerable, socially maladroit underdog who is frequently underestimated, pitied, or dismissed. Interactions often revolve around physical limitation, clumsiness causing problems, and emotional bluntness leading to humorous, awkward, or surprisingly insightful moments. The character can still shine through other stats (especially Calculation, Investigation, Nerve, or Engine) or by leaning into their "harmless fool" or "pitiful" reputation.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize the character's physical frailty, lack of grace, and emotional clumsiness. They should feel awkward, sometimes comedic, sometimes painful, but open unique paths through pity, being overlooked, accidental honesty, or reliance on non-physical/non-social strengths. Many can lead to unconventional alliances, "underdog" questlines, or routes toward specific minmax endings via unexpected means.

## District-Specific Special/Unique Interactions (Minimum 5 per District)

### 1. Cancer (Nurturing/Protective Sanctuary)
- Too weak and clumsy to help carry patients or equipment, often dropping things or getting in the way during emergencies.
- Bluntly says the wrong thing to grieving or traumatized individuals, causing offense but sometimes accidentally cutting through denial or bureaucracy.
- Gets pitied by caregivers and refugees, receiving special (often condescending) help or access to restricted areas.
- Struggles with fine motor tasks in medical settings, leading to comedic failures or reliance on others.
- Emotionally tone-deaf responses to trauma create awkward but memorable interactions that can reveal hidden information through raw honesty.

### 2. Taurus (Residential/Stability)
- Physically unable to assist with heavy lifting or home repairs, leading to being assigned "simple" tasks that expose neighborhood gossip.
- Clumsily breaks or damages household items while trying to help, creating debt or apologetic social situations.
- Makes blunt, socially awkward comments about family tensions or resource hoarding, sometimes accidentally resolving conflicts through oblivious honesty.
- Gets treated as harmless and pitiable, allowing access to private conversations or gated areas.
- Struggles with social navigation in tight-knit residential communities, leading to exclusion or unexpected pity-based alliances.

### 3. Leo (Cultural/Performative)
- Too clumsy and weak for stage work or physical performances, often becoming accidental comic relief or a liability.
- Makes tone-deaf or brutally honest comments about performances or artists, offending some but resonating with others in unexpected ways.
- Gets pitied or mocked by performers, granting backstage access or "harmless fool" status.
- Struggles with crowd movement and coordination during events, creating chaotic but revealing situations.
- Blunt emotional reactions to spectacles can disrupt events in ways that uncover hidden rivalries or secrets.

### 4. Scorpio (Psychological/Transformative)
- Physically too weak for intense rituals and emotionally too blunt for delicate shadow-work, leading to awkward or failed sessions.
- Makes unintentionally callous or overly direct comments during therapy, sometimes triggering breakthroughs or violent reactions.
- Gets treated as emotionally "broken" or pitiable, allowing deeper access to radical transformation programs.
- Clumsily interferes with sensitive equipment or rituals, causing unexpected revelations.
- Struggles with reading trauma survivors, but raw bluntness can occasionally cut through emotional walls.

### 5. Aries (Power Core / Raw Survival)
- Too weak to handle heavy machinery or physical labor in the core, often getting assigned "safe" observation roles.
- Blunt and clumsy comments around stressed engineers create tension but also accidental honesty about dangerous practices.
- Gets pitied by hardened workers, leading to protective behavior or sharing of restricted information.
- Struggles with coordination in high-stress environments, causing accidents that reveal hidden system flaws.
- Emotionally tone-deaf reactions to overclock dangers can provoke strong responses from staff.

### 6. Capricorn (Industrial/Ambition)
- Physically unable to perform heavy industrial work, leading to being given menial or observational tasks.
- Makes blunt, socially awkward comments about meritocracy or working conditions, sometimes exposing corruption.
- Clumsy mistakes in the foundries create accidents that reveal hidden shortcuts or illegal operations.
- Gets underestimated as non-threatening, allowing unnoticed observation of sensitive areas.
- Struggles with endurance and coordination, forcing reliance on pity or clever delegation.

### 7. Aquarius (Visionary/Experimental)
- Too weak and clumsy for handling experimental equipment, often causing minor disasters through fumbling.
- Blunt and emotionally oblivious comments about risky projects or ethics create tension or unexpected insights.
- Gets pitied by researchers as "harmless but unfortunate," granting access to restricted labs.
- Struggles with social navigation among intellectuals, leading to being overlooked or underestimated.
- Tone-deaf reactions to test subject suffering can trigger moral confrontations or alliances.

### 8. Libra (Diplomatic/Balance)
- Physically weak and clumsy in formal settings; makes socially disastrous blunt statements during negotiations.
- Tone-deaf emotional responses offend diplomats but sometimes cut through political doublespeak.
- Gets dismissed as irrelevant or pitiable, allowing passive observation of sensitive meetings.
- Struggles with coordination during tense events, creating chaotic openings.

### 9. Gemini (Data/Communication Nexus)
- Clumsy navigation of data-heavy environments leads to accidental data exposure or system glitches.
- Blunt, socially awkward comments in forums or conversations create memorable (and revealing) interactions.
- Gets treated as harmless or comic relief, allowing access to private channels or gossip.
- Misreads social cues in information networks, leading to unexpected discoveries through obliviousness.

### 10. Pisces (Black Market/Underworld)

- Struggles with coordination in extreme terrain, causing accidents that reveal hidden paths or caches.

- Blunt and awkward comments to maintenance crews create tension but also raw honesty about conditions.

## Design Notes
- These interactions highlight the character's **physical frailty, clumsiness, and emotional/social ineptitude** — they are often a burden or comic relief, but this can be leveraged for pity, being overlooked, or accidental honesty.
- Many interactions can combine with high stats in Calculation, Investigation, Nerve, or Engine for powerful contrast (e.g., brilliant mind trapped in a weak, awkward body).
- Questlines often lean into underdog, pity-based, or "harmless fool" archetypes, potentially leading to protective or unconventional minmax endings.
