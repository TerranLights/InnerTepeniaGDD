# Minmax Build 09: Might + Nerve + Engine (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Might (≤3)**, **low Nerve (≤3)**, and **low Engine (≤3)**. The player is physically weak, mentally fragile (easily frightened, low stress tolerance, quick to panic or give up), and has extremely poor endurance — tires out almost immediately from any effort.

This creates a **"frail, fearful, and constantly exhausted"** underdog archetype. The character is physically limited, panics easily under pressure, and burns out so quickly that they become a major burden in almost any demanding situation. They are frequently pitied, protected, dismissed as useless, or used as bait. Their obvious vulnerability can open unique paths through sympathy, being overlooked as non-threatening, protective alliances, or "coward's luck" moments.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize physical weakness, mental fragility, and rapid burnout. They should feel limiting, stressful, and exhausting, but also create opportunities via pity, protective behavior from others, hiding/fleeing, or accidental discoveries while panicking or resting. Many can lead to underdog questlines, "burden" dynamics, or unconventional routes toward minmax endings.

## District-Specific Special/Unique Interactions (Minimum 5 per District)

### 1. Cancer (Nurturing/Protective Sanctuary)
- Too weak to carry patients and exhausts instantly; panics and freezes during blackouts or medical emergencies.
- Rapid burnout and fear cause the player to hide or collapse, leading to being carried/protected by staff and gaining access to private areas.
- Gets heavily pitied as "the poor frail one," receiving extra care or restricted information out of sympathy.
- Panics and clumsily knocks over equipment while trying to help, accidentally revealing hidden compartments or data.
- Overwhelmed by the emotional atmosphere, breaking down in ways that trigger protective or confessional responses from others.

### 2. Taurus (Residential/Stability)
- Tires out immediately during any physical labor or defense; panics and hides during resource disputes or raids.
- Gets treated as a helpless burden, earning protective behavior and shared shelter from residents.
- Fearful reactions and quick exhaustion cause clumsy accidents that reveal hidden storage or smuggling.
- Struggles with sustained social stress in tight-knit neighborhoods, leading to awkward but revealing interactions.
- Frequent burnout forces the player to rest in private homes, overhearing gossip or secrets.

### 3. Leo (Cultural/Performative)
- Too weak and low-stamina for stage work; panics under crowds or failing spectacles and flees.
- Gets pitied or seen as comic relief, granting backstage access or protective escort.
- Rapid exhaustion during events forces hiding in quiet areas, accidentally discovering hidden rivalries or tech.
- Fearful clumsiness disrupts performances in chaotic but revealing ways.
- Overwhelmed by loud chaos, leading to hiding in VIP or restricted zones.

### 4. Scorpio (Psychological/Transformative)
- Too weak for physical rituals and too fearful for intense shadow-work; panics and burns out quickly.
- Gets pitied as too fragile for real transformation, allowing observational or emotional support access.
- Fearful reactions during sessions accidentally trigger safety systems or hidden revelations.
- Rapid burnout leads to emotional vulnerability that can create deep (pity-based) bonds.
- Overwhelmed by taboo environments, causing the player to hide in forbidden chambers.

### 5. Aries (Power Core / Raw Survival)
- Too weak to handle machinery and panics near heat/radiation; exhausts almost instantly.
- Gets dismissed or heavily protected by engineers, allowing passive observation of restricted areas.
- Fearful panic and quick fatigue cause accidents that reveal hidden backup nodes.
- Rapid burnout forces others to carry or shield the player, exposing classified areas.
- Overwhelmed by core stress, leading to hiding in maintenance tunnels.

### 6. Capricorn (Industrial/Ambition)
- Tires out immediately during labor and panics around heavy machinery or accidents.
- Gets pitied and assigned "safe" menial tasks, leading to observational discoveries.
- Fearful clumsiness causes frequent mistakes that expose hidden operations or shortcuts.
- Rapid burnout in forges forces protective intervention from workers.
- Overwhelmed by industrial noise and danger, leading to hiding in storage or restricted zones.

### 7. Aquarius (Visionary/Experimental)
- Too weak and fearful to handle experiments; panics and burns out during failures or leaks.
- Gets pitied by researchers as too fragile, granting safe observational access to labs.
- Fear-driven clumsiness accidentally triggers prototypes or reveals hidden data.
- Rapid exhaustion leads to hiding in restricted or quarantine areas.
- Overwhelmed by ethical/physical stress, creating moments of vulnerability with staff.

### 8. Libra (Diplomatic/Balance)
- Tires quickly in long sessions and panics under political pressure or confrontations.
- Gets dismissed as frail and fearful, allowing passive observation of sensitive meetings.
- Fearful reactions disrupt formal settings in ways that reveal hidden tensions.
- Rapid burnout under stress leads to hiding in secure spires or backrooms.
- Overwhelmed by diplomatic tension, causing accidental eavesdropping opportunities.

### 9. Gemini (Data/Communication Nexus)
- Exhausts rapidly in data-heavy environments and panics during system failures or pursuits.
- Gets treated as harmless and fearful, allowing access to private nodes or channels.
- Clumsy panic triggers alerts or exposes hidden data caches.
- Rapid burnout leads to hiding in quiet terminals during overloads.
- Overwhelmed by information chaos, creating moments of accidental discovery.

### 10. Pisces (Black Market/Underworld)
- Too weak and fearful for dangerous dealings; panics and tires out during raids or negotiations.
- Gets pitied or dismissed as useless, allowing access to hidden vice areas.
- Fearful clumsiness disrupts operations in chaotic but revealing ways.
- Rapid burnout in stressful environments creates hiding spots in alcoves.
- Overwhelmed by underworld threats, leading to protective alliances or shared secrets.

### 11. Sagittarius (Frostlands/Frontier)
- Tires almost immediately in harsh terrain and panics during storms or raider threats.
- Gets heavily pitied by settlers, who offer protection and shared shelter.
- Fearful exhaustion causes clumsy slips that accidentally uncover buried caches.
- Rapid burnout during expeditions forces constant rest in hidden outposts.
- Overwhelmed by extreme conditions, leading to hiding in abandoned structures.

### 12. Virgo (Undergrid/Maintenance)
- Too weak and low-stamina for tunnel work; panics during collapses or hazards and burns out quickly.
- Gets pitied by maintenance crews, who provide guidance or carry them.
- Fearful clumsiness causes minor collapses that reveal hidden annexes.
- Rapid exhaustion leads to resting in concealed service areas.
- Overwhelmed by dark, confined spaces, creating opportunities for hidden discoveries.

## Design Notes
- These interactions highlight the character's **physical weakness, mental fragility, and extremely poor stamina** — they panic easily, tire out almost immediately, and are often a liability, strongly inviting pity and protection.
- Many interactions can combine with high stats in Calculation, Investigation, Humanity, or Agility for strong contrast (e.g., a brilliant mind trapped in a weak, fearful, quickly exhausted body).
- Questlines often lean heavily into pity-based alliances, "protected burden," or accidental discovery while hiding/resting from fear and fatigue.
