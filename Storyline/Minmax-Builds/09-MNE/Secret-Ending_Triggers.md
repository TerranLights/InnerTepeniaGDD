# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 09: Might + Nerve + Engine

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude), and **Engine** (mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, vehicle/power system fabrication).  
This creates a fearless daredevil forge-walker: an unbreakable adrenaline-fueled mechanic who rips apart failing machines in the middle of disasters, rebuilds them into superior personal weapons and vehicles under extreme stress, and laughs at death while rejecting every safety net the city offers.

**Target Endings**:  
- Iron Nomad  
- Eternal Engine  
- Unbreakable Ronin (daredevil engineer variant)  
- Architect of Collapse (high-risk fabrication variant)  
- Forge Lord (lone thrill-seeker variant)  
- Phantom Courier (custom death-defying vehicle focus)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward balls-to-the-wall risk-taking, mechanical genius under fire, and total personal self-sufficiency.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward charging headfirst into lethal situations, enduring insane stress, and using raw strength + mechanical mastery to turn chaos into custom-built superiority. The player solves problems by pushing machines and themselves to the absolute limit, then walks away stronger — leaving the collapsing districts to deal with their own failures.

3. **Encrypted Overland Route Data Cache (Sagittarius frostline marker)**

4. **Collapsed but Hackable Aries Micro-Reactor (hidden backup node)**  
   Environmental death trap in full meltdown. Player must use Nerve to push through lethal radiation/heat, Might to physically stabilize the core, and Engine to convert it into a compact, overclockable personal power plant.

5. **Ancient Mobility Rig Testing Ground (outer Capricorn scrap fields)**

6. **Hidden Server Vault with Predictive Survival Algorithms**  
   Sealed Gemini ruin with cascading system failures and deadly traps. Requires Nerve to push forward through chaos, Might to breach, and Engine to repair and adapt the systems into portable high-risk survival tech.

7. **Derelict Armored Nomad Convoy Wreck (Frostlands edge)**  
   Unstable, half-buried death trap constantly shifting under ice pressure. Player must use Nerve to explore collapsing sections, Might to move heavy plating, and Engine to salvage and fully restore a reinforced mobile forge-base.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Forge Lord, Eternal Engine.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Nerve + Engine triad — every challenge is a high-stakes adrenaline test of raw power, unbreakable willpower, and hands-on mechanical genius under extreme pressure.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries designs) → NPC 6 (mobile forge).
- Reputation impact: These paths destroy district relations extremely quickly but grant powerful risk-tolerant perks, unique overclocked gear, and self-sufficient fabrication options.
- Tone: Every trigger screams "Not My Fucking Problem" — you stare death in the face, rip apart the old world while laughing, rebuild it better and meaner for yourself alone, and leave the weak behind.
