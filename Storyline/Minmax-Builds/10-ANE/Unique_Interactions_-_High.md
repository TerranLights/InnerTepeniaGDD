# Minmax Build 10: Agility + Nerve + Engine - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines graceful speed and precision movement (**Agility**), courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude (**Nerve**), and mechanical mastery, crafting, repair, optimization, and fabrication (**Engine**). The player excels at moving like lightning through chaos, performing death-defying maneuvers, and rebuilding or overclocking machines on the move while rejecting every safety net the city offers.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward fluid high-speed action, fearless risk-taking, and hands-on engineering performed in motion. The player blurs past danger, repairs or fabricates while running, and turns the environment into personal high-mobility superiority. Many open unique questlines involving lightning-fast vehicles, hit-and-run fabrication, extreme courier work, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Agility + Nerve alongside Engine for death-defying mobility and crafting under pressure.
