# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 04: Might + Agility + Nerve

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Agility** (speed, mobility, precision, stealth), and **Nerve** (courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, risk-taking, and mental fortitude).  
This creates a fearless adrenaline-fueled lone wolf: an unbreakable daredevil who charges into impossible situations, endures extreme stress, moves like lightning, and laughs in the face of death while rejecting every safety net and societal obligation.

**Target Endings**:  
- Unbreakable Ronin  
- Iron Nomad  
- Eternal Seeker  
- Phantom Courier  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Architect of Collapse (reckless/high-risk variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward raw daring, unbreakable willpower, and thrill-seeking independence.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward balls-to-the-wall courage, pushing physical and mental limits, and thriving in high-stakes chaos. The player solves problems by charging headfirst with superior strength, speed, and sheer nerve — then walks away from the consequences, leaving Concordia’s collapsing systems behind.

## World / Environmental Triggers (7)

1. **Abandoned High-Speed Maintenance Rail (Virgo Undergrid)**  
   Collapsed high-velocity rail system. Player must use Nerve to sprint through unstable sections, Might to clear massive blockages, and Agility to make death-defying jumps, revealing hidden high-risk exit routes.  
   **Strongly supports**: Phantom Courier, Ghost of the Undergrid, Iron Nomad.

2. **Glitching Pre-War Military Exosuit Prototype (Aries restricted ruins)**  
   Dangerous, unstable wreckage. Requires Nerve to activate under live-fire fail-safes, Might to rip open armored panels, and Agility to survive the resulting structural collapse.  
   **Strongly supports**: Unbreakable Ronin, Iron Nomad, Eternal Seeker.

3. **Encrypted Overland Route Data Cache (Sagittarius frostline marker)**  
   Perched on a crumbling ice cliff. High Nerve to endure lethal storms, Might + Agility to reach the terminal, unlocking the most dangerous (but fastest) solo migration vectors.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Phantom Courier, Eternal Seeker.

4. **Collapsed but Hackable Aries Micro-Reactor (hidden backup node)**  
   Environmental death trap: physically stabilize a critical meltdown (Might + Agility) while pushing through lethal heat and radiation with raw Nerve to extract portable power tech.  
   **Strongly supports**: Architect of Collapse, Iron Nomad, Unbreakable Ronin.

5. **Ancient Mobility Rig Testing Ground (outer Capricorn scrap fields)**  
   Extreme obstacle course filled with lethal hazards. Completing it demands peak Nerve (to ignore pain and fear), Might, and Agility, unlocking blueprints for insane high-risk mobility frames.  
   **Strongly supports**: Unbreakable Ronin, Phantom Courier.

6. **Hidden Server Vault with Predictive Survival Algorithms**  
   Sealed Gemini ruin rigged with deadly traps. Requires Nerve to push forward through psychological warfare systems, Might to break in, and Agility to dodge final defenses.  
   **Strongly supports**: Eternal Seeker, Architect of Collapse, Ghost of the Undergrid.

7. **Derelict Armored Nomad Convoy Wreck (Frostlands edge)**  
   Unstable, half-buried death trap. Player must use Nerve to explore collapsing sections, Might to shift wreckage, and Agility to escape sudden cave-ins while salvaging elite gear.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Phantom Courier, Unbreakable Ronin.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Agility + Nerve triad — every challenge is a high-stakes adrenaline test combining brute force, graceful movement, and unbreakable willpower.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries schematics) → NPC 6 (mobile rig).
- Reputation impact: These paths destroy district relations extremely quickly but grant powerful risk-tolerant perks, unique survival bonuses, and fear-based reputation among outcasts.
- Tone: Every trigger screams "Not My Fucking Problem" — you stare death in the face, win through sheer nerve, take what you want, and leave the weak and the systems behind.
