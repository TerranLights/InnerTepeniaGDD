# Minmax Build 04: Might + Agility + Nerve - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), graceful speed and precision movement (**Agility**), and unbreakable courage, willpower, stress resistance, boldness, and risk-taking (**Nerve**). The player excels at charging headfirst into lethal situations, enduring extreme danger without flinching, and performing death-defying physical feats while rejecting all safety nets and societal obligations.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward adrenaline-fueled heroism, pushing personal and environmental limits, and thriving in chaos. The player solves problems by staring death in the face, winning through sheer daring and physical excellence, then walking away stronger. Many open unique questlines involving high-risk operations, daredevil challenges, or direct paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence endings. Gates often require high Nerve alongside Might/Agility for pushing through fear, pain, and mortal danger.
