# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - High Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 26: Calculation + Humanity + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a **brilliant empathetic truth-seeker**: a calculating, deeply compassionate leader who uncovers hidden truths, plans optimal long-term solutions with moral clarity, and inspires loyalty in a chosen few while rejecting Concordia’s deceptions and collapse.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Architect of Collapse (visionary leader variant)  
- Relic Hunter (strategic empath variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated moral leadership, insightful foresight, and empathetic truth-seeking.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers reward players who combine sharp deduction, genuine emotional connection, and cold strategic planning. The player is seen as a wise, trustworthy leader who uncovers injustices, calculates the best ethical path forward, and inspires others to follow with both heart and intellect.
