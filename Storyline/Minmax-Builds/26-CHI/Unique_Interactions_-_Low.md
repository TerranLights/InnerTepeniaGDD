# Special Interactions & Quest Triggers - Low Minmax Builds
## Minmax Build 26: Calculation + Humanity + Investigation (Low)

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: Low **Calculation** (poor strategic planning, weak foresight, and difficulty with optimization), **Humanity** (emotional tone-deafness, poor social intuition, and difficulty forming genuine bonds), and **Investigation** (poor perception, weak deduction, difficulty noticing clues, and missing obvious details).  
This creates a **socially tone-deaf, oblivious, terrible-planner**: a character who misses obvious clues, makes awful strategic decisions, says the wrong thing at the wrong time, and struggles to read people or situations, often becoming a pitiable burden or accidental distraction.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions and triggers emphasize the player’s poor planning, social awkwardness, and investigative blindness. These limitations frequently cause awkward social mishaps, missed opportunities, failed leadership attempts, and situations where the player is underestimated or pitied. This can lead to pity-based alliances, accidental discoveries when others compensate, or moments where the player’s incompetence inadvertently reveals information.
