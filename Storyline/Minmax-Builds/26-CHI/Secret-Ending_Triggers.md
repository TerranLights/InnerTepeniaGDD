# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 26: Calculation + Humanity + Investigation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Calculation** (strategic planning, optimization, predictive modeling, and foresight), **Humanity** (empathy, charisma, moral intuition, leadership, and selective bonding), and **Investigation** (perception, deduction, clue-tracking, forensics, and secret uncovering).  
This creates a brilliant empathetic truth-seeker: a calculating, deeply compassionate leader who uncovers hidden truths, plans optimal long-term solutions, and inspires loyalty in a chosen few while rejecting Concordia’s deceptions and collapse.

**Target Endings**:  
- Last Shepherd  
- Wandering Guardian  
- Beloved Exile  
- Architect of Collapse (visionary variant)  
- Relic Hunter (strategic empath variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward calculated truth-seeking, moral leadership, and insightful foresight.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward sharp deduction, genuine emotional connection, and cold strategic planning. The player uncovers secrets others miss, calculates the best path forward with heart and intellect, and inspires a chosen circle — then leads them away from the city’s lies and failures.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Calculation + Humanity + Investigation triad — blending strategic foresight, genuine empathetic leadership, and relentless truth-seeking.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 3 (route data) → NPC 6 (strategic command sanctuary) → NPC 9 (caravan leadership).
- Reputation impact: These paths build deep loyalty with small chosen groups while alienating larger districts that prefer secrets stay buried or controlled.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you uncover the truth, calculate the best path with heart and intellect, protect those you choose, and leave the rest of the dying city behind.
