# Minmax Build 06: Might + Calculation + Engine (Three Low Stats) - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This "Min" build focuses on a character with **low Might (≤3)**, **low Calculation (≤3)**, and **low Engine (≤3)**. The player is physically weak, mentally slow at problem-solving/optimization, and has extremely poor endurance — tires out almost immediately and operates inefficiently.

This creates a "frail, dim, and constantly exhausted" underdog archetype. The character struggles with lifting, understanding complex systems, and sustaining any effort. They are frequently pitied, protected, dismissed as useless, or assigned simple tasks. Their limitations can open unique paths through sympathy, being underestimated, accidental "dumb luck," or reliance on other stats (especially Humanity, Investigation, Nerve, or Agility).

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions emphasize physical weakness, intellectual sluggishness, and rapid burnout. They should feel limiting and frustrating, but also create opportunities via pity, protective alliances, simplistic but sometimes insightful approaches, or accidental discoveries from fumbling/exhaustion. Many can lead to underdog questlines, "harmless simpleton" dynamics, or unconventional routes toward minmax endings.
