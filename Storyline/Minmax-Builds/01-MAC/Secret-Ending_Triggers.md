# Alternate Minmax Endings - "Not My Fucking Problem" Path
## Minmax Build 01: Might + Agility + Computation

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Focus**: High **Might** (raw physical power & endurance), **Agility** (speed, mobility, precision, stealth), and **Computation** (optimization, hacking, predictive modeling, system mastery).  
This creates a hyper-capable lone operator: a fast, lethal, tech-augmented survivor who can outfight, outmaneuver, and outthink threats while rejecting systemic dependency.

**Target Endings**:  
- Unbreakable Ronin  
- Iron Nomad  
- Phantom Courier  
- Eternal Seeker  
- Ghost of the Undergrid  
- Architect of Collapse (tech-dominant variant)  
- Any other "Not My Fucking Problem" endings that reward extreme personal competence and grid abandonment.

**Design Philosophy**: Triggers reward proactive self-reliance, system exploitation, rapid mobility, and calculated risk-taking. They feel like natural extensions of a Might + Agility + Computation playstyle — breaking into places, optimizing failing tech on the fly, winning brutal fights, and choosing to walk away stronger and unburdened.

4. **TBD-NPC – Disillusioned Aries Overclock Trainer**

5. **TBD-NPC – Young Runner & Data Smuggler (Leo underbelly)**  
   After the player agilely rescues them from a collapsing structure and hacks the security logs in record time, they introduce the player to a secret circuit of independent operators who live by "speed, strength, and silicon."  
   **Strongly supports**: Phantom Courier, Unbreakable Ronin.

6. **TBD-NPC – Capricorn Exile (operating in a hidden Sagittarius outpost)**

   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Architect of Collapse.

7. **TBD-NPC – Rogue Psychologist (Scorpio shadow clinic)**  
   After the player demonstrates perfect emotional control under extreme physical stress (high Might + Agility) while cracking encrypted trauma archives (Computation), they reveal the philosophy of total detachment and offer contacts for off-grid rebirth.

8. **TBD-NPC – Anonymous Gemini Net-Runner (hidden in an abandoned data nexus)**

9. **TBD-NPC – Independent Caravan Protector (temporary Taurus camp)**

## World / Environmental Triggers (7)

1. **Abandoned High-Speed Maintenance Rail (Virgo Undergrid)**  
   Collapsed but repairable rail system. Player can use Might + Agility to clear debris and Computation to reactivate power flow, revealing hidden exit vectors and mobile platform schematics.  
   **Strongly supports**: Phantom Courier, Iron Nomad, Ghost of the Undergrid.

2. **Glitching Pre-War Military Exosuit Prototype (Aries restricted ruins)**  
   Interactive wreckage that rewards high Might (to pry open), Agility (to navigate unstable structure), and Computation (to bypass locks and optimize remaining systems) into a personal mobility/augmentation kit.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Unbreakable Ronin, Eternal Seeker.

3. **Encrypted Overland Route Data Cache (Sagittarius frostline marker)**  
   Weather-beaten terminal. High Computation cracks layered encryption; Might and Agility required to physically reach and defend the site during a storm. Contains viable solo migration vectors.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Phantom Courier.

4. **Collapsed but Hackable Aries Micro-Reactor (hidden backup node)**  
   Environmental puzzle: physically stabilize failing core (Might + Agility) while rewriting safety protocols and optimization code (Computation) to create a portable personal power source.  
   **Strongly supports**: Architect of Collapse, Eternal Seeker, Iron Nomad.

5. **Ancient Mobility Rig Testing Ground (outer Capricorn scrap fields)**  
   Ruined proving grounds filled with obstacles. Completing agility courses, strength challenges, and computational optimization puzzles unlocks blueprints for custom high-mobility frames.  
   **Strongly supports**: Unbreakable Ronin, Phantom Courier.

6. **Hidden Server Vault with Predictive Survival Algorithms**  
   Located in a sealed Gemini ruin. Player must agilely bypass traps, use Might to force entry, and Computation to extract and adapt ancient "lone survivor" decision-tree AIs.  
   **Strongly supports**: Eternal Seeker, Architect of Collapse, Ghost of the Undergrid.

7. **Derelict Armored Nomad Convoy Wreck (Frostlands edge)**  
   Multi-stage environmental site. Requires Might to clear wreckage, Agility to reach inner compartments, and Computation to salvage and integrate onboard systems into a personal vehicle/base.  
   **Strongly supports**: Iron Nomad, Phantom Courier.

## Design Notes
- All triggers heavily favor the Might + Agility + Computation triad — physical feats, graceful movement, and intellectual/system mastery are equally required.
- Many triggers can chain: e.g., World Trigger 4 (personal reactor) → NPC 4 (Aries schematics) → NPC 6 (mobile command vehicle).
- Reputation impact: These paths tank district relations fast but grant massive personal power, hidden perks, and unique mobility upgrades.
- Tone: Every trigger reinforces the "Not My Fucking Problem" mindset — you solve problems with superior personal capability, then leave everyone else behind.
