# Minmax Build 01: Might + Agility + Computation - Special Interactions and Questlines

**Document Status**: Draft  
**Build Overview**: This build combines raw physical power (**Might**), graceful speed and precision movement (**Agility**), and advanced optimization, hacking, deduction, and systems mastery (**Computation**). The player excels at physically dominating obstacles, moving like a ghost through complex environments, and hacking/optimizing systems on the fly. They can bypass or resolve challenges in ways other builds cannot, often turning district problems into personal advantages or hidden paths toward "Not My Fucking Problem" independence.

**Design Philosophy**: Interactions reward creative combinations of brute force, stealthy movement, and smart problem-solving. Many open unique questlines, hidden rewards, or direct paths to minmax endings. Gates often involve high stats in the relevant areas or specific perks/skills.

---

## District-Specific Special/Unique Interactions (Minimum 5 per District)

- Physically carry multiple heavy patients or refugees through crowded, multi-level domes while using Agility to navigate soft terrain and water features without disturbing healing environments.

## Design Notes
- These interactions emphasize synergy between the three stats for creative, build-specific solutions (e.g., hack + physically support + move stealthily).
- Many can chain or combine with district conflicts (e.g., energy crises in Aries, experimental risks in Aquarius).
- Questlines often reward unique perks, custom tech, reputation shifts (positive with outcasts, negative with authorities), or direct progress toward minmax endings.
- Balance recommendation: High gates on Computation for hacking/optimization depth; Might + Agility for physical execution and mobility challenges.
