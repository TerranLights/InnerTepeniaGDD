The short version is: it's a post-war Earth, and then later a country established in Antarctica, populated by robots and the humans who love them.

There was a global nuclear war on Sept. 26th, 2083 that permanently changed the world. Countries collapsed, lines got redrawn, humanity and technology continued. At around the same time in 2083, the first fully sentient, fully conscious robot was created at the Underwater Research Union: Kappa (U.R.U.K.), off the coast of (now-sovereign/neutral) Hawaii. Robots became a part of society quickly. Over the next five centuries, human-robot relations flourished at first, and then soured. In the early-to-mid-2500s, there came another global war, this time between humans and robots. This culminated in the signing of the Falkland Treaty on June 21st, 2564 AD, which established a robot nation in (and around the outer coastal ring of) Antarctica, which came to be known as the Federation of Tepenia, where all robots on earth were to be exiled. Robots and the humans who loved and supported them made their lives there and began colonizing space, first in low-earth orbit, and then on Mars. A century later, there was another war, this time pitting the human-occupied lands of earth against the robot/human-occupied Tepenia. Although some Tepenians chose to remain on Earth, most chose to flee to outer space — both groups making desperate choices under conditions of war. The off-world population continued building their civilization there.
